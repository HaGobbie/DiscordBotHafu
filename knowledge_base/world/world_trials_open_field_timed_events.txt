# World Trials — Open Field Timed Events

== Overview ==
World Trials are optional large-scale Interrupting Events that are active for an extended time. Unlike normal Trials, they will be active in multiple sectors, and are scheduled events that are announced ahead of time.
W Markers (World Trial Markers) will be displayed whenever a World Trial is occurring.

=== Stellar Grace Recovery Operation ===
Stellar Graces are released into the world as a result of numerous luminous phenomenon appearing in the sky. The objective of the Stellar Grace Recovery Operation is to gather all the pyramidal containers known as Stellar Graces hidden in each exploration sector.
Mag Sonars can be used to discover the pyramidal containers. When you discover a Stellar Grace, you will receive a Personal Stella Boost, along with any rewards that are found in the container. There are three colored pyramidal container variants: Normal, Silver, and Golden, each with their own unique rewards. The phases will progress in accordance with the number of Stellar Graces discovered within the entire ship.
When the World Trial is completed, every user within the ship will receive a Social Stellar Boost in accordance with the phase progression. Personal Stellar Boosts and Social Stellar Boosts provide a boost to Rare Drop Rate across the entire ship.
There can be up to 8 Stellar Graces in a given region, with 1 Gold Stellar Grace and 2 Silver Stellar Graces within the region. During certain events, the Stellar Graces will be of the same tier
