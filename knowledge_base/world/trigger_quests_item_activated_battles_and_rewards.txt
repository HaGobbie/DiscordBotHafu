== Overview ==
Trigger Quests are unique quests that can be accessed through the Quest Counter, located in each of the main cities. Similar to Battledia Quests, Trigger Quests require the presence and use of certain triggers, namely Quest Triggers (abbreviated as Q-Triggers) in order for the quest to be accepted.
Currently, Quest Triggers can only be found from certain shops, specifically the Alliance Badge Exchange Shop . Quest Triggers are only consumed from the trigger host once the quest has successfully been completed, and are not consumed upon failing the quest as a result of fail conditions.
