== Overview ==
Standing Quests are instanced quests that the player can undertake in Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis. In Standing Quests, the player must fulfill all the conditions in the requested Standing Quest in order to complete the quest. Standing Quests take place in instanced areas that are randomized for each instance.
