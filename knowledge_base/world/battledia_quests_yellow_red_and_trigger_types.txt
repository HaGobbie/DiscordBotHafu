== Overview ==
Battledia Quests are unique quests that can be accessed through a Trigger Portal ( ). Five Battle Triggers, which can be obtained from enemy drops and tasks, are required to access the Trigger Portal Quests. Battle Triggers can be sold on the Personal Shop if players are not interested in Battledia Quests.
There are currently two types of Battledia Quests: Battledia: Yellow, and Battledia: Purple. Respectively, these quests require 5 B-Trigger/Common Yellow to access Battledia: Yellow quests or 5 B-Trigger/Common Purple to access Battledia: Purple quests. Each region has their own specific Trigger Portals.
The Battledia: Yellow quest provides a significant amount of EXP and Materials, and is highly recommended for leveling and gathering enhancement materials. High quality enhancing materials, such as the Gold Primm Sword +20 , Gold Primm Armor +20 , Silver Primm Sword +20 , and the Silver Primm Armor +20 can be obtained upon completion.
The Battledia: Purple quest is a high-difficulty quest which spawns continuous Gigantix enemies, but in exchange for its difficulty, provides the chance of high rarity equipment and items upon completion.

== Blue Portals ==
Blue Portals are a special kind of trigger portal that are only available during certain periods. The battle triggers and quests available vary over time. Similar to Battledia Quests, some of these portals may require the use of a special Battle Trigger to access the quests within.
There are currently three types of quests offered in Blue Portal: Valua. The Drill: Flurry of Flowers Clean Up Op quest requires the use of 5 B-Trigger/Spring '22 to access during the Spring '22 Event, while Drill: Dazzling Chaos Suppression Op and Drill: A Trap in the Forest do not require the use of any Battle Triggers and can be attempted an unlimited number of times during the event period.
