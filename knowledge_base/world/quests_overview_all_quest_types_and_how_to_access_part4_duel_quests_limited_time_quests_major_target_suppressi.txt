Sections in this file: Duel Quests, Limited-Time Quests, Major Target Suppression Missions

== Duel Quests ==
Duel Quests are high-difficulty, solo quests where you have to defeat boss enemies that have been greatly strengthened, and are recommended for players who are confident in their combat capabilities.

=== Standard Duels ===
Standard Duels are Duel Quests facing off against various Boss Enemies that have been made stronger than normal. To help with these quests, Equipment affixed with the Duel Quest Augment series Defi , such as "Defi Eriss P.01", can be used to increase your potency and damage resistance in these quests. In order to fully receive the effects from the "Defi" Augment series, you must be in a Duel Quest, and you must be using a weapon equippable by your Main Class.

=== Extra Duels ===
Extra Duels are high-difficulty Duel Quests facing off against very strong Boss Enemies. Unlike Standard Duel, Defi-type Augments have no effect in these quests and should NOT be used for them.
Fictitious Beam is a high-difficulty solo quest in which your goal is to defeat Ruine Masquerader . Starting from Depth 1, every time Ruine Masquerader is defeated, a new Rank within the Depth is unlocked. Each Rank adds buffs to the enemy, while adding debuffs to the player. Buffs and debuffs are only applied within the Depths they are obtained.

Planetfall Strike is a series of high-difficulty solo quest in which your goal is to defeat the boss enemy presented in each Stage. Stages 1 through 10 are unlocked by default. When you clear a Rank within a Stage, the next Rank is unlocked to a max of 5 Ranks per Stage. Each Rank adds buffs to the enemy, while adding debuffs to the player. Buffs and debuffs are only applied within the Depths they are obtained.
Once all 5 Ranks have been cleared in all 10 initial Stages, Stage 11 is unlocked, featuring as the final challenge of this series of quests.

== Limited-Time Quests ==
Limited-Time Quests are unique quests that become available during a specific period of time, primarily during Seasonal Events or Campaign Periods. They can be accessed through the Quest Counter located within each city, although other Limited-Time Quests may be accessed through the Map Menu.
Limited-Time Quests are not necessarily considered a standalone quest type, but are rather quests identical to that of Trigger Quests, Urgent Quests, or Standing Quests, with the only difference being that these quests are available for a limited period of time, where after the quest availability period ends, are no longer accessible.

== Major Target Suppression Missions ==
Major Target Suppression Missions are instanced high-difficulty quests where players face off against Dark Falz-class Boss Enemies that have been enhanced with new attacks and strategies.
These quests feature a 15-minute Time Limit and a shared Incapacitation Limit of 5 times. The quests fail if the timer runs out or if the Incapacitation Limit is reached.
