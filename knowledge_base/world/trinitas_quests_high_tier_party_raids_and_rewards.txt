== Overview ==
Trinitas Quests are challenging quests that can be accessed through the Trinitas pyramid ( ) located in Central Retem. The entrance to the pyramid is located underground, which can be accessed through a tunnel on the Central Retem coast.
In the Geometric Labyrinth, there are a total of six floors, which must all be completed in order to complete the quest. The rank completion of this quest is determined through the overall score the party has achieved throughout the quest. The score can be increased by completing Side Missions in the Geometric Labyrinth, which add a set amount of score, or through increasing the difficulty of the quest. Side Missions are assigned randomly on each floor, unless customized.
The level of the enemies encountered in the Geometric Labyrinth will scale to be the same as the player with the highest level in the party.
After completing the Geometric Labyrinth, players unlock the ability to customize the difficulty of the quest through various difficulty modifiers, such as enemy stat modifiers and customizable Side Missions, which increase the overall score multipliers in the quest. A total of 10 challenges can be applied during the quest. Additional challenges and upgraded versions of existing challenges can be unlocked through subsequent clears of the quest.
Certain items, such as weapons from the Koukloziat Series grant certain bonuses when used in these quests.
Title rewards exclusive to the Trinitas Quests include Rank 1's Mag Form/Coccyx Machina and Rank 2's Mag Form/Blanc Cheval .
