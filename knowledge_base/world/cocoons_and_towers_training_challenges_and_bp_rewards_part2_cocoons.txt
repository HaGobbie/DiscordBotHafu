=== First Step ===
Location | Party Limit | Recommended Power | Clear Condition | Accept Condition | Fail Condition
Central Aelio | Solo | 800 | Complete the Main Mission | None | Time exceeds 30:00
Detail | Entry
Main Mission | Reach the finish point.
Side Missions | Clear before 5:00 Clear before 10:00 Obtain 10 Point Cubes Complete all Side Missions and clear
Rewards | +1 Skill Point 0 EXP 0 Meseta
Description | This is Trainia, where ARKS defenders can test their abilities. Follow the instructions to reach the goal while learning the new action.
This cocoon tasks you with completing a training course. Using movement techniques and your weapon, you must destroy targets, collect Point Cubes and traverse the terrain with your Photon Dash and Photon Glide. At the end of the course, you use your Photon Blast to destroy a target. After doing this, the Finish Line is unlocked.
