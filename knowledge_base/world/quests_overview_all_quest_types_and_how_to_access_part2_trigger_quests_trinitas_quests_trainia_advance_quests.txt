== Trigger Quests ==
Trigger Quests are quests that can be accessed through the Quest Counter. Quest Triggers are required to start these and can be obtained from the Alliance Badge Exchange Shop or Personal Shop. Quest Triggers are only consumed upon successfully clearing a quest and only a Party Leader needs to have triggers available to enter.

== Trinitas Quests ==
Trinitas Quests are challenging quests that can be accessed through the Trinitas pyramid ( ) located in Central Retem. The entrance to the pyramid is located underground, which can be accessed through a tunnel on the Central Retem coast.
Enemy Levels scale to the level of the highest leveled player in a party and upon clearing the quest, challenge modifiers are unlocked, offering further challenge should you seek it. Using these modifiers grants higher score multipliers, and a total of 10 challenges can be applied during the quest. Additional challenges and upgraded versions of existing challenges can be unlocked through subsequent clears of the quest.

== Trainia Advance Quests ==
Trainia Advance Quests are challenging quests that can be accessed through Trainia Bunkers ( ).
Bunker Type 1 consists of Cannonball Rumble & Cannonball Strike and are located in Central Kvaris and South Kvaris respectively. The objective of the Cannonball Rumble and Cannonball Strike quests is to throw "cannonballs" at the target and destroy a specified number of targets within a time limit.
Bunker Type 2 consists of the Ordinal Tower and is located in South Aelio. In the Ordinal Tower, the goal is to defeat the enemies present in each rank of quest. Completing a rank unlocks the next, going all the way up to Rank 8.
