--- [Section: 概要] ---
One of the weapons that Techter can equip. It is sometimes written as "short cane". For basic behavior, photon blast, PA, etc., see Wand/Action/PA. In addition to long-range attacks using techniques, powerful blow attacks are possible at close range. With the wand in NGS, all attacks, including normal attacks, PA, legal explosions, and techniques, are treated as legal weapon attacks. To increase its power, add special abilities that increase the power of legal weapons (such as Technique I). It is not an increase in the power of striking weapons (such as Power I). ▲ ▼
