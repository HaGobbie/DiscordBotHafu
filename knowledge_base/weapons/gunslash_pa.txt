--- [Basic action] ---
Implemented on April 5, 2023 Weapon category that can be equipped by Slayer. It has the characteristic that attack performance changes depending on charge and direction input, so there are many types of actions, and it is possible to use them depending on the situation. There are four types of Photon Arts in Gunslash, and the feature is that the action changes depending on the presence or absence of movement input *1. In reference to the precedent of the similar job "Raster" in "PSO2", in this Wiki, when there is no movement input, it is sometimes called "Stay Arts", and when there is movement input, it is called "Move Arts". *2 Although there are some exceptions, "Stay Arts" attack on the spot, and "Move Arts" attack while moving. In conversation, the abbreviations are the initials of ``Stay'' and ``Move,'' and ``S'' is often added in front of the Photon Arts name for Stay Arts, and ``M'' for Move Arts. --- [Normal attack] ---
A normal attack for “bayonet (gun slash)”. Fires photon bullets and attacks up to 3 times. By holding down the first stage, you can charge up to two stages, each of which changes the attack behavior. The first stage of charging is a 5-shot attack, and the second stage is a powerful photon bullet. If multiple targets can be involved, PP recovery performance is high, but the charging time is long and it is difficult to adjust the position because you cannot move while charging. If you want to recover PP from a single opponent, we recommend 1 stage charge + charge slash. While charging, you can move slowly, but once the first stage of charging is completed, you will no longer be able to move. The turning speed is no different from that while walking. It can be used when aiming a two-stage charge toward the ground from an over-the-shoulder perspective and involving multiple opponents.
