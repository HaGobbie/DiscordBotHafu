# Wand PA — 基本アクション

## Basic action
Techter's basic weapon. It is a legal attack weapon that supports the power of techniques, but its concept is close combat. Therefore, unlike the rod, even though it is a legal attack weapon, it is easier to earn damage with normal attacks and PA in close combat. Depending on the skill, you can add any attribute to the Law Attack Explosion by using techniques. As a result, when a normal attack or photon arts hits, a legal attack explosion of the attached attribute is generated, and it can be used in the same way as a weapon with attached attributes. Also, when increasing the power of special abilities, the power of legal attack weapons is applied, and the power of striking weapons has no effect. Regarding Attribute Weapons Currently in NGS, unlike the old PSO2, weapons that can deal attribute damage are very limited, and among these, the ability to attack with any attribute by using Wand Element Change is a modest but unique advantage of Wands. The attribute can be added or changed by Wand Element Change, but the damage caused by the wand itself remains non-elemental if it is originally a non-elemental weapon. Similar characteristics also exist in boots, but there are significant differences in the power of follow-up attacks and the amount of down value accumulated depending on the attribute. In the case of attribute weapons, a small amount of attribute down value is given to the weapon attack part other than the legal attack explosion, so it is possible to use it properly, such as making the weapon attribute and legal attack explosion different attributes and distributing two types of attribute down values, or unifying them and accumulating one type of attribute down value intensively. If I were to describe a wand in one word, it would be a melee weapon with a technique firing function. In most situations, the main focus is on close-quarters combat, and the term "hogeki weapon" is a type of weapon that can only be used as a hand-to-hand weapon. Although it is a relatively small weapon, its attack motion is slow. Therefore, the power of each shot is high, and thanks to the simultaneous explosion of legal attacks, it is possible to accumulate both physical and attribute down at the same time. The reach of its normal attacks is short compared to other melee weapons, but since it attacks deeply on both ground and air (especially on the ground), its perceived reach and attack range is longer and wider than it appears. In addition, the mobility and enemy contact performance due to the Wondo Technique Skip Beat are at a very high level, and the performance of normal attacks increases greatly while the time-limited reinforcement skill Wondo Rubber's Reinforce is activated, and the addition of the behavior of rushing towards the target before attacking is added, so there are almost no situations in actual battle where the short reach becomes a problem. As a legal attack weapon, compared to Rod Tallis, there is no way to collect PP at medium to long distances, so long-range combat based mainly on techniques is impossible, and it is difficult to take advantage of the range, so its performance as a launching pad for techniques is extremely low. You cannot move at the moment of firing, and the timing at which you can guard cancel is the same as or slightly earlier than that of the rod, so there is a big gap compared to each of the main melee attacks. To begin with, Techter's techniques cannot be compared to PA in terms of power, and among Wand's actions, it is the weakest option overall, ``being used as a trigger to activate a unique function'', and there are many situations in which the size of the gap becomes a problem. However, it can be used as an option to fill in situations where melee attacks are impossible, so I would like to make effective use of it as one of its functions. Due to the adjustments made in October 2024, performance has been improved across the board, and at the same time, the difficulty of use has been significantly eased. In particular, the range of increase in firepower is considerable, and due to the upward revision of power such as the power up of Photon Arts, Won Lovers and Wondo Element Pursuit, and new support abilities including self-buffs with Tector Extra Field, the firepower output on the field due to the presence of Tector as well as Won itself has increased significantly compared to before. The environment surrounding Tector has been greatly improved, with certain measures being taken to prevent ``Tector overlap,'' which is a concern in multiplayer environments. In order to maximize Tector's support ability, the key was how to continue activating Wand Element Pursuit and the Photon Blast stored with it, and Tector effectively transformed into a wand-dependent class. Although the freedom of attacks that used to be possible, where subclass weapons could easily be used as main weapons, has been lost, Due to the adjustments made on the same day, the wand no longer lags behind subclass weapon types, even when looking at simple firepower performance. If it's huge, a small character can fit between the hand and the wand. As you can see, the attack detection is slightly wider and longer. During the long motion period until the attack is completed, guard points will be generated in all directions for the first step and only in front for the second step, and the guard points will last until the wand swing is completed. Guard points continue to occur for a long time, well over 1 second, until the second attack ends, so once you activate it, you can achieve both firepower and safety at a very high level. However, once the wand swings down, you will lose the guard point, and the gap after that cannot be canceled, so be careful not to get stiffed, even if it is only for a very short time. If you deflect an attack with this long guard point, you will naturally trigger a legal attack explosion due to Wand Guard Reflection, so it is extremely compatible. By making full use of combo route adjustments and delay inputs, and aligning these long guard points with enemy attacks, you can further increase sticking performance, firepower, and safety. Wand Guard Reflection can be activated as many times as you like depending on the number of hits of the enemy's attack, so if you successfully match the fourth hit against a continuous attack like Kerkund's continuous kicks, the firepower will increase explosively. Since it is a two-stage attack, the motion is so long that it takes twice as long as the third attack, but the legal explosion is activated twice. Not only is it highly powerful and has an excellent PP recovery amount, but the legal attack explosion that occurs when the second stage descends is large, so the amount of Pursuit Gauge that can be accumulated is also extremely large. There is a drawback that you are vulnerable during the technique motion, but once you reach the S beat, it is very safe.
