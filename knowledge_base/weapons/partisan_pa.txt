--- [Basic action] ---
A "long spear" that can be equipped by hunters. Among striking weapons, it has a wide basic attack range and range, has excellent instantaneous movement, and is also good at aerial combat. However, rather than being good at range attacks, I'm actually not good at them. Overall, the controls have a very unique feel, and the main focus is on ``heavy'' attacks that are powerful but have limited cancel timing, so you will not only attack aggressively, but also grasp the opponent's attack pattern and choose an action that will allow you to respond in time. Another major feature is that it does not have the Weapon Action → Parry Counter that exists in general melee weapons, and its unique counter attack, Partisan Parry Another Assault, is a stock-type attack enhancement, so Hunter Arts Avenge-related skills are important in order to attack while dealing with enemy attacks. The charged version of Assault Buster with Avenge and Another on it has one of the most explosive powers in the game, more than three times as powerful as normal, and the key is how to use this to hit weak points while fighting. Once I get used to handling it, I want to actively use it. It is good at taking on medium-sized enemies and regular boss enemies, which are enemies that can easily see off attacks and take advantage of Avenge's firepower. On the other hand, small enemies can be defeated without countering, and Partisan PA does not have easy-to-use ranged attacks in the first place, so as mentioned above, it is not good at fighting against large numbers. Additionally, opponents such as Giganticus, whose damage from parrying mistakes cannot be underestimated, will have a higher difficulty level to unleash their full firepower. ▲ ▼

--- [stub javelin] ---
Photon Arts for "Long Spear (Partisan)". After a thrust attack, throw the weapon at the enemy. By holding down the PA button, you can perform a thrust attack until PP runs out, and then perform a throw. Image Lv. 1 Power 830 PP consumption 25 While holding down the PA button, it performs a continuous thrust attack in five fan-shaped directions, and when the button is released, it throws partisans to attack from a long distance. The thrust attack is performed from left to right, but the hit detection is always the same regardless of appearance. The first thrust to the left can also attack the enemy on the right. On the other hand, the way the judgment occurs is different for each thrust attack, and if the PC is in a position where it is stuck in multiple parts, it will be sucked into other parts, but this is a relatively rare case. The reach of the thrust part is a maximum of 7.5m and has a common pattern. The throwing part has strong lock-on correction, and can be thrown almost directly above or below. Similar to a dive attack, it has the effect of knocking the enemy it hits and causing them to fall. The bullet speed is slow, so it takes time to throw it, and the hit detection is not as big as it looks, so it is easy to miss against enemies that move a lot. The firing range is approximately 32m (there is an error of about 1m due to the large projectile size), and the width is 2m in radius (=4m in diameter). PA consumes 25 PP when activated, and if sustained, 1 PP is consumed every 0.2 seconds from the 6th thrust attack. For the first 5 thrust attacks, an attack occurs approximately every 16F, and after that, an attack occurs approximately once every 12F. The PP consumption reduction effect is only effective for the first 25 PP, and is not valid for the continuous portion. Maintains altitude during thrust attack. A short press of the PA button will perform a thrust attack at least once. In terms of simple numbers, there are many aspects where it is inferior to other PAs. The advantages are that ``there is less loss in operation because only one input is required'' and ``You can continue attacking with Hu Arts Avenge on once the enemy attacks.'' If it lasts for a long time, it will become a DPS that other PAs will have to keep up with. Excellent compatibility with actions such as Valas' iron fist tornado and Kerkund's descent attack, where the attack judgment only occurs once and the enemy will not move from the spot for a while. In these cases, even if other PAs are perfect, it will make a difference. Although it is not significantly inferior to other PAs in terms of DPP, its main strength is when it lasts for a long time, so the consumption itself tends to increase. Be careful not to run out of PP after using it and be unable to do anything other than normal attacks. Also, when pressed for a short time, the movement is short and the DPS is not bad, so it is useful as a means of immediate activation or as a parrying method to match the enemy's detailed attacks. Although the throwing part alone lacks some power, the implementation of Hu Arts Paring Advance has made it easier to use as a counterattack against enemies at a distance. --- [Fatal Tornado] ---
Photon Arts for "Long Spear (Partisan)". Rotate your weapon to attack surrounding enemies. Charging changes the attack behavior. Image Lv. 1 Power 1330 PP consumption 20 Charge 0.7 seconds Common properties Continuously attack while stepping. Although it can attack in all directions, its range is quite narrow and it is not suitable for attacking multiple enemies at once. Stepping will not move forward if the target is engaged. When locked on, elements can be added to that side by lateral input. If you input to the right, you will be stepping diagonally forward to the right. It is not possible to change the stepping distance by forward/backward input. In addition to not moving forward when engaging an enemy, you can move around in a circle while locked on. If you're close enough, you can go around 90 degrees with the charge version, and over 180 degrees if you combine it with the assault charge. Cancel actions are only possible in steps between each attack. Turning is only possible at the beginning of each attack, and cannot change direction while attacking. Non-charged version: Rotates the spear and slashes. Step forward and do a two-step attack, the first time on the right hand side, and the second time on the left hand side after taking another step. Charged version A continuous attack of spinning attack > slam > mowing down. He steps in with a spinning attack and a slam, but he uses a mowing down on the spot. Always in a robust state during operation. Coupled with its high power, it is extremely effective against medium-sized enemies that have annoying tumble attacks like Brunkle. Although the slam only attacks the front and does not cover the sides or back, it moves forward a little longer than other attacks. Also, you can move in any direction you like from the time you jump up until you land. ``Both DPS and DPP are excellent,'' ``The charged version is always robust and can be adjusted to a certain degree,'' and ``The non-charged version has the same level of performance as a regular PA and can stay in the air.'' PP consumption is low, and if you use Same Arts Skip Attack Hu or Hunter Arts Avenge PP Gain, it is easy to conserve PP. Since the motion is very long and the cancel timing is limited, it has weaknesses such as being prone to hitting waste and missing counter opportunities. Although it is limited to the main Hu, Hunter Arts Paring Advance can make you invincible during all motions, so I want to aim for it as much as possible.
