# Twin Daggers PA — 基本アクション

## Basic action
A weapon specialized in attack speed and mobility that can be equipped by fighters. Its biggest feature is that it is outstandingly fast among all weapons, and there are very few gaps. By acquiring skills, you can also acquire high pursuit and tracking abilities regardless of open ground. It is possible to perform omnidirectional guard with high cancellation performance by weapon action, and combined with the lack of openings, it is possible to continue attacking any opponent. By using the Twin Dagger Quick Attack, it achieves a high level of firepower worthy of the name of a fighter weapon. Also, it has excellent PP recovery power among fighter weapons, and the fact that it can fire PA repeatedly without having to worry about PP management as much as the Double Saber Knuckle is a big advantage. Another feature is that the PA performance can be arranged in a variety of ways through TechArts customization. Various benefits can be aimed at, such as greatly reinforcing the attack range and attack range, which are originally weak points, and improving the convenience of set attacks. The overall customization content is quite good compared to other weapons, so understanding the characteristics of each customization is especially important for this weapon. On the other hand, the speed of movement also makes it more difficult to operate, and the downside is that you tend to lose track of what you are doing until you get used to it. The fact that the weapon action's guard reception time is below the average among all weapons makes it difficult to use, and combined with the speed of movement mentioned above, mastering it requires not only instantaneous judgment and reflexes, but also familiarity and mastery of the operation. In addition, it is inferior to Double Saber in general-purpose firepower and inferior to Knuckle in counter ability, so it tends to be left behind for other weapons unless it can be operated to find the optimal solution according to the situation.

## weapon action
Weapon action for “twin daggers”. Holds twin small swords and deflects attacks from the surrounding area. If enemy attacks overlap while spinning, they will be nullified (if successful, the same sound effect as Just Guard or Parry will be heard). ## photon arts
A fighter's PA is divided into a first half and a second half, and when the first half ends, it can be canceled with another action. If you learn the class skill Fighter Skip Arts, you can use the latter half directly. Combos such as the first half of PA1 → the second half of PA2 are also possible by combining these. ## hells escort
Photon Arts for "Twin Daggers". After charging forward, it releases a shock wave. Image Lv. 1 Power 570 PP consumption 16 First half Rush attack on the target area. After attacking, it bounces slightly in the direction you are inputting. When locked on, the lunge distance becomes about 3 steps. The attack detection itself can reach up to a distance of 4 steps. Rushing is fixed horizontally and does not pursue vertically. Since it rushes through the enemy's body to reach the target area, it often passes through the target. Second half: Sends a shock wave forward while moving backwards. The accelerator drive generation timing is slower than other PAs. A guard point occurs when sending out a shock wave. If there is a difference in altitude from the target, fire at an angle. The shooting angle is not very wide, about 45 to 60 degrees. If it's angled, the automatic target will cause you to shoot diagonally up and down at nearby enemies, and you'll often be unable to target enemies further away. ## photon blast
After approaching the target at high speed, it performs a wild dance attack, and finally performs a high-speed rotational slash while ascending. By inputting a direction after starting an attack, you can make short, high-speed movements in any direction, and it can handle small movements. However, there is no guidance performance in the vertical direction other than the initial high-speed approach. The altitude increases slightly during the final stage, so it's best to use it from a slightly lower altitude than the area you want to hit. It is an extremely excellent PB that is easy to hit due to its short movements and enemy contact performance among PBs, as well as its total power and DPS of 1000, the highest of the three Fi weapons. After the PB ends, the activation timing for the fighter skill Twin Dagger Quick Attack will occur. This alone has high DPS, so don't forget to activate it. Can be activated by holding down the normal attack button. You can build up the fighter skill dagger attack impact gauge to the maximum. Using combos with normal attacks will lead to improved DPS. PB power distribution is 255% x 5 + 325% + 380% x 5 (11 HIT, total 3500) For considerations common to photon blasts, see Weapons/Armor. A common feature of Photon Blast is that it is invincible while in motion.
