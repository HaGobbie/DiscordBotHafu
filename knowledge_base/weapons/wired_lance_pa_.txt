# Wired Lance PA — 基本アクション

## Basic action
A weapon type that is sometimes called a ``Jizaiyari'' and is characterized by its long reach and wire action. Hunter Has the widest normal attack range of all weapons. It also has excellent PP recovery performance, including swing speed. Most of the PAs don't require charging, and by using PA again during PA, you can attack with an overwhelming number of moves. The only drawback is that PP consumption is heavy. Of course, PA's attack range is basically very good, and it shows outstanding performance especially in group battles. The range is long, and the tip of both normal attacks and PA can basically reach about 2 steps (10m for a small character). With this, it is possible to continue to unilaterally attack a fighting type enemy that does not have hate from outside the attack range. Due to the ease of canceling PA and the movement performance of weapon actions, it is a weapon type that can be used appropriately to respond to situations at will. Another feature is that, like shooting weapons, there is a concept of appropriate distance for normal attacks and PA. Avoid getting too close to the enemy and fight at a suitable distance to inflict the most damage. Therefore, in situations where movement is restricted due to terrain or gimmicks, damage may be kept low. Weapon action is a movement type that pierces the wire into the target and approaches it all at once. Furthermore, by learning the hunter class skill, you can change the action to something other than approaching, such as keeping a distance or mowing down the surrounding area. Unlike swords and partisans, it is impossible to guard or parry without skills. Until a certain class skill is acquired, step avoidance is almost the only way to outrun enemy attacks. It doesn't have its own counter attacks, and its step counters can't be used in aerial combat, which is its specialty, so Hunter Arts Avenge-related skills become important if you want to deal with enemy attacks while also providing firepower. Once I get used to handling it, I want to actively use it. Although it has high movement performance, most attacks can be fired repeatedly without moving. If you want to, you can also keep the hated enemies around you. It's not impossible with other weapons, but it's certainly the easiest weapon to use due to its wide attack range and hunter class skills. It is especially useful in gimmicky quests, and it helps stabilize the firepower of others other than yourself in situations where you are fighting against many tough medium-sized monsters or multiple bosses, so if you are proficient with it, you will be able to contribute to the smooth progress of the quest. Related class skills "Wired Avenge Range Collection" The distance correction of PA activated by Avenge is always maximized "Crossing Feather" Gauge-based high-power attack "Hunter Reflect Fort" If certain conditions are met when being attacked, a counterattack will occur and the damage taken will be reduced for 5 seconds.

## About appropriate distance
Appropriate Distance Assault rifles, twin machine guns, and wired lances have an ``appropriate distance'', and the damage will increase or decrease depending on the distance to the enemy. If the target marker is within the appropriate distance, the target marker will turn orange, but since the actual judgment is made at the hit position of the attack, it does not necessarily mean that the damage will be at the appropriate distance.About the discrepancy between the target marker and the hit positionAbout the discrepancy between the target marker and the hit positionThe discrepancy with the hit position is noticeable with large enemies. The marker is located at the center of the area, but the attack will hit the outer skin (for example, the legs of the Daidal type). The core part is set so that it doesn't shift too much, but it does shift when you lock the core on the surface of the body and attack from behind, or lock the head and attack from the feet. As a countermeasure, pay close attention to the effect differences and damage numbers below. Lower-class and large enemies that get caught in the damage cap are useful as practice partners.
