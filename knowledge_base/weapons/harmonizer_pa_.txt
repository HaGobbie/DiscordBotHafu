# Harmonizer PA — 基本アクション

## Basic action
A weapon that can be equipped by Waker. It belongs to legal attack weapons, and all actions taken refer to legal attack power, but unlike other legal weapons, it cannot use techniques. Through various actions, you can summon photon life forms called "Familia" to fight together. All of Takuto's Photon Arts have the concept of "short press" and "long press", and if you press and hold, 10 PP will be consumed and an additional attack will occur. Basic techniques such as normal attacks, PA, and the class skill ``Tact Parry Counter'' have modest firepower against single targets, but attacks that can be activated by consuming the three types of gear gauges and markers attached to enemies all have high firepower, and in boss battles and the like, the rotation speed of these gauge-consuming techniques is directly linked to long-term firepower. In addition, in terms of defense, instead of recasting, there is a class skill called ``Hot Marmelo Parry'' that allows you to deploy a long-term Just Guard judgment at any time, and in general, managing special gauges and recasting is an important weapon. As you can see, it has a bit of a quirk, but once you get used to it, it is a skillful weapon that can be easily used against single targets, against groups, close range, long range, anti-aircraft, and counterattacks. The class skills related to quince, which are responsible for defense and counterattack, are exclusive to the main class, so when used as a subclass weapon, it is somewhat weak in the counterattack aspect. Related class skills “Familiar Unison” High firepower attack that consumes gear gauge “Familiar Unison Quince Plus” Strengthens Unison by consuming additional quince gauge *Main class only

## Normal attack
A normal attack for “tact”. Swings a musical instrument, pops photons, and attacks with up to 3 steps. By holding down the normal attack button, the attack behavior changes. Image Number of stages 1 2 3 Long press Power 90 95 130 160 Recovery PP 7 7 10 8 All stages are coordinate attacks on the target while normal attacks. The range when locked is about 8 steps. Tact itself does not have an attack judgment, it occurs in the effect. Although it is narrow, it can hit multiple targets within the range. If you hold it down, it will generate an additional attack that is slightly more powerful. In addition to the normal 1-3 stages, it can be directly derived from step attack, step counter, tact parry counter, and linear drive. If your goal is to recover PP, it is more efficient to use the 1st to 3rd hits of normal attacks. The power per hour is slightly higher when combined with a long press, so it will be more effective if you use it appropriately. ## weapon action
Weapon action for “tact”. Summons Familia "Marmelo" and deflects attacks from the surrounding area. Image Action Parry Short press Long press Quince, which can be summoned with a weapon action, accumulates energy while blocking attacks. By holding down the weapon action, you can switch to Quince Strike, which deals large damage according to the accumulated energy. Can guard against attacks from all directions. As an action, it is classified as a parry action rather than a guard posture, and the duration of the guard judgment is about the same short time as the rod weapon action. If you whiff, you won't be able to hit repeatedly, so if you get the timing wrong, your opponent will hit you directly. Its use in canceling attacks is limited. Basically, you will rely on "Hot Quince Parry" to cover up gaps during PA, but even though it can be shortened with class skills, there is a recast, so you can't use it "as many times as you want." ## fredranbres
Photon Arts for “tact”. Summons the familiar "Fredoran" and fires a piercing breath forward. If you hold down the PA button, instead of consuming additional PP, you can release a breath attack on multiple enemies in sight. Image Lv. 1 Power 410 PP consumption 13 《Short press》 Releases a breath that spreads in 3 directions in front of you. The attack detection is larger than it appears, covering not only the space between the breaths, but also the space further outside the edges. The collision detection for terrain is larger than it appears, and the attack detection is even larger, but it does not seem to hit targets that are not in the player character's line of sight. Since the exit of the projectile is the mouth of the familiar, it is fired from a height above the character's head. It seems that the bullet's attack judgment is an eccentric circle type with the center shifted downward from the bullet, probably to ensure that it hits the ground even when fired from a high place. If you shoot over your shoulder so that the top of the bullet grazes the target, it won't hit, but if you shoot the target so that it passes through the bottom of the bullet, it will hit even if it is far away from the main body of the bullet (limited to the bottom, the detection is wider than a dash attack piercing bullet).As a result, even if the target is blocked by terrain during a normal attack or landing, there are situations where a breath can overcome the terrain and hit. It has a wide angle of elevation and depression, making bombing from high altitudes and collecting ore possible possible comfortably thanks to the size of the judgment. The characteristics, behavior, and performance of a wide range of forward attacks are similar to the first-stage deployment technique of Taris's PA "Spread Feather", making it reliable in group battles such as during PSE bursts. In comparison, the attack range is wide horizontally, the firing range is a little short, and the DPS is a little high. In that the class skill "Linear Drive" cannot change direction until just before the reception time occurs (= timing when rigidity can be canceled with the next action), its ability to scatter in many directions is inferior compared to Talis's PA and techniques. However, since the firing angle is originally wide, there is no need to worry unless you are at close range. The amount of markers accumulated for "Treble Cliff Marking" is very small. Power distribution is 50% of the listed power. 《Long press》 Locks multiple enemies in front and fires a tracking bullet to chase them all at once. It is not possible to continue the lock-on action, and it is not possible to change direction and attack enemies in all directions like the assault rifle's PA ``Homing Dart''. The tracking performance is not very strong, and the attack detection is smaller than that of a short press, so it may not hit a small target if it continues to move. Unlike the short press, there is a large amount of "Treble Cliff Marking" markers accumulated. If you are considering PP recovery while using it for hard enemies, you should consider using this. If the number of locks exceeds 2 against the same attack target, the DPS of Fredoran Riding's short press hits will start to exceed slightly, and the PP consumption will be severe, but it will be an effective attack method against some boss enemies that have more lock points. Especially against enemies that have multiple lock points, such as 4, in addition to high DPS, it can be used as an excellent means of attack due to its gear accumulation and marker accumulation. If you shoot from close range, you can concentrate on the same area. Boss enemies that have 4 or more parts at the start of the battle and can be locked at the same time include ``Bansar/Banshee type'', ``Ork type'', ``Daedal type'', ``Nex type'', ``Fortus type'', and ``Rezentos''. The number of parts often decreases due to destruction of parts, so use them appropriately. However, in most cases, the PP consumption is not commensurate with the increase in DPS in these two locations, and even if DPS exceeds Breath, considering the efficiency of gear, PP, etc., it is best to aim for 3 to 4 locks or more unless you have too much PP. Power distribution is 50% of the listed power. ## Wolfer Demolition

If you hold down the PA button, the attack changes to a chargeable attack instead of consuming additional PP. If not charged, it will generate a shock wave that will remain in place for a certain period of time. When charged, it releases a powerful area attack. ## photon blast
Activated by pressing "Photon Blast" on the palette when the Photon Blast Gauge is at maximum. This is a coordinated attack by the Familiars, with Wolfer and Fredoran simultaneously firing a thick beam with a certain attack range, and finally, Quince flies diagonally up and to the right in the air, running over the area in front of it and pursuing it. The attack range is only a wide straight line in front of you. The character cannot turn after activation, so you need to aim carefully. The width and depth of the first row are wide enough, but the height is not thick. If you shoot from a high altitude, such as from the top of a double jump, it will not hit near your feet. (Ordinal Tower Rank 7, PB was activated from the top of the step in front of the second training target for opening the Verdias route and did not hit) There is no judgment on the back. The beam fired by Fredoran and Wolfer in the first stage has a long range, but the second stage, in which Marmelo flies away in front, can only reach medium distances. Therefore, it is important to keep in mind that this is a PB that only functions in the forward direction and at medium ranges. Attacks occur quickly, and the time it takes to confirm damage (end of attack) is particularly short. It has a certain range and range, and has a high DPS, so it is a relatively high performance PB. The total time of the motion itself is 4 seconds (240f). Along with Katana and Thalys, it is one of the shortest PBs overall. Power distribution is 540 x 2 + 840 x 3 (total 5 hits, total power 3600) 70% of the total is concentrated in the second stage. For considerations common to photon blasts, see Weapons/Armor. A common feature of Photon Blast is that it is invincible while in motion. Adjustment/Modification History 2022/10/05 Update Power has been upwardly revised

## Familia
A "photon lifeform" that is summoned by Tact's various actions. There are three types, and the corresponding familiar differs depending on the action used.

## Fredoran
A pterosaur-shaped familiar with a distinctive blue-based coloring. Specializes in long-distance attacks using breath. Called by ``Fredoran Breath'' and ``Fredoran Riding''. The design homage seems to be the pet "Redoran" that appeared in the old "PSO2". In the concept art during the development stage, the tentative name was given as "Familiar ①_Long Range Attack". If you use and apply the motion change item "Mo "Standby: Fredoran"", you can see the player character floating and playing with Fredoran. Fredoran concept art *Click to enlarge

## Wolfer
A canine-like beast-human type familiar whose coloring is based on red. Specializes in close-range combat with heavy blows. Called by ``Wolfer Demolition'' and ``Wolfer Raid.'' The design homage seems to be the pet "Shinkurou" that appeared in the old "PSO2", but Wolfer has a larger body compared to Shinkurou and has little resemblance to the original. In the concept art during the development stage, the tentative name was given as ``Familia②_Close-range attack''. When you use and apply the motion change item "Mo "Standby: Wolfer", you can see the player character and Wolfer standing back to back in a stance. Wolfer concept art *Click to enlarge

## Quince
Familia has an adorable appearance and is characterized by its yellow-based coloring. It specializes in blocking enemy attacks, and can also perform powerful counterattacks. Called by "weapon action". The design is thought to be based on the pets "Maron" and "Melon" that appeared in the old PSO2. "Quince" is a real plant that is sometimes called "quince". The family name is Quince, which belongs to the Rosaceae family, and produces fruits that resemble pears and apples. Like the above-mentioned ``maron'' and ``melon,'' this familia probably follows naming conventions related to food. Among users, maron and melon are sometimes collectively referred to as "maromero," and this also shows the similarities. In the concept art during the development stage, the tentative name was given as "Familiar ③_Special Attack Main". Quince concept art *Click to enlarge
