--- [Basic action] ---
A melee weapon that can be equipped by Braver. Its biggest feature is that it has various photon arts that change its behavior when used continuously, and it has guard and counter actions just like the sword. The attack speed and tempo of both normal attacks and PA are quite quick, and the overall action speed is very fast. The reach of normal attacks is average, but there are PAs that can attack even from some distance and have high firepower, PAs with high range attack performance, rush PAs, PAs that consume less PP, and have excellent mobility, and PAs that are very excellent in attack. On the defensive side, it has an extremely excellent structure that combines defensive performance, firepower, and PP recovery while emphasizing continuous combat power, such as the high-performance weapon Action Guard that has a omnidirectional guard effect and can be activated instantly, and its unique action of countering immediately after an attack, which secures DPS and recovers a large amount of PP. Furthermore, by adding related skills such as Katana Just Guard PP Gain and Brave Spirit, it is possible to dramatically increase the power and PP recovery rate of counters. Overall, it is a weapon that shows its true value by making maximum use of its counters. On the other hand, due to the PA combo, PP consumption is poor (especially Fuyotenrai), so PP support when you can't aim for a counter is an issue. The same thing happens when the enemy stops attacking, so it's important to know how much you can cover with Brave Combat and subclass skills. Against boss enemies, the key is to collect PP with counters from Hayate Tsuwabuki, who has excellent cancel timing, and how much you can insert Fuyotenrai when you see an opening. Its strength lies in the success and establishment of a battle situation through just guarding and reading the enemy's next move, and it can be said that it is a weapon that requires player skill to pursue firepower. On the other hand, the grace period for counters by guard is relatively long, and in addition to guard counters, it has Fairless Attitude, which allows it to wait and see what happens after a very long counter reception time after a PA is activated.Coupled with the ease of canceling various PAs, it has the advantage of being a counter with high firepower, but easier to aim for counters than other melee classes. After the Braver adjustment carried out on April 2nd, the overall hitting points, including counter and PA firepower, etc., have further increased, and we have succeeded in completely overcoming the current situation where we could not help but feel a slight lack of power. If you can continue to grasp the enemy's movements to a certain extent, you can expect it to perform at a high standard, combining ease of use, stability, and high firepower than before. ▲ ▼

--- [Normal attack] ---
Image Number of stages 1 2 3 Power 103 106 110 Recovery PP 7 7 7 Iai continuous attack that sheathes the sword with each swing. 1 hit per stage. The sheathing motion of all stages can be canceled by guard, step, or jump. Although it's only for a short time, canceling doesn't work while it's being swung. Cancellations cannot be made via PA. All three stages have the same motion time. It is one of the fastest striking weapons (Twin Dagger and Knuckle's 1st to 3rd stages are about the same).Because there is no extra attack, the loss in damage and PP is relatively small no matter which stage you stop it. On the ground, step on each step. Ignores direction input and only moves forward. If it is not locked and there is no direction input, it will automatically turn to the target at the moment it is swung. In the air, it is possible to move horizontally in input directions other than backwards, albeit only slightly. The altitude decreases little by little with each stage, but it can remain in the air. There is currently no special action for long-pressing, so you can continue swinging at the fastest speed by holding down. Note that unless you hold down the button, you won't be able to attack at the fastest timing. By learning Fairless Attitude, guard points will be generated for each stage of sheathing. If you hold it down for a long time, it will move to the next stage before GP occurs. Depending on the situation, you need to decide whether to keep swinging or block with GP. By learning Brave Combat Close Range, you will be able to track enemies at a certain distance, including up and down, while Brave Combat is under effect. Combined with the fastest swing, it tracks at a speed comparable to Photon Dash. Brave Spirit Increases power and PP recovery amount when activated. The increase in PP recovery amount is minimal, but in terms of power (DPS), it is the highest among normal attacks for melee weapons. Now that it has been strengthened and adjusted, it now has firepower comparable to a normal Knuckle's 4th to 5th stage. --- [photon arts] ---
Like normal attacks, there are up to 3 stages, and when activated repeatedly, the behavior changes according to the number of stages. As an aside, Katana's PA names are based on the Japanese names of flowers and plants, just like the old PSO2. Related class skills Brave Combat Katana PA Combo Retention Katana PA Combo Finish ▲ ▼

Image Lv. 1 Power 265 PP consumption 12 After charging for a moment, attack by cutting through a certain distance at high speed. Has a robust effect on the entire motion. There are some enemies that are useful, such as the Gororon series and Metius, who can get behind the enemy in an instant, but there are many situations where it is a little mismatched as a katana with a powerful counter. Since damage is also caused to targets that pass by while moving between the activation point and the lock target, it is possible to run over and tangle in targets over short distances. Only the third step has a falling effect. In situations such as Geometric Labyrinth, where there are many scenes where a large number of enemies appear close together, this PA is extremely reliable, along with Ayamerepou's third stage, which has a wide range fall attack.
