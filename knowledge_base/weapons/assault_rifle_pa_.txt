# Assault Rifle PA — 基本アクション

## Basic action
A rapid-fire shooting weapon that can be equipped by the Ranger Gunner. There is a concept of appropriate distance, and it has the characteristic that the greatest damage can be dealt by fighting at medium distance. In PSO2, it was often required to fire from a stationary position, but in NGS, it is a weapon that fights while moving lightly and sliding with weapon action. After the upward revision, its evasion performance has become extremely excellent, and it also has a high level of safety. The power of both normal attacks and PA is quite modest overall, but it has several types of attack methods using high-power PA for when it is down. In addition, he also possesses high-power attack skills such as bomb throwing and setting traps that use a cooldown time and scatter attacks that use a gauge. Both have excellent instantaneous firepower and PP recovery, and if you master them, you can rely on them even in battles with large enemies. The Ranger's ability to attack a large number of swarming enemies can be said to be relatively high due to the Ranger's excellent class skills. However, before the implementation of Tech Arts customization, the only PA that had area attack was Homing Dart, which had a slow impact and slightly low power (DPS), making it incompatible with bursts or being surrounded by hard enemies. Range attack methods such as traps, wide-range weak bullets, and spread shots derived from the above-mentioned weapon actions also have a fairly limited number of bullets, and if you are unable to defeat the enemy group by the time you shoot them all, you will immediately fall into a disadvantageous position (this is especially noticeable when you are surrounded by tough Pedas-type enemies during defense, etc.). After the implementation of Tech Arts customization, two types of PA customization were implemented that greatly strengthened the range attack power of Raging Shot and Blaze Shot, so concerns about range attack performance were resolved. Furthermore, due to class skills, Rangers are able to fully utilize all functions. ## Normal attack
A normal attack for “long guns (assault rifles)”. Fires bullets and attacks. Hold down the normal attack button to continue firing bullets. Image Normal Impact Power 27 50 Recovery PP 2 3 There is no concept of continuous attacks; pressing the button once will fire 3 shots, and if you press and hold it, the shots will continue as long as you hold it down. Fires photon bullets. It is also possible to walk while firing continuously. Regardless of the number of bullets, it will perform a reload operation after firing, but there will be no operational delay due to the presentation alone. It is the least vulnerable of the assault rifle's attack methods. If Rifle Attack Impact is not activated, its power is quite low, so there is not much point in using the normal version. At the time of the service's launch, it was a relatively high-level compromise, but now it is difficult to evaluate it beyond ``the damage caused is not zero.'' I want to use PA properly. Rifle Attack Impact When activated, the power is greatly enhanced. Stock type with 60 shots. Depending on how long it continues to fire, it transforms into a powerful single-target attack that can match or exceed Revolt Aim in DPS. The special gauge returns to 0 after firing a normal attack even once, and can begin to accumulate again. Hitting it with a rifle's photon blast instantly fills the gauge. The flow of Attack Impact -> Photon Blast -> Attack Impact (with a spread shot or rifle grenade in between) is quite powerful. It's not as extreme as the launcher's Fear Eraser because there is a slight transition motion before it starts shooting, but the longer you shoot at once, the better your DPS will be. Ideally, you should shoot only 60 shots, but if you feel like your PP will run out between the time you finish shooting and the time the gauge fills up, you can save money by making adjustments such as firing 20 to 40 shots at a time, with a good balance between PA and PA. ## weapon action
Weapon action for “long guns (assault rifles)”. Fires bullets while moving quickly. Image Power 25×4 Recovery PP 2×4 Slide shot. Fires photon bullets while moving at high speed. Fires four consecutive shots while stepping in any direction, front, back, left, or right. PP recovery available. If no direction of movement is entered, move backwards. When used in the air, it moves horizontally while maintaining altitude. Can be used to evade attacks and adjust position while hovering. It is not suitable for long-distance movement in the air as it falls a little between reuses. In order to rise again, you will need a multi-weapon with other weapons. For the time being, the falling distance per horizontal distance is smaller than that of Glide. As it is, it has no defensive or evasive effect. In order to have evasion ability, it is necessary to learn Slide Shot Advance. With the update, it has become an extremely high-performance evasion ability that can be used actively. An invincibility period occurs when an action is activated, and if the evasion is successful, the invincibility is extended until the end of the action, so even if you attack with a sustained attack, you can continue to evade by using it continuously. Rifle Grenadier When learned, hold down the weapon action key to generate a high-power bomb or trap. Furthermore, when you learn Rifle Grenade Advance, you will become invincible when using the above bombs. Details are available at each link. If the evasion is not successful, the invincibility time will be interrupted at the end of the slide shot motion, resulting in a defenseless time between the end of the slide shot and the bomb firing or placement. If you are attacked at this timing, the grenade activation will be canceled. When using it in conjunction with enemy attacks, be careful not to use it too quickly. Rifle Grenadier Quick Reload When learned, reduces bomb cooldown each time you dodge with a weapon action. Since it can be reused immediately at maximum level, it can be treated as a pseudo counter action. If the attack intervals match, continuous evasion and blasting is possible. ## Generic action
Action Notes Dash Attack Power 250 Turn your body and sink down while firing a single piercing bullet in front of you. Recovery PP 18 Step Attack Power 135 Step forward and fire a single explosive bullet at the target. The attack range is very narrow, but it can involve surrounding enemies. Recovery PP 6 Step Counter Power 300 Rolls small and fires a large explosive bullet towards the target. (If input from the back, it shoots while sinking the body) The blast is a range attack and can engulf multiple targets. Recovery PP 8 Dive attack (altitude: low) Power 200 Kick towards the ground while falling. Recovery PP 0 A common feature of ranged weapons is that the dash attack judgment is thicker than it looks, and it will hit even if the bullet doesn't seem to be grazing. Because it penetrates and is powerful, it can wipe out small-sized enemies and low-rare ores with a single shot, depending on how you aim. For step attacks and step counters, you can change the direction of stepping using the direction keys. When a counter is established, not only the character's behavior but also the effect and range of the explosion change. Step attacks and counters can explode and engulf multiple people around the impact point, but the amount of PP recovered per hit is modest. If the attacks are dense enough to be involved in an explosion, a dash attack is generally superior in power, certainty, and amount of recovery. Step attacks are superior in accuracy, firing speed, and bullet speed, so use them accordingly. Due to the difference in the size of the bullets, step attacks may pass through in intricate terrain, but dash attacks may be blocked.

## homing dart
Photon Arts for “long guns (assault rifles)”. Fires a special bullet that tracks multiple enemies in sight. Image Lv. 1 Power 360 PP consumption 24 While holding the PA button, attach circular markers to targets within the target range, and when the button is released, guided bullets will be fired towards each marker. If you rotate your character (or look over your shoulder) without locking on, you can easily mark many targets. You can lock up to the maximum number even at the fastest activation without holding. Targeting range is 50 meters at 45 degrees left and right. (Only the front extends by about 3 meters) (*Values ​​before reinforcement on January 31, 2024) Although it is thinner than in the horizontal direction, the lock range is fairly thick in the vertical direction as well. If you are at an altitude like the top of a dash double jump, you can lock on small enemies from directly above. The maximum number of shots per PA is 20 shots. If you mark 21 or more, the oldest ones will disappear. Since the target is not an enemy but a part that can be locked on, multiple darts can be fired at one large enemy. However, please note that if you hit the same enemy multiple times, the power will decrease with each hit. If multiple shots all hit the same enemy, 5 hits would result in 140% total damage (504 in power notation), 10 hits would result in 150% total damage (540 in power notation), and the maximum of 20 hits would result in total damage of 170% (612 in power notation). Due to its ability to fire bullets at multiple targets within its range at once, it functions as an attack against many targets even if the enemies are dispersed. However, if there is another enemy on the trajectory to the target, the total damage may drop due to the aforementioned power attenuation, so it is more efficient to keep a certain distance or shoot from above. Since the power of a single shot is low, it is a poor means of attack against a single medium to large enemy with a single lock. Still, it can be used if multiple enemies appear at the same time, but in situations where there are a lot of players, such as during a PSE burst, there is a frequent pattern where the small enemies are already dead by the time they land, as shown below, so as of 2023, when various area attack PAs have been strengthened and newly implemented, the performance position is quite delicate among anti-multiple attack PAs in such situations. On the other hand, PA is extremely useful in exploration sections, some limited-time quests, and situations like Luciel Exploration, where enemies are scattered in small numbers over a wide area, or where each player has to hunt separately. It is unique in that it can attack targets in all directions while maintaining a line of sight to more enemies at an altitude comparable to the apex of a double-dash jump. It is better to use it with the understanding that it is a to-many rather than a range. Since it is now possible to lock up to 20 shots, there are fewer cases where most of the shots are absorbed by enemies with multiple parts. Also, in level zones where the enemies are hard, such as the recently implemented high-rank section, it is possible to lock on to medium-sized enemies all at once even during bursts. If you enter a movement input when firing, it will fire while stepping toward you. You can attack while evading, and even in situations where there are multiple enemies using shooting attacks, you can attack while minimizing damage. It has extremely high guidance performance that tracks the target itself rather than the enemy's position at the time of launch, so it will almost always hit enemies that have moved between launch and impact. If the target is destroyed before impact, the bullet will fly to the target's last location. Therefore, there is a high possibility that it will be a wasted bullet in situations where it is immediately annihilated from the beginning, such as during a PSE burst. ## blaze shot
Photon Arts for “long guns (assault rifles)”. Fires rapid fire at the target while avoiding enemy attacks. Long press to continue firing as long as PP lasts. Image Lv. 1 Power: 310 PP consumption: 20 A very easy-to-use PA that can move freely at medium speed while continuing to attack from a long distance. Consumes 20 PP when activated, and consumes 1 PP every 0.1 seconds during rapid fire. PP consumption reductions such as preset abilities and quick food only have an effect on PP20 at the time of activation, and the consumption during continuous firing does not change. This PA alone requires coordination with the surroundings as it attacks a single target, but when combined with PP recovery methods such as the Force Skill's Killing PP Gain and the Bouncer Skill's Defeat PP Gain, the continuous firing time can be significantly extended. If you incorporate it into a multi-weapon, you can also use the weapon actions of Launcher and Tallis. The movement speed is not as fast as Photon Dash, but it is faster than walking when sheathing the sword. It can be used to escape from enemy attacks and adjust distance while continuing to attack. Movement is only on the horizontal axis, jumping is not possible. If you don't look carefully at where you're going, you might be stopped by a small ledge. If activated in the air, the altitude will slowly fall at a descent speed similar to that of a charge landing. From an over-the-shoulder perspective, you can also perform FPS-like tricks such as ``approaching the target while shooting at it''. This is useful when there is a gimmick that you want to ignore the enemy group in front of you and quickly destroy it. The firing interval is shorter than normal attacks, and the total number of attacks per time is higher. Therefore, it can also be used to gain count when activating Weak Bullet Reinforce when there are few people on the same battlefield. Since the vertical firing angle is quite narrow, it is difficult to adjust the firing angle when aiming at enemies in the sky, let alone enemies on the ground. This is relatively fatal, and malfunctions are a common occurrence, especially during boss battles that move up and down. The finish can also be applied top and bottom. The shooting motion at the end of the PA takes a long time, but there is an invincibility period. In the past, the timing was so severe that it was practically non-existent, but with updates it has been extended, making it practical. ## revolt aim
Photon Arts for “long guns (assault rifles)”. Fires a powerful bomb against a certain location. While the button is held down, the impact position will be displayed, and when the PA button is released, the attack will be performed. Image Lv. 1 Power 700 PP consumption 20 Creates a continuous explosion that hits 5 times in front of you, slightly outside the line between close range and appropriate distance. While holding down the PA button, a marker will be displayed at the attack position. It is also possible to walk and jump. When the marker and the sight overlap, the color turns red. It's easy to get confused, but it's not a charge PA. If there is an obstacle between the player and the point of impact, the bullet will fail to fire. The attack range is very narrow (distance: 12-19m), and the attack position is fixed at a fixed distance and can only be slightly adjusted by the angle of depression. The attack range is as narrow as the charged version of Granz, and the remaining explosions may not hit when he is moved or even when he bends over. Due to the position and range of the attack, if it hits, the appropriate distance correction will be applied. (Even if you hit a very small target such as Rappy or various Tames so that it grazes the part closest to the attack detection, the appropriate distance correction will be applied, so even if it does not hit, it seems that there will be extremely limited situations.) The target position for the hit is immediately after falling from close range to the lock-on and reaching the appropriate distance. Due to its difficulty in hitting and long recovery time, it is basically an attack method to gain damage while the enemy is down, but its firepower and DPS are by far the most powerful among rifle PAs, and in situations where a full hit is expected, it can deliver outstanding damage comparable to Fallen Impact. If you know the boundary between close range and appropriate distance, you can stab the enemy while they are standing still for even a few seconds, but it is too addictive to use in normal times, so it is only for experts. If you fire without thinking, you won't be able to get a full hit, and on the contrary, you'll end up reducing your firepower. The narrow angle of elevation and depression makes it sensitive to height differences, and depending on the environment such as the keyboard, it may continue to move on its own while you press and hold it, or it may not be able to move at all, so it can be a real hindrance for people who are inexperienced in using it. Even when using it while down, it is necessary to quickly adjust the distance, so understanding and learning the appropriate distance is extremely important. Although the distance is fixed, this is the only coordinate attack for rifles. Even if the line of fire to the weak point is blocked by the enemy's body, it can be hit. In many cases, it is necessary to be in position to hit a weak bullet, but it is useful when a charged raging shot with a thick judgment does not hit well. On the other hand, the long operating time also has the advantage of low PP consumption per hour, and the fuel consumption per hour is quite good even when activated immediately. ## photon blast
Fires multiple lasers from the sky, and finally fires a single giant laser. A cylindrical area centered around the aiming position is irradiated. The diameter of the circle is quite wide, about 10 characters. Damage is determined from top to bottom, so if an enemy has a weak point near their feet or below, you can't hope to hit that weak point, making it difficult to deal damage. This is very clever, and there are many bosses whose weak points cannot be hit, making it a photon blast that has great compatibility with enemies. ## About appropriate distance
Appropriate Distance Assault rifles, twin machine guns, and wired lances have an ``appropriate distance'', and the damage will increase or decrease depending on the distance to the enemy. If the target marker is within the appropriate distance, the target marker will turn orange, but since the actual judgment is made at the hit position of the attack, it does not necessarily mean that the damage will be at the appropriate distance.About the discrepancy between the target marker and the hit positionAbout the discrepancy between the target marker and the hit positionThe discrepancy with the hit position is noticeable with large enemies. The marker is located at the center of the area, but the attack will hit the outer skin (for example, the legs of the Daidal type). The core part is set so that it doesn't shift too much, but it does shift when you lock the core on the surface of the body and attack from behind, or lock the head and attack from the feet. As a countermeasure, pay close attention to the effect differences and damage numbers below. Lower-class and large enemies that get caught in the damage cap are useful as practice partners.
