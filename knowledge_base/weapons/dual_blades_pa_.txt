# Dual Blades PA — 基本アクション

## Basic action
A melee weapon that can be equipped by Bouncer. A variety of attacks are possible with two swords and a photon blade. Another feature is that by using the same Photon Arts again during Photon Arts, you can shift to a derivative action and change the power and performance of the attack. Basic attack speed and reach are average. It has a well-balanced lineup of enemy contact PAs with vertical guidance, omnidirectional area attack PAs, and fixed point attack PAs, and its ability to respond to situations and maneuverability is not bad. While Fanatic Blade is active, the actual PA firepower increases greatly, and it exhibits top-class attack power mainly against single targets. If you master Blade Arts Parry, which will be described later, its firepower will further increase. Although it is a melee weapon, it is also attractive in that it can demonstrate a relatively high level of firepower in medium-range combat with some PA and skills. Various photon arts have a high degree of freedom in the timing of step avoidance, and with the exception of some derived PAs, steps can be canceled most of the time from the beginning to the end of the motion. On the other hand, the unique feature is that during Photon Arts, there are only a few times when you can cancel with actions other than steps (normal attacks, jumps, and other PAs). As an action to compensate for this, there is a skill called ``Blade Arts Parry (Counter Edge)'' that allows you to parry and counter at any time during Photon Arts. It has a very unique operating feel and very strong quirks, but if you master it, it can function as a very powerful counter action that combines offense and defense. The operational drawback is that the PP recovery method is quite unreliable for the PP consumed by the PA you own. In particular, the total power of the 3-stage normal attack is quite high, but the amount of PP recovered is better if you recover it with a dash attack. It is a weapon that can fight at all ranges, including far, close, and range, so if you want to make the most of the Dual Blade, we strongly recommend choosing a subclass such as Force or Gunner that aims to increase the amount of PP recovery. You can also cover it with multi-weapons. If you are going to a combat section where there are many opportunities for small fry battles, choose Sub Force. The sub gunner is a choice for boss battles and with an emphasis on attack. Since it is now possible to collect a large amount of PP with pinion blades, I would like to utilize that as well to somehow strengthen the weaknesses in PP. There is a trick (details) that activates Counter Edge and Step Counter at the same time, and it is powerful not only in terms of firepower but also in terms of recovering PP, but it is extremely difficult to activate, the attacks that can be activated are limited, and it is a special action for very advanced players that can be weakened if activated. Side note: Photon Blade is often abbreviated as "FB", but the English translation is p hoton b lade, so "PB" is the correct abbreviation. (It is similar to Photon Blast p hoton b last.) In this work, fanatic blade f anatic b lade has been added as a class skill, so you should be careful about abbreviations when discussing it. Related Class Skills Pinion Blade

## Normal attack
A normal attack for the “Flying Sword (Dual Blade)”. Swings a flying sword and performs up to 3 attacks. Image Number of stages 1 2 3 Power 65 100 60+240 (shock wave) Recovery PP 6 6 3+6 (shock wave) 3 consecutive attacks with dual blades. Attack while moving forward slightly on the ground or in the air. The 1st and 2nd steps are the orthodox Kesagiri → Gyakukesa. The third stage is an X slash while crossing the dual blades, which simultaneously generates a shock wave and has a flying distance of about 3 steps. This shock wave is judged separately from the sword attack, and it hits the same target twice with the sword and shock wave. The shock wave part is powerful. There is a guard point in front of you at the start of the motion. The overall behavior is quick and the transition to steps is easy. The amount of PP recovered is extremely small compared to the average consumption, making it unreliable as a means of recovering PP. The amount collected has increased somewhat with the update, and depending on the situation of Photon Blade PP Recovery, it can finally exceed the PP recovery efficiency of Dash Attack. On the other hand, it has relatively high power among normal attacks, and is comparable to a katana when Brave Spirit is activated. However, due to the update, the power of the first hit has been reduced and the power of the third hit has been increased, resulting in an extreme difference in firepower, so when swinging it, it is recommended to wait until the third hit can be used. Also note that unlike other striking weapons, there is no Same/Another Arts Skip Attack (class name), so the third hit cannot be used directly from the PA. For better or worse, it has a unique feeling of use. ## photon blast
Continuous attack by firing a large amount of photon blades at medium and medium ranges in front of you. Finally, it mowed down with a huge photon blade generated by combining the dual blades. The photon blade firing part in the first half has a certain range. However, the blade firing part only has a slight direction change after activation, and there is no vertical guidance or muzzle correction due to height differences, and the vertical range of judgment is not very wide, so it often misses unless the altitude and firing direction are properly adjusted. Be very careful when using it on targets that move vertically. Additionally, the final stage allows for a 360 degree change in direction. Moreover, due to the size of the photon blade firing part, it may be sucked into areas around the weak point, so be careful about the firing position. In addition, the last mowing part has only a reasonable reach and there is almost no stepping, so don't think too much about the reach of the blade firing part and try to activate it as close to the target as possible.
