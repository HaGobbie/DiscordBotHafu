# Bullet Bow PA — 基本アクション

## Basic action
A charged shooting weapon that can be equipped by Braver. It is a weapon type composed of PA whose behavior changes depending on whether it is charged or not, and is characterized by a rich repertoire of attack methods. Although it is a shooting weapon, it does not have an appropriate distance correction, and its strength is that it can exert stable power regardless of the distance. However, it is difficult to be flexible in the elevation and depression angle of shooting, and even if you aim directly below from an over-the-shoulder perspective, the bullet will fly forward at about 30 degrees, so when shooting from a high altitude or from a high place, you will not be able to respond if it sneaks below you. Evasion and counter actions are possible using weapon actions and related skills, but as shown below, they are extremely difficult to use and have poor performance, so there is little benefit when aiming. You don't need to be conscious of it except in situations where you have to respond to enemy attacks, such as when you are likely to accidentally activate it, when playing solo, where you have to rely on it, or when a boss uses a series of wide-range attacks or coordinate attacks. In multiplayer, it's basically safe to act as a battery from a distance. Due to this characteristic, if the enemy's target is facing you, the long range advantage will not activate and it will be extremely difficult to fight, so a hate reduction build that utilizes hate reduction skills (sub-Ra) and EXOP is essential. In addition, as a countermeasure to the drawback of being vulnerable to being flinched by stray bullets, I would like to make full use of subclass Hu's Massive Hunter and Extra Shield, as well as some skill rings, Brave Combat Arrowmates, and Bullet Bow Attack Impact, which provide robustness. It also has the disadvantage that its PP recovery power is somewhat low (especially when you are in the rear line and are not aware of counters), so you should consider using PP reduction reduction during Brave Combat, PP recovery from Finish Prize, and cover with subclasses and EX special abilities. The weapon action is the activation of an evasive motion as described above. A counter attack from there is also possible, but its raw power is not high. If you acquire the relevant skills, you will be able to pursue attacks and avoid counters by inputting weapon actions during PA, so you will need to be aware of this in terms of firepower. However, Bullet Bow is mainly a charge PA or a PA with a large gap, and it is difficult to cancel timing with weapon actions, so it is difficult to use in its original state. Step cancels are possible with some flexibility, but step counters are also less powerful. Regarding this point, by repainting the characteristics of the PA through various TechArs customizations, it becomes easier to take advantage of weapon actions. In solo battles where attacks frequently fly at you, you won't be able to play with fixed turrets, so you want to make good use of it to cover up your shortcomings. After adjusting Braver for 4/2, the overall firepower has been raised, which has improved the firepower problem to some extent, and combined with the fact that the chances of activating Bullet Bow Attack Impact have increased significantly due to the influence of the new skill Combat After PA Extension, the firepower when applying the long range advantage has become able to be at a reasonable level. As before, it is also powerful as a sub for the katana, in charge of waiting and pre-emptive attacks, and when the enemy is defeated, it can discharge the stocked firepower enhancement elements all at once. On the other hand, compared to the Katana, it is definitely inferior in counter firepower, and it is difficult to continuously produce PA firepower without a long range advantage, so it is quite difficult to tie up with bosses. Related class skills: Slow Landing Bullet Bow
