--- [Basic action] ---
One of the weapons that can be equipped by gunners. It is also a weapon type that cannot demonstrate its true performance at all without making full use of the Gunner's main class skills, and even if Gunner is set as a subclass, it is useless, so it is almost treated as a weapon exclusively for the main class Gunner. Therefore, on this page we will only list information when using Gunner. It is characterized by a weapon action that combines evasion and special behavior, and can constantly attack by evading attacks and counterattacking at the same time. It behaves much more easily than other shooting weapons, has outstanding evasion performance, and has extremely high aerial combat performance and airborne performance, and depending on its behavior and PP value, it can continue to engage in high-mobility aerial combat for an extended period of time. In addition to single-target attacks, if Tech Arts customization is included, range attack performance is also quite excellent, and it is also attractive that it can be used in multiplayer scenes regardless of the scene. Although it is a shooting weapon, the appropriate range for the weapon is close range. Although it is possible to attack from a long distance, it is a weapon that can demonstrate its true value by making full use of weapon actions and maintaining close to medium range. As mentioned above, as soon as you get a little far away from the target, the firepower decreases significantly, so you must always fight while keeping in mind the appropriate distance = close range. The high evasion performance also seems to have been adjusted to take into account the fact that it is a weapon type that focuses on close-range combat. It is characterized by a set play in which it deals instantaneous large damage after accumulating a count with Chain Trigger, and at the same time uses the gauge accumulated in the process of chain accumulation to chain finish to continue rotating Hail of Bullets, an extremely powerful active skill, at the necessary timing. This Hail of Bullets has extremely good PP recovery ability and has a fairly good turnover rate, so it can be said to be the best weapon that has almost no trouble managing PP. The down value given is weak under normal circumstances, but since the effect of Chain Finish is now added to the down value, it has become a powerful level suitable for a melee specialized class when viewed as a whole. For counter attacks, you are given two choices: step counters and weapon action counters by learning class skills, but both are still limited to attack performance that does not go beyond the range of shooting weapons. However, if you carefully calculate the S-roll shooting + counter bullet after strengthening, it has been strengthened to a high level of DPS of 566 or higher when you carefully consider the series of turns from chain storage to chain finish, so there is no longer a need to use assault rifles as multi-weapons and leave counters to them like before. Related class skills “Hail of Bullets” Gauge-based continuous attack “Hail of Bullets Amplify” *Main class only Gauge acceleration ▲ ▼

--- [weapon action] ---
Weapon action for “twin machine guns”. Avoid enemy attacks while moving. Press the normal attack button during a weapon action to fire more shots. Image Action Normal Chain Boost S Roll → Normal Power 20×4 15×6 Recovery PP 2×4 1.5×6 SR Arts Power 25×4 26×4 Recovery PP 0 SR Counter B Power 2.5×8 Recovery PP 0 S Onslaught Power 135 Recovery PP 0 Performs a stylish roll that allows you to avoid enemy attacks. It is abbreviated as "S roll", "SR", etc. Rotate your body and move forward, backward, left and right as if bouncing. There is no limit to the number of consecutive attempts. When performing an S-roll in the air, it is possible to maintain altitude if the locked enemy is within the appropriate distance, but if it is outside the appropriate distance, the altitude will gradually decrease with each S-roll. Previously, its performance as an evasion method was questionable due to its evasive invincibility specifications, but with the adjustments made on December 15, 2021, the invincibility time will now be extended upon successful evasion, greatly increasing its reliability. Even though there is Dodge PP Gain, it cannot be connected to strengthening actions that are non-consumable and have excellent PP recovery like other classes' actions with counters, so it will be easier to fight if you are aware of how to use step counters depending on the remaining PP. --- [About appropriate distance] ---
Appropriate Distance Assault rifles, twin machine guns, and wired lances have an ``appropriate distance'', and the damage will increase or decrease depending on the distance to the enemy. If the target marker is within the appropriate distance, the target marker will turn orange, but since the actual judgment is made at the hit location of the attack, it does not necessarily mean that the damage will be at the appropriate distance. The marker is located at the center of the area, but the attack will hit the outer skin (for example, the legs of the Daidal type). The core part is set so that it doesn't shift too much, but it does shift when you lock the core on the surface of the body and attack from behind, or lock the head and attack from the feet. As a countermeasure, pay close attention to the effect differences and damage numbers below. Lower-class and large enemies that get caught in the damage cap are useful as practice partners. If you hit an attack at the appropriate distance, a strong purple light effect will occur.
