--- [Basic action] ---
A technical legal attack weapon that can be equipped by Bouncer and is suitable for close combat using kicking techniques and technique use. Overall, it is fast, has a high performance PA, and is good at aerial combat. Press and hold each PA to consume additional PP and change its power and performance. It's easy to understand if you think that if you press and hold, the second half of the PA becomes different. There are many actions that become possible by acquiring skills (such as pursuing attributes after PA, engaging enemies after using techniques, etc.), but surprisingly there are only a moderate number of skills that are exclusive to the main bouncer, and sub-bouncers are sometimes used for powerful skills such as Jet Intensity. However, since the power of each PA is modest, in order to use it as a main weapon, the rotation rate of Sturmsieker is important = it needs to be a main bouncer. Technique Automatically move forward while charging. Also, you cannot jump, and when you start in the air, you will descend slowly. For forward movement, you can move at the same speed as a dash by inputting forward movement. This charge movement always applies speed forward, and the speed can only be adjusted in the front and back directions relative to the character's direction, so if you lock the enemy, you will continue to close the distance (even if you input backwards, you will gradually move forward). If it is unlocked, you can change the direction of movement (forward movement is mandatory, but you can change the direction of the character, so you can move to the side or in front of the screen). Similarly, from an over-the-shoulder perspective, you cannot directly move the character left or right, but you can adjust the direction of movement by changing the camera angle and changing the character's direction. When the technique is activated, the weapon action or step will freeze with a motion that cannot be canceled, so there is a big gap. Due to the unique specifications of jet boots mentioned above, it cannot be said that they are easy to use as a technique firing weapon, and in this area, it will be limited to use as a main bouncer rather than an action weapon. In addition, jet boots in ``NGS'' are treated as legal weapons like wands, and all attacks are treated as legal attacks. In the update on December 6, 2023, sparkling effects were added to each action. When used on the ground, it moves forward, when in the air, it attacks on the spot, and the fourth stage moves forward and retreats a little regardless of open space. If everything is in close contact with the lock target, it will not move forward. The 4th stage can be used by learning the class skill "Jet Attack Extra". It floats a little when used on the ground. The inability to avoid right after the 4th hit, which had been bothering Boots for a long time, has been resolved with the update on December 6, 2023.
