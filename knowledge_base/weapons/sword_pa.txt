--- [Basic action] ---
A weapon type that wields a large sword held in both hands. Although the attack speed is a little slow, the operation feels straightforward. The attack range and attack range are quite good among melee weapons, and it is an easy-to-understand and easy-to-handle weapon. It has attack methods that can properly respond to single-target attacks, area attacks, and high-firepower fixed-point attacks, and overall it is a textbook-like weapon suitable for learning the basics of close combat and how to move like a hunter. Also, by using Tech Arts Customization, you can obtain long-range attack methods. The performance of the weapon action guard is also excellent, and it is possible to cancel with the guard at any time during PA, making it highly safe. Hunter Arts Avenge is also easy to activate, and related skills (Hu Arts Just Paring, Hu Arts Avenge PP Gain, Hu Arts Paring Advance) are useful. Furthermore, as a feature only for swords, it is possible to automatically activate PA with Avenge with Sword Arts Charge Avenge. Even if you're seeing an enemy for the first time, you can find out the parry timing just by charging it. Additionally, by learning Sword Arts Overcharge, the charge will become two stages, and you will be able to choose the activation stage depending on the enemy's behavior. ▲ ▼

--- [weapon action] ---
Weapon action for “sword”. Hold up a large sword to block attacks from the front. Continuing by holding down the weapon action button, when attacked, PP is consumed to reduce damage. By performing this right before receiving an attack, you can become a Just Guard and nullify the attack without consuming PP. Image Action Guard Damage rate 25% PP consumption 20 Guard counter power 200 Recovery PP 8 Counter plus power 630 Recovery PP 20 While holding down the button, performs a guard that can only block the front. If you are attacked while guarding, PP will be consumed and the damage will be greatly reduced. If you receive an attack immediately after the guard is activated, it will become a Just Guard (JG), which is different from a normal guard, and completely negates damage and PP consumption with a unique sound effect. Just Guard is very good as a defensive action because the delay in judgment is quite lenient. Please note that there are also enemies such as Crag Bear that have attacks that cannot be blocked. When Just Guard is established, you will be invincible for a certain period of time. The invincibility period is long, and it exists to the extent that if you JG the slash of Pedas Sword, you can avoid the straight arm thrust immediately after by swinging the first hit while being invincible (you will get hit if you are caught in the back step). When acquiring the skill "Just Guard PP Gain", PP will be recovered when just guarding. When you acquire the skill "Sword Guard Counter", you will automatically unleash a counter attack when just guarding. Without the skill, even if you succeed in JG, you will be frozen for a short time. When learning the skill "Sword Guard Counter Plus", you can perform the enhanced version of the 5th attack by inputting a normal attack after a successful Just Guard. This 5th stage of the normal attack is more powerful than the normal attack, and it is invincible from the start until the end of the motion, leaving no chance. There are also attacks such as the ground version of Nex Vera's flame throw that allows the next JG to arrive in time while maintaining invincibility time. The reach is quite long due to the step of one step and the front swing. The impact wave causes a sideways hit. You can use it immediately by holding down the normal attack while countering, but the input reception time itself is not very long. Therefore, input after the counter has finished appearing may result in a misfire. If the enemy moves a lot during the guard counter, the counter plus is likely to miss, so depending on the enemy's behavior, it is important to be sure not to give the plus. Although it is a skill from another class, Braver's "Mighty Guard" is applied. Therefore, if you have a configuration that includes Br, you will have less loss even with a normal guard, making it easier to practice defense.
