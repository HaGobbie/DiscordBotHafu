--- [Section: 概要] ---
The official abbreviation is Fi (Fighter). It can be equipped with the highly mobile Twin Dagger (PA), the high firepower Double Saber (PA), and the Knuckle (PA) specialized for counter attacks. A melee vanguard class that specializes in attacks, featuring the skill "Limit Break" which greatly increases the damage dealt in exchange for increasing the damage received for a certain period of time. The amount of HP it has is second only to the hunter as it is a close combat class, but as mentioned above, its durability is not high because it is an attack-focused style that abandons defense. However, since he possesses a skill that completely prevents instant death due to accidental bullet damage during Limit Break, his resulting durability is comparable to Hunter's. Its attack performance against single opponents is extremely high, and it boasts incredible firepower that overwhelms other classes. On the other hand, with the exception of some customized PAs, they are basically weak in team fights. Each weapon type's PA is divided into two parts, the first half and the second half, and the feature is that you can fight by combining or using them properly. Class skills other than Limit Break are also full of skills that are directly connected to firepower, such as skills that increase damage to downed enemies and automatic pursuit skills that speed up rotation by consciously using more PA, and the lineup is truly worthy of the name of an attack-specialized class. On the other hand, due to the fact that it is a class that specializes in close combat without sacrificing durability, and the difficulty of operating each weapon type, it is also one of the most hectic to operate. No matter what type of weapon you choose, it can be said to be a class for experts, requiring a certain amount of effort and time to demonstrate its true value. Implemented on June 5, 2024 Accumulate a gauge by hitting enemies with PA multiple times. If you hit PA while the gauge is at maximum, an additional attack will occur. During one gauge accumulation, the gauge accumulation amount of attacks that have not yet hit an enemy during the first half and second half of each PA increases. *Main Class/Fighter Weapon Only Image Lv. 1 Additional attack recast 20 seconds If you hit PA while the icon gauge at the top right of the palette is filled, a high-power area attack similar to an explosion will automatically occur. With multi-stage PA, it is activated in consecutive attacks immediately after it is charged, so it is difficult to control the activation timing accurately. The pursuit occurs at the coordinates of the lock target, not at the area hit by the PA. Regardless of the type of weapon, the gauge will fill up to the maximum if you hit about seven different patterns among each PA. The amount accumulated differs for each PA, and the longer the motion is, the so-called wild dance type PA tends to accumulate more. Generally, it accumulates when you hit the first hit, but in some wild dance type PAs, it doesn't accumulate until you hit the final hit. If you repeat attacks that have already been used before activation, the amount of gauge accumulated will drop significantly. The extent to which the accumulated amount decreases varies depending on the PA, ranging from about 1/4 of the initial amount to less than 1/10. For example, if you attack a training target with Twin Dagger PA Vulture Moment, it will accumulate over 70 times if you only use the first half, and about 20 times if you only use the second half. If the recast has been completed, the character's right hand will glow when the sword is drawn (the amount of gauge accumulated is irrelevant, so glowing = not ready for activation). --- [Section: ナックルアーツコンボPPセイブ] ---
When activating PA continuously, PP consumption decreases according to the number of times. *Main class/Knuckle only Image Lv. 1 2 3 Reduction rate 5% 8% 10% Reduction rate upper limit 60% 40% 20% The number of consecutive shots to reach the upper limit is 13, 6, and 3, respectively. To achieve efficiency exceeding Lv.3 at Lv.1-2, you need to fire it four times or more in a row, which is difficult if you have maximum PP and are not down for a long time. Therefore, basically it seems that learning Lv.3 is enough. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: ナックルアーツコンボボーナス] ---
When the first half of PA is activated, the PP consumption reduction rate of Knuckle Arts Combo PP Save further increases. *Main class only/Knuckle only Image Lv. 1 - - Knuckle Arts Combo PP save reduction rate will be doubled to 10%, 16%, and 20%, respectively. The upper limit of the reduction rate itself does not change, so if you want to increase the maximum PP and release PA continuously until PP runs out, it is better to set Knuckle Arts Combo PP Save to Lv.1 or 2. Example: With a maximum PP of 100, the number of consecutive Swift Rushes you can fire is 5/6/5 at Lv.1/2/3 respectively, but with a maximum PP of 150, it becomes 11/9/7. ▲ ▼
