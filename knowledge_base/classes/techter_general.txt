## shifter deband
[Active Skill] For a certain period of time, the damage dealt to enemies by you and surrounding characters increases, and the damage received decreases. *Main class only Image Lv. 1 Power 105% Damage taken 90% Effect time 60 seconds Maximum effect time 60 seconds Recast 15 seconds As stated in the description, it gives a buff effect that increases damage dealt and reduces damage taken. Resources such as PP are not required. There is no preparatory action, and the effect is activated immediately. The effect area remains for approximately 5 seconds after use. If you touch it before it disappears, you can get the effect. The upper and lower hit detection is thicker than it appears, and can be reached at altitudes equivalent to a normal double jump. If the player becomes incapacitated, the effect of Shifta Deband will disappear. This class skill is unusual in "NGS" in that the "cool time starts immediately after the effect is activated (during the effect duration)", making it one of the few skills that can impart a buff effect that can be maintained at all times. As a side note, class skills other than these generally have a ``cool time that starts after the effect ends'', and require ``effect time + cool time'' before they can be used again. In the ``Phantasy Star'' series works before ``NGS'', ``Shifter'', which increases attack power, and ``Deband'', which increases defense power, appeared as techniques. In this work, it is not a skill to learn the techniques ``Shifter'' and ``Deband'', but is implemented as an active skill exclusive to Techter. ## Shifta Deband Party Share
When Shifta Deband is activated, it also applies the effect to characters in the same party who are far away. *For main class only Image Lv. 1 - - The effect will be applied no matter how far apart you are in the same room. When playing while moving with Photon Dash or Photon Glide, there are few opportunities for players to gather together and reapply Shifter/Deband. If you're going to play in a party, learning this will make you more comfortable.

## Long range shifter deband
[Active Skill] Activate Shifta Devand in front. The time until reuse is possible is shared with normal shifter debanding. *Main class only Image Lv. 1 - - A skill that changes the activation point of Shifter Deband from the user's feet to the target. ## foie brand
By hitting an enemy multiple times with a fire attribute technique, you can carve a mark of flame. If you hit it with a technique of the same attribute, the engraving will detonate, an additional attack will occur, and PP will be recovered. Image Lv. 1 - - Common skill of Force, Tector, and Bouncer. The number of techniques required to apply the stamp and detonate varies depending on the type of technique and whether or not there is a charge. See also fire element technique. In addition to techniques, it can be engraved with Fomergion, and can be imprinted and detonated with the Jet Boots Element Set. Elemental Bullet cannot do either. Accumulated value required for application and detonation (verification required) The accumulated value of each action is 2.7 for Foie (fireball 2.4 + vortex 0.1 × 3 hits), Charge Foie 3.8, Gifoie 2.7 (0.9 × 3 hits), Charge Gifoie 6 (2 × 3 hits), and Jet Boots pursuit 2.4. A stamp is given when the cumulative accumulated value is 9 or more, and it detonates when it is 14.5 or more. Since there is a maximum difference of 5.5 from the time of imposition to detonation, the detonation may not occur with the action immediately after the imprint is applied. The follow-up power when detonated is 80. PP recovery amount is 10. The power and PP recovery amount do not change depending on the type of technique or whether or not it is charged. foie brand

## barta blot
By applying an uncharged ice attribute technique to an enemy, it will carve an ice emblem that will be strengthened up to two levels depending on the number of times it is used. If you hit it with a charged technique of the same attribute, the emblem will detonate and deal additional damage according to the level of reinforcement. Image Lv. 1 - - Common skill of Force, Tector, and Bouncer. If you continue to use uncharged ice-based techniques, ice emblems will be carved on the enemy. (Upper limit is 2 levels) If you hit any charged ice attribute technique while the ice emblem is visible, the emblem will be consumed and a follow-up attack will occur. The number of hits required to carve a crest is 4 hits for the first stage and 7 hits for the second stage. The emblem will disappear automatically after about 15 seconds after being carved. Even if you hit a charge attack with no emblem (1st to 3rd stage), the number of stages will not be reset. The pursuit power is 200 in the first stage and 400 in the second stage. It is not affected by the detonation technique, and whether it is detonated with Bata or Gi Bata, the power will be the same if it is at the same stage. If you destroy parts (including the enhancer) using a detonation technique, the emblem will be reset without an explosion. The emblem detonation itself does not have a hit detection, but is it treated as a follow-up attack that is triggered at a time difference? It seems that if you apply the detonation technique as described above and at the same time cause part destruction or the part is hidden, a phenomenon will occur where no damage will be done even if the emblem detonation is activated. In this case, the emblem will be reset since the detonation itself has occurred. Note that ice-attribute techniques changed by Wondo's ice-attribute attack explosion or Talis' PA are not included in this count. Pursuit attacks with Elemental Bullet or Jet Boots Element Set are counted. If you set the particle quality in the graphic settings to 2 or higher, the emblem will become white and clearly visible. ## Grants glitter
By applying the light attribute technique to an enemy multiple times, a ring of light will develop around itself for a certain period of time. During deployment, if you use a technique with the same attribute, an additional attack will occur depending on the technique. If you deploy the ring of light again while it is being deployed, the effect will not overlap, and instead, the effect time of the ring of light that is currently being deployed will be reset. Image Lv. 1 - - Common skill of Force, Tector, and Bouncer. The halo lasts for 10 seconds. Additional attacks vary depending on the technique, so refer to Technique/Light Attribute. If it is a light attribute technique, it can be deployed and maintained regardless of whether it is charged or uncharged, and additional attacks do not consume dedicated resources, so after deploying the light ring, you can fight without worrying about its operation. The deployment technique by Talis PA cannot deploy and maintain the ring of light, and no additional attacks will occur. Pursuit with the Jet Boots Element Set allows you to deploy and maintain a light ring. The number of hits required for deployment and maintenance is not simply the number of hits, but a cumulative value set for each technique's attack judgment is counted each time it hits, and when it reaches a certain level, it will be deployed and maintained, which seems to be similar to physical down/attribute down. For example, Granz can deploy the ring of light in 4 hits with either charged (4 attacks) or non-charged (1 attack), but if he continues to hit the charged version only for the first hit, he can finally deploy it on the 19th hit. On the other hand, if Gi Grants (charged and non-charged attack twice) hits many enemies at once, it can be deployed in one attack. The hit count during the development of the ring of light is maintained even after it disappears. For example, if you hit the non-charged version of Grunts three times during deployment and then disappear, you can deploy it again just by hitting the non-charged version of Grunts one more time. Grants glitter

## Megidosphere
By hitting an enemy with an uncharged dark attribute technique, you can stock up to 3 dark beads around yourself. Furthermore, when you use a charged technique of the same attribute, all Dark Beads are consumed, and the technique is strengthened according to the number consumed. Image Lv. 1 - - Common skill of Force, Tector, and Bouncer. The Dark Bead lasts approximately 20 seconds. Additional attacks differ depending on the technique, so refer to Technique/Dark Attribute. In addition to the non-charged version of the technique, you can also stock Dark Beads with Elemental Bullet and Jet Boots Element Set, but you cannot stock them with Talis Deployment Technique. Also, if you hit multiple enemies with Megiddo, you can stock up to 3 at once. It is difficult to say that stocking up Dark Beads one by one using the non-charged version technique is efficient in terms of PP, so be aware of stocking up using the methods described above. One of the benefits of being a subtector is that techniques can be used even in non-legal shooting positions. Although it is a limited situation, there is room for consideration in situations such as short-term battle quests with only a single boss, where ``with other subclasses, the photon blast gauge will not fill up completely, or even if it does, the opponent's HP will be low'' and ``you can achieve the quest progression requirements faster by firing a single photon blast, rather than using the increase in expected damage obtained by subclasses other than Tector.'' If you set it as a subclass of Main Force, you will get benefits other than increasing the photon blast gauge. Tallis and attribute-related skills are shared, so if you take them in a class with extra skill points, you can transfer the points to the other class. Even if the same skills that exist between the main and sub classes are acquired overlappingly by both, the effect will not increase and the overlapping will simply be wasted, so when combining classes with common skills, you will ultimately have to think about ``which skill tree should you acquire that skill from?'' when building skills. ``U Technique Gauge Amplify Te'' and ``U Technique Gauge Amplify Fo'' are treated as separate skills, and their effects can be used together, resulting in the great benefit of increasing the turnover rate of composite attribute techniques.
