--- [Section: 概要] ---
Implemented on August 4, 2021 The official abbreviation is Br (Br aver). Can be equipped with a short-range weapon, Katana (PA), and a long-range weapon, Bullet Bow (PA). A special composite performance class that has two types of specialized weapons, one specialized for close-range combat and one specialized for long-range combat, with completely different characteristics and ranges. However, no matter which weapon you use as your main weapon, a hate reduction build is required to activate the long range advantage that is the lifeline of the bullet bow, and if you use a hate reduction build, it will be difficult to counter, which is the main strength of the katana, so it is extremely difficult to use both weapons in a perfect balance. Particularly in multiplayer, most situations are such that players only need to specialize in one or the other of their ranges. Due to this characteristic, the characteristics of both parties when they are holding their respective weapons as their main weapon are listed in the explanation summary section of each weapon performance page, not on this page. Regarding class skills that do not depend on the weapon used by the Braver itself, it is characterized by the fact that most of the skills are defensive skills. The main skills are skills that increase survival ability, such as increasing the recovery amount of Resta Sign, recovering PP when using Resta Sign, and recovering HP when recovering from a downed situation. Similarly, compared to Hunter and Waker, whose class skills specialize in defensive configurations, those two classes specialize in reducing damage itself, while Braver can be said to specialize in the ability to rebuild after actually receiving damage.
