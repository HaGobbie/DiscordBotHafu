--- [overview] ---
Implemented on October 13, 2021 Official abbreviation is Bo (Bo uncer). It is characterized by having many class skills related to physical down, attribute down, and body parts destruction. The weapons he uses are the Dual Blades (PA), which is a melee weapon but can also handle mid-range combat, has a range attack ability that is on par, and boasts strong counter performance despite its unique operational feel, and Jet Boots (PA), which have a technical firing function, have high firepower during knockdown, and are highly capable of responding to aerial combat. Dual Blades and Jet Boots both have unique and excellent performance, and can be used to great effect even if you specialize in either. Furthermore, by combining the two and handling them appropriately, it is possible to increase firepower and support performance. Because the firepower can be greatly increased by player skills such as accurate counters and ingenuity in controlling movements with small movements, the difficulty level of operation when pursuing maximum firepower is also extremely high. In particular, dual blades have many unique specifications and require a certain amount of skill to operate stably. In addition, there are class skills for various knockdowns that can make the situation worse if you learn them without thinking, so it is essential to have prior knowledge. Overall, it can be said to be a class for intermediate players and above, with high performance but difficult to handle. Bouncer subclass Bouncer EX style Bouncer as a subclass Comments ▲ ▼

--- [Defeat PP gain] ---
Recovers PP when enemies around you go down. *Main class only Image Lv. 1 PP recovery rate 50% PP is recovered the moment you are down. It is effective not only for physical downs and attribute downs, but also for downs caused by parts destroyed by boss enemies such as BREAK and Dark False. Falls due to dive attacks or launch effects are not covered. For more information, please also refer to the Enemy Down/Part Destruction page. The amount of recovery is high, and the small fry Dolls after Ritem have a lot of physical strength, but there are more things that lower attributes, and if you properly aim for weak attributes, you can save a lot of trouble in recovering PP. Especially during a PSE burst, if there are multiple Tallis users in the room, it will be activated every few seconds, so there is no need to collect PP on your own. The targets of small fry are mainly to reduce the weaknesses of various medium-sized Dolls and Starless. It is easy to recognize the Gororon type's abdominal hardening (treated as a special down due to attribute down value accumulation) and the Dots type's foot punch (physical down), which commonly appear in the 4 regions and Luciel. If you're facing a boss-class opponent, you'll have fewer chances to activate it, but the original recovery amount is high, so you can rely on it. If you can calculate the rate of down value accumulation based on the class composition of the room, you can also adjust the timing when PP is depleted. The reaction distance needs to be verified. Since it is activated separately from your own actions, it can also be used in combos with continuous PAs such as Fear Eraser and Blaze Shot. ▼ Adjustment/Modification History 2024/06/05 Changed specifications so that the effect is applied to downs other than physical downs and attribute downs ▲ ▼

During deployment, if you use a technique with the same attribute, an additional attack will occur depending on the technique. The hit count during the development of the ring of light is maintained even after it disappears.
