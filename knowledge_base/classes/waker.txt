--- [Section: 概要] ---
Implemented on August 3, 2022 The official abbreviation is "Wa" (Wa ker). Can be equipped with the only special weapon, Tact (PA). Tact belongs to the law weapon, but it cannot use techniques like the weapons handled by Force, Tector, and Bouncer. Make full use of the three photon lifeforms called "Familiar" with different specialties that are summoned by PA, and use each of them equally to accumulate a gauge and aim for the combination attack Familia Unison. The most notable class skill is Quince Patience's counter action from a pseudo guard. And, optional parry during PA action by hot quince parry. Because it is a defensive action that can be activated at any time, it is very easy to use, which is why it is a class recommended for people who have difficulty grasping the timing of counter attacks. In addition, there are some that have the effect of recovering HP when hit by a bullet, and others that increase the effect while suppressing the consumption of Resta Sign and Reverser Sign, and their durability is also attractive as that of Hunter. Skills that increase durability have a great effect when Waker is set as a subclass, so it is also an excellent companion for other classes that require durability. Thanks to Fredran Breath's range attack performance, it is highly suitable for multiple battles, but the firepower of PA in general is extremely low overall, and there is no way to improve it like long range advantage. Since the cancel points are also small, the normal combat power is particularly weak against single medium-sized or larger enemies. Therefore, in terms of attack, it is extremely dependent on how to constantly activate the two types of Familiar Assist to raise the standard firepower, quickly build up the gauge of the three Familiars, and fire as many shots as possible from the ultra-high firepower Familiar Unison Marmelo Plus. On the other hand, in an environment where the two types of assists and Quince Plus can be fully used, it is possible to display extraordinary firepower that is the highest among all classes and rivals that of the fighter, which is a class specializing in firepower. In any case, both offense and defense are strongly dependent on class skills, so be sure to understand the performance of all class skills. ▼ About the fighting style of “Waker” Based on the materials distributed at the advance trial venue of “ARKS EXPO 2022”, we will describe the basic fighting style of this class. Basic Fighting Method Each Familiar has a preferred fighting distance. If you want to attack from a long distance, use Fredoran, and if you want to fight at close range, use Wolfer. Quince, which can be summoned with a weapon action, accumulates energy while blocking attacks. Once the gear gauge is at maximum, hold down Weapon Action and throw a quince to deal great damage to the enemy. We recommend fighting by actively aiming for the powerful class skill "Familiar Unison". Check the gear gauges of Fredoran and Wolfer, and use the missing Photon Arts. Once you get used to it, a marker will appear by hitting the familiar's attack multiple times. By holding down the normal attack button, you can detonate the marker, recover a large amount of PP, and also generate a continuous attack called ``Familiar Assist''. "Familiar Assist" also has the effect of accumulating the gear gauge of the last familiar you summoned using PA, so while "Familiar Assist" is in progress, it's a good idea to fight mainly with the other familiar. By performing a normal attack in line with the purple guide that appears after the PA, a "linear drive" will occur. It can be moved forward or backward depending on the input direction. This is useful when switching between "Fredran" and "Wolfer" or when you want to adjust the distance between you and the enemy. "PSO2 New Genesis New Class 'Waker' Explanation Video" is also available on the official YouTube channel, so be sure to check it out as well. ▼ Differences from the old PSO2's "Summoner" The decisive difference when comparing Waker with "Summoner" is that, like other classes, it is a "normal class" that becomes stronger by increasing its class level and strengthening its weapons. Familiars do not have any breeding elements like the old PSO2 pets, and a fixed one is summoned from among three types depending on photon arts and weapon actions. Also, Direct attacks using tact are also possible. Unlike Summoner's Tact, which could not be given special abilities, Waker's Tact can be given special abilities. Overall, Waker is a new class that is very different from the old PSO2 Summoner. Waker subclasses Waker EX style Waker as a subclass Comments

When set to a subclass of Force or Tector, it can be expected to have the effect of increasing the number of times the class skill "Restafield" can be used. There was an issue where the reduction in the amount of recovery from Resta Effect Continuance was also affecting range recovery, but this was fixed as a bug on August 31, 2022. As previously announced, "Waker" is a class that is similar to "Summoner". Until now, the classes distributed in "NGS" were designed as classes that followed the specifications of the old classes in "PSO2". However, there were some parts that were arranged for "NGS", and some people were confused by the difference in this respect from the same class in "PSO2". Therefore, when we decided to create a completely new class that uses tact as a weapon but goes in a different direction from "Summoner", we were concerned that if we kept the same class name, more people would feel uncomfortable with the difference from the old "Summoner" than the previous class, so we devised a completely different name. Another reason is that we wanted to encourage those who weren't interested in PSO2's Summoner to learn about Waker as a new class without preconceived notions. Furthermore, this costume is based on the theme of "Waker", but while the costumes of previous classes were based on white, the costume is based on red to further emphasize the difference from previous classes.
