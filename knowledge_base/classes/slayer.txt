--- [Section: 概要] ---
Implemented on April 12, 2023 Abbreviation is "Sl" (Slayer). A shooting class that uses Gun Slash (PA) as its only exclusive weapon. Stylish and dynamic battles unfold, including rapid continuous attacks that interweave various actions. Due to the characteristics of Gun Slash PA and class skills, although it is a shooting class, in reality it mainly focuses on close combat. Overall, it has excellent mobility, and while it has long invincibility evasion actions and class skills, its basic HP is the lowest among all classes. (Reference: Status/Class Comparison) Although it has excellent firepower, mobility, and evasion, it can be said to be an attack-specialized class that is vulnerable when hit. Since its basic HP is low, it is very effective to follow up its HP with armor, special abilities, and add-on skills. If you cover it properly, durability issues can be suppressed to the point where you don't have to worry about it. If you prepare properly, you will enjoy good operability with few quirks. On the other hand, the exclusive weapon Gun Slash has a gear gauge, which is rare in NGS, so it requires the ability to manage that gauge (at the same time as managing the active skill's gauge and CT).Furthermore, the PA is divided into stay version and move version, and there are a wide variety of counter actions and types, so there is a lot of need to memorize and think about choices during battle. The difficulty level of operation is quite high because the slash pass that is activated by the normal attack input when using PA is required almost every time, so the operation is very busy. Based on the above characteristics, the overall review is that this class is suitable for intermediate level students and above. Many of the class skills are related to increasing the critical rate, and when used as both main and sub skills, firepower can be stably increased without activation conditions. --- [Section: 余談] ---
The dolls ``Dezas Slash'', ``Zamus Slash'', and ``Gazas Slash'' all carry Gunslash-like weapons, and their designs suggest a connection to Slayer. In the old ``PSO2'', ``Raster'' appeared as a successor class that had Gun Slash as its own weapon category. Similar to the examples of Waker and ``Summoner,'' Slayer is a class that is very different from Luster, but it can be seen as inheriting some of its concepts and designs, such as being good at combat that takes advantage of its high mobility. On August 7, 2020, Tetsuo Yoshioka, who was in charge of EP6 director of PSO2 at the time, revealed on the official web program "PSO2 STATION!+" ('20.8.7) that there were many twists and turns before deciding on the name of the new class as "Raster". In addition, in the ``Phantasy Star Online 2 EPISODE 6 Setting Document Collection'' (Dengeki Game Editorial Department, 2021), the costume ``Sardu Ozorel Style'' with the above-mentioned raster motif, and the setting images for the weapon camouflage ``*Victo Waleele'', ``*Graubenele'', and ``*Superbiaele'' are accompanied by the word ``Slayer'', which seems to be the class name. Based on these facts, it can be inferred that the name "Slayer" referring to this class was one of the ideas proposed when Raster was in its development stage. In the March 29th broadcast of "NGS Headline+" on the global version's official YouTube channel, the global version's community manager said that "Slayer" was chosen from several name candidates given by the Japanese development team. In the PV etc. released on the official YouTube channel, the effects of the actions performed by the Slayer-themed characters (Ogi & Ana) are green. This is due to Gunslash's exclusive weapon camouflage "*Slavage Slash", and the base color is usually light blue like other weapon categories. In the corner "NGS Operation Report" of the official web program "NGS Headlines" broadcast on March 28, 2023, two questions and answers regarding Slayer were introduced. ▼ About the concept of design and graphics (click to expand) Q. I would like to know the concept of the design and battle graphics of the new class "Slayer". A. Regarding the design, we were conscious of developing it to give a ``new'' impression that is different from previous classes by making the back side of the frame shine and using black as the main color. In terms of combat, we aimed to match the Slayer's high level of mobility and convey that feeling through the graphics. In particular, we developed actions such as counters and photon blasts to make the movements and effects feel more dynamic, so by having them intertwine in battle, you will be able to enjoy the actions that look great on screen. ▼ How did the class name become "Slayer" instead of "Raster" (click to expand) Q. I would like to know how the class name of the new class became "Slayer" instead of "Raster", whose main weapon was a gun slash like in "PSO2". A. Unlike Raster in PSO2, Slayer is not a "successor class." There are system differences such as the ability to set subclasses, and the operability is also different so that more users can play than Raster. Therefore, we have created a new class called "Slayer". Like Luster, Slayer's appeal is combat that takes advantage of its high mobility. I hope those who played Raster will enjoy it as well.
