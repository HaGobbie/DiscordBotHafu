He uses two types of weapons: the Assault Rifle (PA), which is easy to use, has good evasive power, and good mobility, and is good at attacking from medium range, and the Launcher (PA), which has a strong habit and low evasion ability, but has no distance attenuation and can be used regardless of range, so it is good at attacking from a long distance and has high explosive power at key points.
