--- [overview] ---
The official abbreviation is Ra (Ranger). A class that specializes in long-distance shooting attacks and possesses the ability to greatly increase the firepower of all allies with skills that have powerful debuff effects, and is excellent in support and support abilities. He uses two types of weapons: the Assault Rifle (PA), which is easy to use, has good evasive power, and good mobility, and is good at attacking from medium range, and the Launcher (PA), which has a strong habit and low evasion ability, but has no distance attenuation and can be used regardless of range, so it is good at attacking from a long distance and has high explosive power at key points. The featured class skill, Weak Bullet, is extremely powerful as it can be applied to an enemy to weaken the area it hits, greatly increasing the damage dealt to all multiplayer players on the spot. Other class-specific general-purpose skills include skills that are specialized for status abnormalities, such as increasing resistance to status abnormalities and shortening the duration of status abnormalities. It's probably worth considering mainly as a countermeasure against panic when searching for Luciel. If you manage weak bullets at the same time as producing firepower, the difficulty level of operation will be a little high because the weak bullets themselves require calm sniping accuracy. It would be best to act as a supporter and prioritize maintaining your weak bullet without overdoing it until you get used to it. Furthermore, the Assault Rifle has high survivability because you can acquire high-performance evasion and counter abilities by acquiring skills. On the other hand, if you use a launcher as your main weapon, your ability to ward off attacks will depend mostly on steps and will be quite unstable, so you will need to be careful and careful. Possessing the powerful Weak Bullet class skill and two types of high-performance shooting weapons, the Ranger is a powerful class that goes beyond being a simple debuff support job.However, due to recent advances in the strengthening of other classes in general, the ranger's attack performance is at a fairly modest level. As a result, the position and characteristics of a class that specializes in supporting allies has become considerably stronger. Ranger Subclass Ranger EX Style Ranger as a Subclass Comments ▲ ▼

[Active Skill] Fires a bullet that increases the damage dealt to the area it hits. *Main class/assault rifle only Image Lv. 1 2 3 4 5 Power 120% Maximum stock 3 times Effect time 15 seconds Recast 30 seconds 25 seconds 20 seconds 15 seconds 10 seconds *Hereinafter, "WB" will be used as an abbreviation for "Weak Bullet". As a common feature of active skills, they can also be set on weapon palettes. About markers: Adds a marker to the area hit by the bullet, increasing the damage multiplier to that area to 120% (1.2x) of the original value. If applied again to the same target during the effect period, the remaining time will be overwritten in 15 seconds. (15 seconds is not added to the remaining time) This effect affects all players in the same multiplayer field. In a multi-area, if there are multiple rangers, it is possible to add markers to each body part. The status of Weak Bullet Reinforcement is shared by each marker. The remaining time of the effect is not displayed. It's a good idea to shoot early while you're not used to it. About bullets The bullet itself also has a damage determination. Power is 220%. The bullet is fired immediately, but there is a loading motion after the bullet is fired, and there is a period of about 1 second in which the bullet cannot be attacked. Movement is possible. If you shoot all the stock, a cooldown will occur, and once it's over, you'll be able to shoot three shots again. It is slightly different from the skill with the same name in PSO2, and does not replace normal attacks with WB after activation. It behaves similar to A.I.S's "Weak Buster". The single-shot power is higher than a normal attack, and it does not require PP, so it is useful as an attack method. The behavior is normal shooting, so if the line of fire is blocked by other parts or obstacles, it will not hit the lock-on point. Also, since the bullet does not hit instantly, it may miss if you move at high speed at the time of firing. If the skill goes into a swimming motion between activation and firing, it will fail to fire. It's best to avoid fighting near water. At this time, if this misfire occurs when the remaining bullets go from 1 to 0, the cooldown will no longer occur, and the Weak Bullet may become unusable until you return to town (it will be fixed at 0 bullets remaining). --- [Weak bullet reinforcement] ---
By hitting the area hit by the Weak Bullet a certain number of times, the damage dealt will increase and the skill's effect time will be extended. *Main class only Image Lv. 1 Weak bullet reinforcement rate 125% Weak bullet extension time 15 seconds Required number of times 100 When the effect is activated, the weak bullet's red circle turns purple. The power multiplier increases by 5% from the original 120% to 125%. (1.25 times 120% does not equal 150%) The effect time extension (+15 seconds) is only when activated, so the effect time will not increase to 30 seconds when resetting the Weak Bullet while maintaining Reinforce. The number of hits required for activation counts not only your attacks but also those of everyone in the same multiplayer field. Even if a new Weak Bullet is applied when a Weak Bullet has already been applied, the activation status of Reinforce and the number of hits until activation will be carried over. The same applies if weak bullets are applied to different parts. If the Weak Bullet application part is destroyed, the activated state will be maintained if you quickly reapply the Weak Bullet. There are also enemies whose counts are reset when their parts are destroyed, such as Dark Falz's second form's arm and Luino Ragne's leg. If there are multiple rangers, if one person sticks it on the main body, it will not be reset when destroyed and will remain active. ▼ Change history 2023/06/07 The specifications have been changed so that the number of hits required for activation is determined by the total number of hits of all "Weak Bullets" attached to that enemy ▲ ▼

Shares the number of uses and time until it can be used again with normal weak bullets. *Main Class/Assault Rifle Only Image Lv. 1 - - Normal WB consumes one shot per shot and only applies to one target, whereas this one consumes one shot per shot and gives markers to all targets within range. Useful when dealing with a large number of small fish in defense or PSE burst. A weak bullet is attached to each body at the first point of contact, so it is difficult to target weak points. It is especially reliable for assault rifles that have a certain amount of firepower but have no range attack ability. As of 2022/06/08, it has been confirmed that using a wide range will make it easier to target Snork cores, etc., where the judgment is ambiguous. It is no exaggeration to say that this is the most important skill for a ranger. Affects all players in the room and increases damage dealt by 20-25%. Especially when you have to defeat multiple Despair Enemies by moving around the room, efficiency is greatly affected. If you are concerned about improving the firepower of all players, it is important to find the timing when some enemies will go down, save them, and immediately reposition them to their weak points. (Example: Types such as Daidal and Nex, whose weaknesses are usually not exposed but are exposed when knocked down.) Play styles are free, but if you are looking for efficiency, you should actively use them yourself. If there is someone else in the room who can stick it well, I can basically concentrate on using eraser etc. to generate firepower, and if I feel like that person makes a mistake and is likely to recast, it is even better if I can back up. Even if you miss a shot, if you rush to shoot again and again, you may end up getting stuck in the wrong place again, creating a vicious cycle. Stay calm when you make a mistake. The most painful case is the cooldown time after a miss shot, so I want to avoid it as much as possible. If you are the only one with Ra, be careful about remaining bullets, and if it is 1, it is better to shoot when you can. There are enemies such as Pangoran whose parts change even in normal times and whose WB appears and disappears, so it's best to understand the characteristics of each boss in advance. In this work, skills can also be set on the weapon palette. If you have enough space on your pallet, you can set up a weak bullet and shoot with a feeling similar to PSO2. ▲ ▼

--- [Ranger as a subclass] ---
Although he cannot use important support skills such as Weak Bullet, he can use the assault rifles Rifle Grenada and Spread Shot Auto Charge, as well as Obscure Form, which reduces hate. Weak Bullet Self Benefit is effective even when you cannot use Weak Bullet. It is especially effective for classes that mainly deal with technique, such as the Daidal type who want to attack areas that are not covered with weak bullets. For weapon types that support various Long Range Advantages, Obscure Form also becomes a pure firepower support skill. It can also transfer hate to weapons that are good at countering, which can also contribute to PT's firepower. Active skills are excellent in DPS and have the ability to recover PP, so they are excellent as a means of increasing firepower for shooting classes. Furthermore, since Spread Shot is invincible while in action, it also improves evasion ability. Furthermore, since the active skill refers to the power of the rifle, it tends to be difficult for striking classes and legal attack classes due to the OP. You can also use it by switching to a rifle, but if you attach it to a multi-weapon, you can see the various cooldown times, so we recommend that. It can also handle sticky bombs from launchers. Its power is subtle, but it can be used to recover PP. As for the status ailment reduction skill, the main threat is freezing from Kuvaris, so it can be used as a countermeasure, but whether it is effective or not depends on the opponent. It will provide stability when dealing with the boss enemy Raidal Sword and the normal enemy Zamus Launcher. When you wear equipment that lowers your status ailment resistance, your resistance will visibly increase, but as long as it doesn't lower it too much, an add-on may be enough. Although the range firepower of Assault Rifle PA/Homing Dart and the firepower of Launcher PA/Fear Eraser are excellent, please note that if it is not a main class weapon, the weapon boost will not be applied, so it will be inferior in terms of firepower. In order to deal with a large number of small fish when hunting small fish, it may be effective to subclass Ranger and use Homing Dart and Divine Impact. Compared to sub-Gu who can also use assault rifles, he has many assault rifle skills and is convenient to use. However, the sub-Gu also has a useful attack PP recovery for the main weapon, so choose it according to your play style. It is not effective to subclass Ranger and use a launcher in Despair Enemy, Emergency, etc. Use your main class weapons, make Ranger your main class if you have a good launcher, or use multi-weapons. Divine Impact when fully charged can be used as a powerful waiting move. Charge it in advance when your opponent is temporarily unable to attack.
