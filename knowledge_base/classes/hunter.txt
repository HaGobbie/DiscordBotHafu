--- [overview] ---
The official abbreviation is Hu (Hunter). It can be equipped with the Sword (PA), which is easy to use and has no weak points, the Wired Lance (PA), which has a wide attack range, and the Counter-oriented Partisan (PA). A class that can use guarding and parrying techniques to evade enemy attacks and deal great damage with counter attacks. It is a strong and self-contained class that attracts enemies with War Cry, withstands attacks with Massive Hunter and delivers a powerful charge PA, and uses Photon Arts Avenge to further increase its power and earn the enemy's hate value to serve as a shield and sword for the party. However, Massive Hunter has a long recast time even though its effect time is short when learned alone. You will also need to adjust the short cycle acquisition level to the operational purpose, and be calm enough to use guard or counter during the cooldown time. --- [war cry] ---
[Active Skill] Attracts the attention of surrounding enemies for a certain period of time. Additionally, you will be more likely to be targeted by the enemy you hit. *Main class only Image Lv. 1 Hate effect 800% Effect time 30 seconds Recast 30 seconds There is no hate reset effect. It seems to be a process of adding a fixed value. In boss enemy battles that you joined late, you won't be able to get enough Top Hate in one War Cry, but you can get it by doing it multiple times. However, depending on your stance, it may become difficult for other players to fight. If you decide to target an enemy, you want to make it easier for not only yourself but also other players to fight. Rather than attracting enemies and protecting allies, the main purpose of this skill is to control the enemy's direction and position in a way that makes it easier for allies to fight enemies (make it easier to score counters, make it easier to involve multiple enemies, etc.). This is especially useful in situations where the boss is moving around while the player taking the hate moves, or where multiple bosses appear. When you have the boss's hate, it is recommended that you do not change the boss's position or direction as much as possible. However, there may be situations where it is better to keep them away from the ocean where they are forced to swim or in forests with poor visibility. For bosses that use backsteps a lot, it is also effective to have them turn their backs to the mountain side. With the addition of Long Range Advantage as a skill for long-range weapons, the advantage of continuing to take hate from enemies in multiplayer boss battles has increased. ▼ Adjustment/Modification History 2025/02/05 Adjusted the hate effect upward (200% → 800%) Expanded the range that attracts the attention of surrounding enemies ▲ ▼

--- [Hunter Arts Avenge] ---
If you are attacked by an enemy within a certain amount of time after the PA is activated, the power of that PA will increase. *Hunter weapon only Image Lv. 1 Power 150% Down value increase 150% "Activation" in the description refers to the moment you release the button if it is a charge PA. This allows you to ignore knockdowns and blowbacks and hit PA. Please note that there is no damage reduction. Can be activated even with attacks that cannot be blocked. You can also use the damage zone and status abnormality shock to knock back. Even with attacks that are impossible to block or evade, which were introduced in Dark Fals Souras, if you time it right, you can still take damage and still perform Avenge. If you use it, it's best to also use Massive Hunter to limit the damage. --- [sword attack alternation] ---
After Hunter Arts Avenge is activated, the first normal attack will turn into a powerful attack. *For sword only Image Lv. 1 - - A high-speed thrust that stops before the lock part, followed by a sweep from right to left. As it looks, the judgment is wide and it is easy to involve multiple people. Furthermore, there are guard points all around from the start to the end of the motion. In addition to long steps and a wide range, it is an excellent attack method that has the same high single-shot power as the 5th hit of a normal attack and the amount of PP recovered as the 4th hit. Be careful as you cannot block unblockable attacks such as Cragbear type grabs. It has the property of ``activating in place of the normal attack that was originally supposed to occur.'' In other words, if you make a normal attack after activating this skill, the order will be "Alternation → Normal 2-5". By combining it with Arts Skip Attack class skills, it becomes possible to perform coordinated attacks such as "PA1 → PA1 Avenge → Alternation → Normal 4-5" and "PA1 → PA2 Avenge → Alternation → Normal 5". The route from Alter to Normal 5 has guard points all around it that last for nearly 2 seconds, making it a combo route that combines firepower and safety at a high level. If the activation timing overlaps with "Sword Guard Counter Plus", Sword Guard Counter Plus will take priority and the alternation will be activated on the next normal attack. If simultaneous presses of dive attack are enabled, dive attack will take priority if the inputs overlap. You need to be a little careful when you want to take out the alternator at high places.
