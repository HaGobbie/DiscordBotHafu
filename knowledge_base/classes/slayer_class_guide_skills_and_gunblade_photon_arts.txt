# Slayer Class Guide — Skills and Gunblade Photon Arts

Hunter | Fighter | Ranger | Gunner | Force | Techter | Braver | Bouncer | Waker | Slayer
Slayer is a class in Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis.

== Class Weapon Overview ==
Slayers have access to one unique weapon: Gunblades .

== Class Overview ==
Slayers are a highly mobile class with a large variety of abilities, through the use of their Gunblade weapons.
The Class Skill Gunblade Focus will cause the Focus Gauge to be displayed above the Main Palette. The Focus Gauge will build up after attacking enemies, increasing the Focus Gauge level. Your PP Consumption will decrease and your Attack Potency and Offensive PP Recovery will increase depending on the gauge level.
The Class Skill Gunblade Focus Overdrive can be activated by completely expending Gunblade Focus gauge when it is at its maximum level. This will greatly decrease your PP Consumption and greatly increase your Attack Potency and Offensive PP Recovery temporarily. Using this skill again while already activated will unleash a powerful finishing blow.

== Gunblade ==
Gunblades are highly-mobile ranged weapons that possess a mix of ranged attacks and potent melee attacks. Using Photon Arts while inputting a direction will cause the Photon Art to change.
The Class Skill Relentless Blade allows you to perform an additional attack if you press the Normal Attack button at just the right time while performing a Photon Art. The additional attack deals damage and has a PP recovery effect, so try to use this whenever you can.
