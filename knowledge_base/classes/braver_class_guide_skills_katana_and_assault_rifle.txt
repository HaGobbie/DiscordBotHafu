# Braver Class Guide — Skills, Katana and Assault Rifle Arts

Hunter | Fighter | Ranger | Gunner | Force | Techter | Braver | Bouncer | Waker | Slayer
Braver is a returning class in Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis. A class geared towards combat at any range. Can seamlessly switch between close and long-range weapons. Each attack is simple yet hides a deeper complexity.

== Class Weapon Overview ==
Bravers have access to two unique weapons: Katanas and Bows .

=== Katanas ===
An agile weapon that is ideal for rapid attacks. Katana Photon Art Combos can be used to dish out a variety of attacks when the same PA is performed repeatedly. Among the melee weapons, it has limited aerial mobility, which can make it inflexible for some combat scenarios.
Using the Guard Weapon Action, you can block attacks and negate their damage when guarding right before the attack hits you with a Perfect Guard, and release a counterattack with the skill Katana Guard Counter. Unlike Hunter's Sword Weapon Action, this Guard can only guard from the front.

=== Bows ===
A ranged weapon that has a wide variety of precise attacks depending on whether the Photon Art is charged or not. Unlike other ranged weapons, Bows do not need to be in optimal distance to the enemy to gain any bonus damage, so can freely attack from long ranges.
Using the Elusion Weapon Action, you can quickly evade enemy attacks in place.
