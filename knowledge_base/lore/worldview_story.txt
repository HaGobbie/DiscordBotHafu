--- [introduction] ---
The world of ``PSO2 New Genesis'' ≪Arks≫ It is a collective name for those who fight against the unknown life form ``Dolls'' on the planet Halfa. The battle with the Dolls has been going on for many years, but it is still unknown why the Dolls attack the people of Halfa or where they come from. Among the people living in Halfa, those with talent become Arks, but there are also people who become Arks for different reasons. ≪Starwalkers≫ The people of Halfa often called people who descended from space to earth in drop pods. The starwalkers have no memory of what happened before they descended. And, like the Dolls, it is unknown where they come to Halfa and for what purpose. However, the Starwalkers have unique abilities as Arcus, and many of them have chosen to coexist with the Halfans, and have dedicated themselves to the fight against Dolls as members of the Arcus. And now, as a new Arcus, a starwalker visits the planet's largest city, "Central City"... Quoted from https://pso2.jp/players/about/world/

--- [Footprints of Resurgent Arcs] ---
The organization ``Resurgent Arks'', whose governor is Zephet, has been conducting a long-term experiment with the aim of reviving the fighting group ``Arks'' that existed 1,000 years before the world in the story, in order to counter the powerful enemy ``Starless'' who suddenly appeared. In this section, we will organize the descriptions of Resurgent Arcs' activities shown in the work. "Arks" is an entity that Resurgent Arks has held up as an ideal being. The strongest fighting group that achieved fame in a major battle 1000 years ago. Thanks to the actions of Arcus, the war that took place 1,000 years ago came to an end, ushering in a long era of peace. At the same time, its great power became unnecessary and its existence was forgotten. After Starless appeared, the existence of Arcus began to attract attention again. In order to counter the threat of external enemies, Resurgent Arcus believed that the power of the former Arcus was necessary. By the time the power of Arcus was sought after again, many records had been lost to history. What they were able to recover was fragmentary battle records and some of the Arcus's genetic information. One of the genetic information recovered was from Arcus that had been put into cold sleep (dormant state). Arcus, which has been put into cold sleep, cannot be unfrozen due to strict protection, and the details remain unknown. Luciel was built based on the facilities in the Lower Nameless City Experimental Zone.

--- [Main story prologue (delivered on 2021/06/09)] ---
The story begins when the drop pod carrying the protagonist lands on Kanai Island, a small island in the southwestern part of West Airio. Aina, who was collecting Resta signs under Galois' instructions, discovers a "star-crossing" drop pod coming from space and rushes to the crash site. She manages to open the hatch of the burning pod and rescues the protagonist. However, unlike Manon, who had a variety of fragmented knowledge, the protagonist, like other "star travelers," has lost all memory except for his own name. On the way to Airio Town, where Aina and her friends live, the two are attacked by the Pedas Sword, and together with Manon, they fight them off. However, he is surrounded by a large number of Pedas Swords that appear soon, and finds himself in a predicament. Galois, who sensed a bad premonition, barges in and the three escape from the predicament. The group returns to base. When the protagonist arrives at Airio Town, he is welcomed by the residents as a new starwalker, and is taught about "Planet Halfa" and "Arks" at Galoa's house. The battle between the Dolls and the Arks has been going on for over 500 years, and it is said that the Starwalkers and the Halfans have been cooperating with each other since ancient times. Then, at a banquet held by Galois to welcome them, the protagonist is asked to fight alongside the Dolls as Arcus, and he agrees. Just as the party is about to get underway, a large number of dolls, including super-large dolls, suddenly appear on the sea. Arcus and the others from Airio Town engage in battle. Galois, who saw the town quickly collapsing and sensed the threat of the super-sized dolls, instructed the protagonist, along with Aina and Manon, to retreat to the western cave. Then, Galois's counterattack with all its strength was in vain as the super-large Dolls fired a cannonball, and the three people passed out in the aftermath. When he woke up, what spread out before him was the remains of Airio Town, which had been gouged out and disappeared. A search was carried out by the ARKS who came to the rescue from Central City, but no survivors were found other than the protagonists, and Galois' whereabouts were unknown. The protagonist and Manon, who are star walkers, escaped with only minor injuries, but Aina, an apprentice Arcus, was seriously injured and was transported to Central City first. The two arrive at Central City in pursuit of Aina, and are summoned to the command room by the Arks Supreme Commander Crawford. Crawford deduces that the super-large dolls that attacked Airio Town are "Dark Fals." This is said to be the same species as the one that caused severe damage to the entire Halfa area 100 years ago, destroying the then capital city in an instant. As the only witness to Dark Falz, Crawford requests the three main characters to cooperate in the fight against Dark False. With this acceptance, the protagonists became Arks belonging to Central City.

--- [Main story Chapter 1 (delivered on 2021/06/09)] ---
The next morning, the protagonists are asked by Crawford to head to Cocoon, where an instructor is waiting to learn the skills necessary to become an Arcus. Waiting inside Cocoon "First Step" was Arcus Instructor Bruder. Aina and Manon have received the same training, and Bruder has given them the seal of approval that they are capable of fighting on the front lines. Next, Crawford asks him to show off his abilities in actual combat and asks him to subjugate Nagles on Mount Magnus. Aina engages Nagles near the mountaintop with Bruder, who serves as support, but when Nagles becomes angry, Aina recalls the Super Large Dolls. After entrusting Aina, who has been pressurized, to Manon, Bruder and the protagonist confront Nagles. Repel this. Bruder tells Aina that it will be difficult to continue fighting unless she overcomes her fear, and advises her to recognize what she is fighting for. After saying goodbye to Bruder and returning to Central City to report his victory, the protagonist is on the lookout tower where Aina tells him of her determination to fight and her gratitude. While they were analyzing the data given to them by Crawford at a cafe, they received word that a large number of Dolls were occurring in the Resol Forest and the Wanford Laboratory ruins, so the three rushed to the Wanford Laboratory ruins. While unable to receive support from the command center due to jamming, the three destroy the Daedal Sword that appears at the ruins of the Wamford Institute. The joy of victory for the three of them was short-lived, but contact with Bruder's team, which was in charge of Resol Forest, was lost, and Crawford sent an emergency summons to Arks. When Crawford tells them that the large dolls "Nex Airio" that dealt a devastating blow to Airio 10 years ago have appeared, Arks is shaken. While everyone was hesitant to search for Bruder's team in the Resol Forest where Nex Airio appeared, Aina was the first to raise her hand. While searching for any remaining biological reactions in the Resol Forest, the three discover Bruder's Arcus. However, night falls without being able to track Bruder's footsteps, and he begins to think about retreating when he hears a roar echoing through the forest. However, communication was interrupted due to strong jamming. The culprit, Nex Aerio, appears in front of the group, but Bruder also rushes in and the battle begins. Bruder blames the remaining three people for searching, but Aina's words about helping you make him unable to respond, and the four of them cooperate to continue the battle after assessing the situation. In front of the angry Nex Aerio, Aina is once again cowering in fear, but she overcomes this when she sees her friends watching over her and her father in her memories. They achieved the great feat of subjugating Nex Aerio with just four people. After reporting the victory, Bruder reports the recovery of certain parts obtained from Nex Airio. Here, the protagonists are told by Crawford the concept of the "Central Cannon", which is a trump card against Dark Falz. The idea was to ``collect, compress, and shoot out photons in the atmosphere, far more numerous than the photons possessed by humans, to destroy the thick body of the Dark False and bombard the core with photons directly.'' This is also one of the reasons why he asked the three people who witnessed the Dark False to cooperate. However, there are still not enough parts for the Central Cannon to operate, and Airio Region's Arcs alone is short of manpower, so they have decided to request cooperation from the Ritem Region on the east side of Airio. Crawford asks the three protagonists to act as a go-between.
