# Mission Pass Seasonal Rewards, Stars, and Prize Tracks

Mission Pass is a reoccurring feature from Phantasy Star Online 2, where players can complete tasks to earn deluxe items. By completing Tier Missions, players are able to level up their Mission Pass and collect rewards respective to their current Mission Pass tier.
Tiers can be purchased at a rate of 25 per tier. As of Season 15, there are now 40 tiers in a Season, discontinuing the "Overrun" mode of the Mission Pass.
An N-Mission Gold Pass can be used during a Mission Pass Season to receive Gold rewards, which can be purchased from the SG Shop for a total of 100. As of Season 3, Users with Premium Status will have automatic access to Gold Rewards.
Previous Mission Passes can be viewed on the Mission Pass Archive .
