--- [Section: 概要] ---
Implemented on April 19, 2023 A highly difficult solo quest in which you fight a powerful boss enemy within a limited time. Orders can be placed from the ``Quest Counter'' that can be accessed from each ARCS base and the main menu. There are two types of Duel Quests: Standard Duel The target is a significantly strengthened boss enemy with the name "Indomitable". It is roughly divided into five stages from "Phase.1" to "Phase.5", and only "Phase.1" is divided into four quests. Extra Duel Targets a specific type of powerful boss enemy that becomes stronger with each rank released. Fictional Light is roughly divided into 10 levels from "Depth.1" to "Depth.10", and each depth has a difficulty level from [Rank.1] to [Rank.10]. The Onslaught of Star Wars is roughly divided into 10 stages from ``Stage.1'' to ``Stage.10'', and there are difficulty levels ranging from [Rank.1] to [Rank.2], where Starless appear, and [Rank.3] to [Rank.5], where High Enemies appear. In the added "Stage.11", there is only [Rank.1] that has been specially strengthened and fights enemies equivalent to the conventional [Rank.5]. ▼ Addition/Change History 2025/10/29 Update Added Extra Duel “Star Crash Onslaught” (December 24, 2025 Stage.10 addition completed) 2025/01/15 Update Extra Duel “Fictional Ray” added (February 26, 2025 Depth.10 addition completed) 2024/07/03 Update Added "Duel: Phase.5" 2024/06/05 Update Upwardly revised the number of "Fugia" type special ability capsules as quest clear reward S rank: 3 → 5, A rank: 2 → 4, B rank: 1 → 3 2024/03/13 update Added "Duel: Phase.4" 2023/10/18 Update Added "Duel: Phase.3" 2023/10/11 Update Changed the time limit from 5 minutes to 10 minutes Changed so that the boost effect of region mag is not applied Decrease in enemy HP 2023/06/21 Update Added "Duel: Phase.2" 2023/04/19 Update Added "Duel: Phase.1-1", "Duel: Phase.1-2", "Duel: Phase.1-3", "Duel: Phase.1-4" ▲ ▼

[About clear/failure conditions/inability to fight] The clear condition for each is "defeating the target enemy." The quest will fail after the 10 minute time limit has passed. A one-on-one battle will take place against a boss enemy with the double name "Indomitable." If you are unable to fight, you will not be able to use "Revival on the spot" after a certain period of time has passed, and it will fail unless you use "N-Scape Doll" or "N-Half Doll". type special abilities do not conflict unlike "Soul" type or "Gigas" type, so multiple types of "Defi" type special abilities can be added to one equipment. "Defi" type special abilities are effective only when using weapons that can be equipped by the main class. Determined by whether or not you are using a weapon for your main class. For multi-weapon weapons, the effect will only be applied while the weapon type for the main class is being used. The same judgment applies to the armor side, as long as the type of weapon being used matches. There is no problem even if the weapon does not have the "defi" type special ability, so if you attach it to the armor first, it will be easier to use it for each class. However, be aware that you will inevitably not be able to fight with subclass weapons, as they will all become invalid the moment you switch to a non-main class weapon. The power increase effect per "defi" type special ability is extremely high at +7%, so if you aim to clear the battle consistently, specialized equipment is almost essential. Adding "Defi Haumes P.01 ~ P.05", "Defi Elisis ~ P.05", and "Defi Sayres ~ P.05", which can be purchased cheaply from My Shop, to all weapons and armor is enough. In particular, attaching "Defi Elisis P.01 to P.05" (damage resistance +20% to 30%) in four locations will prevent instant death from non-major moves, so give it priority. After the update on October 11, 2023, the specifications have been changed *1 so that the boost effect of Region Mag is disabled in Duel Quest.
