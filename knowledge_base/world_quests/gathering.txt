--- [Section: 概要] ---
Gathering materials can be obtained by mining ore in the exploration section, gathering vegetables, fruits, and seafood, and dropping (meat) from Tames seeds. "Gathering materials" are used as quick food, equipment strengthening materials, and food for region mags. In addition, there are expensive items that can be sold at high prices. The placement of ores, vegetables, fruits, seafood, and expensive items, as well as the drops from Tames, are individual to each player, so they can mine and collect them freely. You can set the mag's active sonar to respond to gathering materials. All "gathering materials" cannot be listed in My Shop. It seems that ores, vegetables, fruits, seafood, and high-value items will be rearranged at 4 a.m. *1 The acquisition status of ores, vegetables, fruits, seafood, and high-value items is shared between accounts, so you cannot obtain them multiple times with multiple characters or ships. You can check the types of gathering materials found in each exploration section by going to World Map → Section Information → Gathering Tasks The guide marker will be displayed when the distance is less than 100. Once displayed, it will continue to be displayed even if you leave it ▼ Modification history 2023/01/25 Update The arrangement of the Gathering in the Airio region has been adjusted, and Alpha Reactor will now only appear in the central Airio (total number remains unchanged) 2022/05/18 Maintenance Fixed a bug where Randomite may reappear the next day 2021/12/15 Update There is no longer a limit on the damage dealt when the player's attack power is high. 2021/12/15 Maintenance Fixed a bug that occurred since July 2021 where fruits were not displayed and could not be collected. 2021/08/04 Maintenance Fixed so that "Alpha Reactor" is displayed in "???" --- [Section: 鉱石] ---
It is mainly found in or near rocky areas, and can be mined by destroying it with an attack. Used as material to strengthen equipment or as food for Legion Mag. Regarding the destruction of ores and item drops, the effect of increasing the rare drop multiplier is applied.Ores other than Photon Scale and Blizzardium have medium and low probability drop slots in addition to guaranteed drop slots, and up to 3 can be dropped.The HP of all ores is 330, and the higher the rarity, the harder it is (higher defense power).The lock-on range is very narrow, and you can lock it by approaching to a distance of less than 10 ▲ ▼
