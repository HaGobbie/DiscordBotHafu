## ore
It is mainly found in or near rocky areas, and can be mined by destroying it with an attack. Used as material to strengthen equipment or as food for Legion Mag. Regarding the destruction of ores and item drops, the effect of increasing the rare drop magnification is applied.In addition to guaranteed drop slots, ores other than Photon Scale and Blizzardium have medium and low probability drop slots, and up to 3 can be dropped.The HP of all ores is 330, and the higher the rarity, the harder it is (higher defense power).The lock-on range is very narrow, and you can lock it by approaching to a distance of less than 10.

## Food ingredients (vegetables, fruits, seafood, meat)
Vegetables, fruits, and seafood can be gathered and harvested by "picking." Meat is dropped by Tames. Use as an ingredient for Quick Food or as bait for Legion Mag.
