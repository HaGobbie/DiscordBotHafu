--- [Section: 概要] ---
Implemented on December 15, 2021 If you meet certain conditions, you can earn "Title Name", "Title Points", and SG (Star Gem). You can check the acquisition status by sharing your account and going to Main Menu → Tasks/Quests → Title. The "number of titles acquired" is not the number of title tasks completed, but the total number of "title names" acquired. You can freely combine up to three title names by editing the ARCS card. As the title point value reaches a certain level, the number of background patterns that can be set on the ARCS card increases. Some of the play achievements in the acquisition conditions will be counted from after the title is implemented. ▼ Titles that will be counted after the title system is implemented (Regarding the specifications of the "title" system scheduled to be implemented in December) Titles related to travel distance Titles related to the number of purchases in My Shop (Achievements will be recorded from February 2022) Titles related to fighting/subjugating enemies Titles related to PSE burst Titles related to item lab Titles related to food stands Titles related to party play Titles related to emergency quests Titles related to Battle Deer Titles related to trials Titles related to daily tasks/weekly tasks Titles related to chat/screenshots Titles related to gathering Titles related to region mags Titles related to the number of item containers destroyed (excluding titles related to item container [red]) ▼ Change history 2023/08/02 Update Moved the position of the main menu "Title" item from Personal → Tasks/Quests 2022/06/08 Update We will change the specifications of title rewards so that you can directly obtain star gems. ▲ ▼
