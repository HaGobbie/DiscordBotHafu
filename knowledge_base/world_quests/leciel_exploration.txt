## Strategy
About random maps In Luciel Exploration, the structure of the map where the quest takes place and the enemies that appear change randomly every day. The map is made up of a number of rooms called ``sectors,'' and each sector has various conditions for ``enhancing player abilities'' (described later). About LP Points shared by all participants. Probably “L ife Point”. If one of the participants becomes unable to fight, it will decrease by 1, and when it reaches 0, the quest will end at that point. The maximum value varies depending on the number of participants. There doesn't seem to be any way to recover during the process, so be careful if you're participating at the very limit of your combat strength. Regarding player ability enhancement There is a special gimmick called ``player ability enhancement'' that strengthens the player character in Luciel's search. Player ability enhancements can be obtained by fulfilling the ``ability enhancement conditions'' set for each sector. Obtainable player ability enhancements can be checked from the icon on the radar map. Regarding Enemy Abilities Enhancement Enemies with two names, ``Strange Invasion'' and ``Miscellaneous Invasion,'' have various abilities strengthened. Regarding various status corrections depending on the number of quest participants Enemy HP correction is stronger depending on the number of quest participants. Even small enemies like Tinos will be very tough, so be careful. About boosts Various boosts from quick food effects (premium set applied), add-on skill boosts, stage live effects, and boost-type consumable items are valid. R Mag Boost [Overall]/[Individual] is invalid. (Because "Luciel" does not fall under any of Aerio, Litem, Qvaris, or Stear.) "Rare drop multiplier increase" boost effects of various campaigns will be applied. (Official)

## About objects specific to Luciel search
Photon Boost Crate A capsule that appears after clearing the Isolation Sector. By accessing it, all participants can obtain exclusive strengthening effects. Shared Container A container that can be destroyed after clearing the puzzles in each sector. [PSO2 Day] The destruction count of the container [green] progresses, so it seems that the system treats it the same as a normal item container. Although it cannot be confirmed while completing the quest, it seems that items such as N-Grinder and Growthment are stored there. As the name "shared" suggests, information such as destruction and dropped items is shared among all quest participants. Stunt trap (mine) Can be found in the bulkhead sector where you fight two boss enemies. Usually, it is half buried underground, with a faint red light pointing directly above and a red line indicating the blast area. It activates when the player approaches and detonates after a countdown. Can be guarded and avoided. When an enemy is involved, a special effect like a yellow ring will be displayed in the center of the body, and the enemy will remain in standby mode for a certain period of time and will not move or attack. Infernel (Time Bomb) A huge bomb that can be placed in the Varigale Isolation Sector. A gauge indicating a one-minute count will be displayed, and the moment the gauge reaches 0, an explosion with instant death power will occur throughout the sector, nullifying guard/evasion, invincible penetration other than PB, and shielding. It will be unlocked if all the enemies in the sector are annihilated within the time limit. It is possible to withstand it if you have a high level of damage resistance. Not only the player but also the enemy will receive instant death damage, so if it explodes, the enemy's subjugation will be completed automatically.

## About reward items
Even if you cannot defeat the boss enemy that appears at the deepest part of the quest within the time limit, you will receive a reward based on the damage you inflict. When searching for Luciel, the material item "Growthment" will drop. By using Growthment at the item trader's ``Growthment Exchange'', you can exchange it for items such as the ★9 rarity weapon ``Farshmel'' series and the ★8 rarity armor ``Octos Armor''. In addition to being dropped when exploring Luciel, Growthments can also be obtained as rewards from daily tasks. Special ability capsules such as "C/Halfiniria LC", which have a high success rate but are 0.25% less powerful than the original special ability, will also be dropped. None of the "LC" version capsules can be listed in My Shop. After maintenance on November 8, 2023, the specifications have been changed so that the campaign boost effect "Rare Drop Multiplier Increase" will be applied to Luciel exploration.
