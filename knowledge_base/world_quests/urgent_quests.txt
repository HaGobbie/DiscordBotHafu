--- [Section: 概要] ---
A special quest that occurs randomly and can only be accepted for 30 minutes from the time it occurs. Battles occur in specific locations in the exploration section, but battles take place in an isolated state like a dedicated room. Order conditions are set for each quest. When they occur, they occur simultaneously in each region. The number of times that quests that occur at the same time can be cleared is the same. Even if the quest is from a different region, you cannot accept it more than the number of times you can complete it. --- [Section: 受注方法] ---
A pre-start warning announcement will be played in the game 15 minutes before the emergency quest occurs, and an icon will be displayed on the world map and at the location on the field. When an emergency quest occurs, you will be able to accept the quest. If you are in the same section as the occurrence location, a guide to accepting the quest will be displayed on the screen (note that the guidance will not be displayed if the sword is drawn or an access command such as "[E] Take" is displayed). You can accept an order by selecting the icon. When you accept an order, it becomes "Matching", and when the number of players is complete, you will be automatically transferred to a special isolated field. Be careful as the matching will be canceled if you cross sections during matching. If you cancel the matching by crossing the teleport section while multiple people are matching, you will not be able to participate in the emergency quest for that time (you will not be able to accept quick orders, and all emergency quest icons on the map will disappear). The quest will start with a countdown of just under 1 minute.The countdown time will vary as it continues to count down during transfer.

--- [Section: クエスト即時開始の提案] ---
If you make a ``proposal to immediately start a quest'' from the ``Quest Order Menu'' during matching, you can start the quest even if there are not enough people as long as the participating members agree. Unless there is a majority vote and the consent of all members, the proposal will not start immediately, and if even one person opposes the proposal, it will be rejected. Non-responses will be treated as consent at the end of the proposal period, and the proposal will begin immediately. If you reject the immediate start proposal several times, the rejecters will be excluded from matching, and only those who agree will approve the immediate start. Even during the proposal, new participants are accepted, and when there are enough other participants to reach the capacity, a joining process is entered and the event begins. It depends on the quest, but since emergency quests have enemy HP adjusted by the number of participants, it is less likely to fail even with a small number of people.

--- [Section: マッチング中に受注時間が終了した場合] ---
The matching will be maintained, and the quest will start after a certain amount of time after receiving a confirmation to start the quest. By agreeing to start the quest immediately from the new notification, you can start the quest without waiting.

--- [Section: パスワードマッチング] ---
If you set a participation password and select ``Restrict multiplayer with other parties,'' you will only be matched with players who have set the same participation password. This can be used when you want to play with a fixed party. Be careful if you set a simple password like "a" because you will be matched with a player who also happens to have the same participation password.

--- [Section: 撮影推奨など一部特定ブロックでのマッチング] ---
If you accept a quest with a photography recommended block, beginner recommended block, or interaction recommended block, matching will only be done between blocks of the same type. In the beginner-recommended block, it is not possible to order or match quests other than Airio Region. These blocks have a small number of blocks and few players, so matching may take a long time or may not be possible. The current block name is displayed above the "Simple Status" when opening the main menu.

--- [Section: 緊急クエスト中の戦闘不能とレスタサイン・リバーサーサイン] ---
If you become incapacitated during an emergency quest, you will automatically revive on the spot after a certain period of time. If the Reverser sign is used, it will be revived immediately like normal. Resta Sign and Reverser Sign placed in the emergency quest field will not be rearranged during the quest.

The probabilities for each are as follows. After 2 hours: 25% After 3 hours: 50% After 4 hours: 25% If a stage live event occurs, an emergency quest will occur 30 minutes after the live event occurs, so the time will be 30 minutes instead of 0 minutes.

--- [Section: ダウンゲージ] ---
Source for damage compensation and accumulation: NGS verification data archive In subjugation type emergency quests, the enemy's HP gauge is displayed at the top center of the screen, and the "down gauge" is displayed below it. The down gauge decreases by attacking the enemy, and when it reaches zero, the enemy will be down for a certain period of time with a "BREAK" message displayed and a purple effect that looks like it is shattering. While downed by BREAK, damage dealt by the player is doubled. Double damage correction is valid until the special debuff applied when BREAK goes down disappears. While down, the down gauge will gradually increase, and when it is fully recovered, it will return from the down state. Even while the gauge is recovering, the gauge is already fully recovered internally, and it is effective to reduce the gauge by attacking while it is down. Although it is a performance that reduces the gauge, it seems that the mechanism is that it occurs by accumulating physical down value and attribute down value.The amount of gauge decrease increases as the physical down value and attribute down value increase with Bouncer's class skill Defeat Amplify.In addition to normal attacks, down gauge is also reduced by physical down, attribute down, and special down by destroying some parts.If the down gauge becomes zero during physical down/attribute down, it will be down again by BREAK after recovering from physical down/attribute down. Defeat class skills related to Bouncer's "downed enemy" are not applied when downed by BREAK.See also Enemy Down/Broken Parts for various types of downed.

--- [Section: エネミー撃破時の報酬とクエスト一時倉庫] ---
In emergency quests, even if you defeat an enemy, "GREAT!!" will be displayed and you will not be able to obtain experience points, meseta, or drop items on the spot, but you will be able to obtain them as a clear reward immediately after clearing the quest. After completing the results, the clear reward ``Receive Reward Items'' will be displayed, and you will be able to individually select whether to transfer or sell item packs or other warehouses. The automatic item sale function does not apply to items stored as clear rewards, but all items that are not received will be sold, so you only need to obtain what you need. If you accidentally sell something you need, you can buy it back at the item shop before logging out. In addition, the drop items as clear rewards are stored in the "Quest Temporary Warehouse" when you clear the emergency quest. If you return to the ARCS base without paying for any reason, you can open the receipt screen from the new notification on the main menu. It can also be retrieved from the regular warehouse menu. The contents of the "Quest Temporary Storage" will be saved even if you log out. However, you will not be able to accept new quests if any items remain in the "quest temporary storage".
