--- [Section: 概要] ---
Implemented on October 13, 2021 You can play when the party leader (solo is fine) has the required number of corresponding "Battle Trigger (B Trigger)" in the item pack or material warehouse and accesses the "Trigger Portal". You can also accept orders from the quest counter. Similar to Trainier, once you register Trigger Portal locally, you can use it from the World Map. When cleared or discarded, you will be moved to the front of the trigger portal. Similar to emergency quests, matching with other parties will be done automatically. If you want to challenge solo or with a small group, you will need to limit matching with other parties when accepting a quest. If matched with another party, the player who activated the trigger portal will consume the required number. Players who use Battle Trigger will receive the following ``Battle Trigger User Effects'': experience points gained +10%, rare drop multiplier +20% *Excluding Battle Deer (Yellow), which has fixed items. Battle triggers are consumed upon clearing. If you abandon the quest midway through, the battle trigger will not be consumed. By playing halfway through and discarding it, you can practice without consuming battle triggers. If you do any of the following after accepting an order, the quest will be automatically discarded. After accepting the quest, move the battle trigger to a location other than the item pack or material warehouse.The party leader logs out.Battle triggers have a low probability of being dropped by enemies in the exploration section and combat section (same as 4★). *1 You may also be able to obtain it by completing some limited-time tasks. You can also list it on your My Shop. If you are matched solo or in a small party, you can save on battle triggers by consulting while waiting after accepting an order, canceling the quest, and forming a party with 4 people again. If you cancel the quest after fulfilling the quest clearing conditions, you can get items and experience points and go out immediately. When playing multiple rounds, the party leader can discard it to save time. ▼ Change History 2023/08/02 Update Up to 3 assist NPCs can now participate in "Aerio Troopers", "Ritem Troopers", and "Quvarist Troopers" 2022/08/03 Update B Trigger/(region name) Yellow and B Trigger/(region name) Purple have been unified to B Trigger/Common Yellow and B Trigger/Common Purple (Previous battle triggers are item recycling. (can be exchanged for B Trigger/Common Yellow and B Trigger/Common Purple) You can also order from the "Quest Counter" at each ARCS base ▲ ▼

--- [Section: バトルトリガー使用者効果] ---
Boost effect given to players who use Battle Trigger. During the Battle Deer quest, the following effects will be applied. Acquired experience points +10% Rare drop multiplier +20% Excluding Battle Deer (Yellow) where the items obtained are fixed ▲ ▼

--- [Section: バトルディアの種類と必要トリガー] ---
Battle Deer: Yellow A quest where you can obtain a large amount of experience points and equipment for strengthening +20. It is especially easy to earn experience points because the reduction in experience points due to level differences is nullified. Region Mag Boosts are disabled. "B Trigger/Common Yellow" is required when accepting the quest. Battle Deer: Purple A highly difficult quest to defeat powerful enemies such as Gigantics that appear one after another within the time limit. The reward changes depending on the number of enemies you defeat, and you can get the maximum reward by defeating all the enemies within the time limit. Region Mag Boosts are disabled. "B Trigger/Common Purple" is required when accepting the quest. Blue Portal: Variable A quest accepted at the trigger portal "Blue Portal: Variable", which is placed at each ARCS base only during a specific period. The quests that can be accepted vary depending on the period. Depending on the period and quest, you may be able to accept the order without a battle trigger. *Perhaps because the quest counter was implemented, it has not been placed since June 2022 ▲ ▼
