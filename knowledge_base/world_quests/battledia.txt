## Battle trigger user effect
Boost effect given to players who use Battle Trigger. During the Battle Deer quest, the following effects will be applied. Acquired experience points +10% Rare drop multiplier +20% Except for Battle Deer (yellow) where the obtained items are fixed

## Battle deer types and required triggers
Battle Deer: Yellow A quest where you can obtain a large amount of experience points and equipment for strengthening +20. It is especially easy to earn experience points because the reduction in experience points due to level differences is nullified. Region Mag Boosts are disabled. "B Trigger/Common Yellow" is required when accepting the quest. Battle Deer: Purple A highly difficult quest to defeat powerful enemies such as Gigantics that appear one after another within the time limit. The reward changes depending on the number of enemies you defeat, and you can get the maximum reward by defeating all the enemies within the time limit. Region Mag Boosts are disabled. "B Trigger/Common Purple" is required when accepting the quest. Blue Portal: Variable A quest accepted at the trigger portal "Blue Portal: Variable", which is placed at each ARCS base only during a specific period. The quests that can be accepted vary depending on the period. Depending on the period and quest, you may be able to accept the order without a battle trigger. *Perhaps because the quest counter was implemented, it has not been placed since June 2022.
