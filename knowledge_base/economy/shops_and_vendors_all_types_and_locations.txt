# Shops and Vendors — All Types and Locations

== Item Shops ==
Item Shops are shops where you can exchange N-Meseta for other items.

=== Central City ===
- Item Shop

== Exchange Shops ==
Exchange Shops are shops where you can exchange certain items for other items.
The available selection may vary depending on the events that are underway. In addition, some exchange shops may have set limits on how many of a specific item you can exchange, until the limit is reset.

=== Main Menu ===
- Item Recycle Shop

=== Central City ===
- Item Exchange Shop
- Item Recycle Shop
- Augment Capsule Exchange Shop

=== Event ===
- Seasonal Points Exchange Shop

== ARKS Cash Shop ==
ARKS Cash Shop is a shop where you can exchange ARKS Cash for premium items.

=== Main Menu ===
- ARKS Cash Shop

== Star Gems Shop ==
Star Gems Shop is a shop where you can exchange Star Gems for premium items.

=== Main Menu ===
- Star Gems Shop
