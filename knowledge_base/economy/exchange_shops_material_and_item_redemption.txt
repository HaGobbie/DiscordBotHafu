# Exchange Shops — Material and Item Redemption

== Overview ==
Exchange Shops are shops where you can exchange certain items for other items.
The available selection may vary depending on the events that are underway. In addition, some exchange shops may have set limits on how many of a specific item you can exchange, until the limit is reset.
