# Currency — Meseta, Star Gems (SG) and AC

== N-Meseta ==
N-Meseta is a currency exclusive to Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis. N-Meseta can be used to purchase various goods from shops, such as the Treasure Shop , and is used to pay for services, such as enhancement services from the Item Lab .
N-Meseta can be obtained by converting unwanted items, or converting specific materials obtained from gathering and events, such as Alpha Reactors . N-Meseta is also rewarded from completing Tasks and quests such as Urgent Quests .

== Star Gems (SG) ==
Star Gems (SG) are a form of currency that is used for purchasing and renting convenient items and features that you can use in the game. At the SG Shop , you can purchase tickets and items as well as play SG Scratch Tickets . SG can also be used to purchase items from the Treasure Shop . SG is shared throughout Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis and Phantasy Star Online 2.
Star Gems can be obtained through various sources, such as Campaigns , completing Titles , obtaining Login Stamps , from Seasonal Exchange Shops , or by redeeming SG Tickets, which are obtained through sources like Special Scratches and ARKS Records .

Tips and Recommendations
Objective | Tips
Giving Lookbook Likes | Likes have to be given to up to 5 different people, you can't give the same person a like several times for more SG in a day
Login Stamps | Login Stamps and Daily Tasks do not reset at the same time, Login Stamps reset 3 hours after Daily Tasks
Completing Daily Tasks | Normally, you can complete up to 7 Daily Tasks in a day This amount is often increased during Events, make use of this if you're tight on time!
Sunday & Monday Tasks | Sunday and Monday tasks are only available for 24 hours during their respective days These also count as Daily Tasks
ARKS Records Badge Exchange | 40 ARKS Record Badges are required to get all weekly SG out of this exchange and Badges are rewarded per ship Just participating rewards you with 20 badges, do this on 2 ships and you can redeem all the SG from the exchange without much effort You can make up to 3 characters for free at once, spread them out between different ships to easily get this SG
Alliance Badge Exchange | It is required to be in an Alliance to access this exchange and you need a total of 4 badges to get all the SG out of this
Item Recycle Shop | N-EX-Cubes can be acquired from many places, such as leveling at max level, exchanging class cubes, Nameless City, Alter Realm Exploration and more
Major Target Suppression Missions | For the Major Target Suppression Missions, it is required to be properly geared and be skilled enough in your class to clear these quests as the time limit and incapacitation limits are tight. Intended to be done with friends and/or alliance members.
Casino | Aside from simply playing the Casino games, you can get 5 Casino Coin Passes from dailies for each character you own on a ship. This ensures a minimum of 500 Casino Coins every day per character, but can also give more since every pass can give up to 500 Casino Coins each
Battle Coin Barterer | Obtaining Battle Coins requires playing Ranked Matches and is based on your personal performance, winning or losing and your current PVP rank. Check the Battle Arena page for more info on how to obtain Battle Coins and how much you can get
Buster Medal Exchange | The most efficient way to get Buster Medals is from the Cradle of Darkness and Drawn to Darkness Urgent Quests on Ultra Hard, as these quests drop a very large amount of Buster Medals by comparison to the time spent in Buster Quests
PSO2 Day Login Rewards | This simply requires you to login once at any point between the 1st & 3rd and 21st & 23rd in a month
PSO2 Day Tasks | The set of tasks to do during the 3-day period of PSO2 Day are very straight-forward and can be done at any level of play, often within the routine of doing Daily Tasks
PVP Battle Ranking | Definitely the hardest of the tasks if you want to get a huge amount of SG out of it. Getting SG out of this requires you to be at least at rank C5 at the start of a new month, which would give you 40 SG. Getting the full 2000 SG requires you to be at the top of the Legend rank. It is also very important to remember that if you do not collect your reward before a new month rolls in, the previous SG reward is forfeit and you can't collect previously obtained rewards if you miss them

== ARKS Cash (AC) ==
AC (ARKS Cash) are paid points used for purchasing and renting convenient items and features that you can use in the game. At the AC Shop , you can purchase tickets and items to expand the benefits of the Premium Set and Premium Storage, as well as play AC Scratch Tickets . AC is shared throughout Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis and Phantasy Star Online 2.
