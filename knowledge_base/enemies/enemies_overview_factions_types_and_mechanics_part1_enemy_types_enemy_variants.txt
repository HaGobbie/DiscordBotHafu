The various enemies that are encountered in the Open Field and Quests. Note that some enemies have weak points, and others will become stronger under certain conditions.

== Enemy Types ==
=== TAMES ===
"TAMES" is a generic name for the relatively docile creatures that live on Halpha. If you attack them, they may run away or charge at you.
Defeating a TAMES unit awards you with gathering materials.

=== DOLLS ===
"DOLLS" are the mysterious enemy that ARKS must face on Halpha. When you engage them or inflict a certain amount of damage, they turn red, their behavior changes, and their attacks become more powerful.
A DOLLS unit's weakpoint is typically a yellow core on their body. However, some individuals might only reveal their cores after taking a certain amount of damage.

=== ALTERS ===
"ALTERS" is the common name for enemies with strange, eyeball-like symbols on their heads. They change form depending on Day and Night Transitions, and are particularly powerful at night, so be on guard.
An ALTERS unit typically have a weakpoint in the form of a yellow core located somewhere on their body. This part can be attacked to deal more damage.

=== FORMERS ===
"FORMERS" is the common name for enemies that look like beasts or automata. They are more violent than TAMES and will attack ARKS defenders on sight.
Although FORMERS lack a unified weakpoint indicator, a beastlike FORMERS unit typically take more damage on its head, while a automata FORMERS unit will typically have a glowing blue core that takes more damage.

=== Starless ===
The Starless are a mysterious enemy that suddenly appeared on Halpha. When you engage them or inflict a certain amount of damage, parts of them will turn purple.
Not only can do deal more damage to a Starless by attacking its yellow core weak point, but destroying a Starless' jewel-like core also inhibits its abilites.

=== Ruinus ===
"Ruinus" is a generic term for enemies that are shrouded in a black aura.
You can deal extra damage by attacking their yellow core weak point. Additionally, the majority of Ruinus enemies have a rare chance of dropping a unique Weapon Camo upon defeat.

=== Special Enemies ===
"Special Enemies" is a generic classification for enemies with unique drop tables, special gameplay effects, or other noteworthy features. This classification includes more-conventional enemies like Rappies and Captans, as well as destructible tools generated and utilized by enemy forces.

== Enemy Variants ==
=== Giant Mutants ===
Giant Mutants are a series of large boss enemies that appear during designated event periods. In Exploration Sectors, certain Dread Enemies will be marked with a Task Marker. Defeating Dread Enemies marked with these Task Markers will have a chance to spawn the Giant Mutant enemy respective to that region.
Giant Mutants and have a chance to drop rare items, such as the Neos Justitean Series , and the Dread Keeper Augments , Domina Augments , and Note Augments of the Giant Mutant's respective region. Additionally, each Giant Mutant has a chance to drop a specific Weapon Camo and Accessory respective to the following Giant Mutant enemy.
