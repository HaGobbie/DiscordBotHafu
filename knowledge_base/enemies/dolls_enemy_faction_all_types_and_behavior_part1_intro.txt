# DOLLS Enemy Faction — All Types and Behavior (Part 1)

Enemies : | DOLLS | ALTERS | FORMERS | Starless | Ruinus | Special Enemies | TAMES
Enemy EXP
DOLLS are the mysterious enemy that ARKS must face on Halpha. When you engage them or inflict a certain amount of damage, they turn red and become enraged, altering their behavior and increasing their attack power.
When attacking, yellow cores that appear are weak points, so be sure to aim for them to deal more damage.
In the Global version of Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis, the singular form of DOLLS is "DOLLS unit."
