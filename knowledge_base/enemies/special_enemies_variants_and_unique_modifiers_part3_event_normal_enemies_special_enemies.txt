=== Rappy Spot ===
Occasionally, many Rappies will spawn when defeating Dread Enemies in any exploration sector. You may be able to hear Rappies chirp after defeating a Dread Enemy, so seek their spawn point by using the provided audio cues.
In Rappy Spots, special enemies such as Emperappy may spawn as well, other than just Rappies. On very rare occasions, a Great Rappy will spawn instead of an Emperappy.
