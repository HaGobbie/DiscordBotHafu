## giant mutant
Implemented on July 13, 2022 An enemy “mutant” that may appear on the field by meeting the conditions specified at the time of a specific seasonal event. All of them have the word "Geo" at the end of their names, and boast a much larger body than normal enemies. For details, see giant mutant.

## high energy
Implemented on July 2, 2025. A powerful enemy with a jet black body, white highlights, and a halo-shaped ring on its head. A powerful enemy whose parameters have been greatly enhanced compared to the original enemy. Attack patterns have also become more intense, including frequent use of area attacks and continuous attacks. There are common effects such as characteristic charges and technique activation effects. It is not an enhancement with two names, but a subspecies like Renus Vera for Renus Litem, and is treated as a separate enemy. Therefore, even if you defeat High Renus, you will not be able to proceed with the "Renus Ritem Subjugation Task" or the "Renus Vera Subjugation Task." However, the race does not change and tasks such as defeating the Dolls continue to count.

## Different attribute mutant
Implemented on October 18, 2023 A special enemy that shines in the color of its weak attribute. There are also enemies that have changed from their original weaknesses. They are equipped with a barrier that reduces damage other than their respective weak points. Barriers can be destroyed by dealing a certain amount of damage, and non-weak attribute attacks can pass through them. As of November 2023, it only appears in the limited-time emergency quest "Haunted Domain". For details, see Different Attribute Variants.

## starless
Medium Boss | Large Boss A mysterious being that came from space. It is said that she was the model for the Dolls. When it enters a state of engagement or receives a certain amount of damage, a portion of it turns purple (an angry state) and its behavior changes. Except for some small minnow enemies, there are reinforced parts (destructible parts) somewhere on the body, and if these remain, each enemy will be given a unique reinforcement. The types of reinforcement are roughly determined by the color of the reinforcement parts, and are strengthened, weakened, additional attack, and recovery. Even if boss-level reinforcement parts are destroyed, they will regenerate after a certain period of time or when the character enters an angry state. Its weak points are the yellow core and reinforced parts. Halves damage to areas other than weak points.
