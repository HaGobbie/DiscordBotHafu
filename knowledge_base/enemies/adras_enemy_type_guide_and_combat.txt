# Adras Enemy Type — Guide and Combat

Hunter | Fighter | Ranger | Gunner | Force | Techter | Braver | Bouncer | Waker | Slayer
Adras is an EX Style introduced to Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis in the October 1st, 2025 update.

== EX Style Overview ==
The Adras EX Style primarily supports defensive playstyles, awarding extra attacks and increased Photon Blast gauge buildup by negating enemy attacks. After activating Adras After Blast, Adras Absorption will allow you to heal while attacking enemies for a short amount of time.
