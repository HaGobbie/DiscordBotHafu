# Celeste Enemy Type — Guide and Combat

Hunter | Fighter | Ranger | Gunner | Force | Techter | Braver | Bouncer | Waker | Slayer
Celeste is an EX Style introduced to Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis in the October 1st, 2025 update.

== EX Style Overview ==
The Celeste EX Style supports supports playstyles that accumulate large amounts of Down Factor, awarding extra attacks and increased Photon Blast gauge after inflicting a certain amount of Down Factor. After activating Celeste After Blast, Celeste Absorption will allow you to heal while attacking enemies for a short amount of time.
