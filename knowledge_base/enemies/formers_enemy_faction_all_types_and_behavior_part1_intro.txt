# Formers Enemy Faction — All Types and Behavior (Part 1)

Enemies : | DOLLS | ALTERS | FORMERS | Starless | Ruinus | Special Enemies | TAMES
Enemy EXP
FORMERS is the common name for enemies that look like beasts or automata. All FORMERS are variants of enemies encountered by the interplanetary fleet Oracle, 1,000 years in the past.
They are more violent than TAMES and will attack ARKS defenders on sight.
