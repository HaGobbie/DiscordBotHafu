# Blitz Enemy Type — Guide and Combat

Hunter | Fighter | Ranger | Gunner | Force | Techter | Braver | Bouncer | Waker | Slayer
Blitz is an EX Style introduced to Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis in the October 1st, 2025 update.

== EX Style Overview ==
The Blitz EX Style supports offensive playstyles, awarding extra attacks and increased Photon Blast gauge buildup continously attacking with the same Photon Art or Technique. After activating Blitz After Blast, Blitz Decay Rounds can be used to weaken nearby enemies.
