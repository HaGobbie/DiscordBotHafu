# Starless Enemy Faction — All Types and Behavior (Part 1)

Enemies : | DOLLS | ALTERS | FORMERS | Starless | Ruinus | Special Enemies | TAMES
Enemy EXP
Starless are mysterious entities that have emerged from space. Starless strongly resemble Dolls that the ARKS have encountered.
Starless enemies turn from red to purple when you engage them. When engaged, specific Starless will activate their cores to grant them additional abilities, such as Shifta and Deband. Destroying their cores will disable these abilities.
