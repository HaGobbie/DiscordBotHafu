# ALTERS Enemy Faction — All Types and Behavior (Part 1)

Enemy EXP
ALTERS is the common name for enemies with strange, eyeball-like symbols on their heads.
They change form depending on Day and Night Transitions, and deal +30% more damage at night. However, Alts Secreta Augment Capsules drop more frequently from Night Shifted ALTERS.
You can deal greater damage to them by aiming at the yellow core in their abdomen.
In the Global version of Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis, the singular form of ALTERS is "ALTERS unit."
