=== Aelio Region ===

Ard Banser | • South Aelio • Mt. Magnus | Forms Soul | • Head • Claws (x4) • Head (weakpoint) | Weak to: | FORMERS minibosses akin to the Banser Sylvants . Unlike his female counterpart, Ard Banser cannot launch razor winds while grounded. However, he can instead perform quick charge tackles, backflips, and use his wind tunnel while in the air.

Ard Banshees lack several moves exclusive to their male counterparts. However, they do have the unique ability to launch razor winds while grounded.

Granmizer | • Maqead Lower Level | Forms Machini Soul | • Core (weakpoint) | Weak to: | FORMERS minibosses akin to the Automata Tranmizer. An adaptive mech that can take the form of a biped brawler, which focuses on melee swipes and slams, or an armored vehicle, which focuses on gunfire and homing missiles. When enraged, it can additionally transform into a laser turret that makes up for its lacking mobility with powerful melee and ranged attacks. A Granmizer's vulnerable core is only temporarily revealed after it uses its current form's strongest attack. In addition, Granmizer is susceptible to neither Physical or Elemental Downs.
