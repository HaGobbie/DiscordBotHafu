--- [Section: 概要] ---
The main enemy types are: ``Dolls'', an unknown life form that is hostile to ARKS; ``Alters'', whose appearance and strength change during the day and night; ``Formers'', similar to the old PSO2's enemy types ``Primals'', ``Mechs'', and ``Dragons''; ``Starless'', mysterious beings that came from space; ``Ruinus'' There are ``Tames'', harmless creatures that basically do not take hostile actions. There are also special individuals such as ``Rare Enemies'' and ``Enhanced Enemies,'' as well as powerful boss enemies such as ``Dread Enemies'' and ``Gigantics.'' Markers on the map allow you to distinguish Tames, regular enemies, boss enemies, dread enemies, and Gigantics. *1 Tames Normal Enemy Boss Enemy (Medium) Boss Enemy (Large) Dread Enemy (Expert) Gigantics (Despair) Enemies that are attracting attention (hate) will have an eye-like icon next to the enemy name. Enemy markers on the map also have a thin red frame. For information on experience points that can be obtained from enemies, see Enemy Experience Points. For information on enemy down and part destruction, refer to Enemy Down/Part Destruction. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: レアエネミー] ---
(C)SEGA An enemy whose entire body is silver or gold. It usually has two names: ``silver silver'' or ``golden''. In the case of Dread Enemy, the two names remain unchanged, ``Veteran.'' Gain experience points, rare drop multiplier, and seasonal points (during seasonal events) are higher than normal enemies. Drops a weapon with the special ability "Esperio" that increases the amount of experience points gained when used as reinforcement material. See Rare Enemies for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: エンハンスドエネミー] ---
(C)SEGA A strengthened enemy whose body is pierced by a wedge-like object with a yellow core called an "enhancer." Since the enhancer puts a shield on the main body, almost no damage will pass through it unless it is destroyed. In the battle section, they may appear as trial subjugation targets. In the exploration section, in addition to trials, individuals with a higher level than normal enemies may appear together with rare enemies. See Enhanced Enemy for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: ドレッドエネミー] ---
(C) A powerful boss enemy that appears in the SEGA exploration section. It has two names: ``Oren no''. The locations and types of enemies that appear are basically fixed, but may change due to events, etc. See Dread Enemy for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: ギガンティクス] ---
(C)SEGA Implemented on June 23, 2021 A very powerful boss enemy that appears in the exploration section during certain weather conditions. It has two names: ``Despair'', and its whole body is black and purple, giving it a sinister appearance. Specific weather conditions vary by region. Airio Region Thunderstorm Ritem Region Dust Storm Qvaris Region Blizzard Steer Region Fire Hell See Gigantics for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: シーズンエネミー] ---
(C)SEGA Implemented on July 7, 2021 An enemy with a season symbol on its head. They appear in certain sections for a limited time, such as during events. The weakness attribute changes to the specified attribute for each event, and a special drop is set. By defeating them, you can obtain seasonal points, which can be exchanged for items only during the period. See Seasonal Enemies for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: メガロティクス] ---
(C) SEGA Implemented on February 9, 2022 A variant of the enemy that appears only in the battle section for Lv.35 and above. There are not only boss enemies but also small fry. It has the dual name of ``elite,'' and from a distance it appears reddish, but when you get closer, its entire body becomes a glittering crystal-like coloring of blue and white. As the description on the official page states, "It has attack power comparable to Giganticus," it has the same damage correction as Giganticus. See Megalotics for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: イコライジングエネミー] ---
(C)SEGA Implemented on April 6, 2022 A strengthened enemy that appears only in the battle section for Lv.40 and above. A red spiral wedge-like object called the "Equalizer" runs through his body. It has the ability to strengthen the attack and defense power of surrounding enemies. Since the Equalizer cannot be destroyed, it is necessary to destroy the Equalizing Enemy itself. See Equalizing Enemy for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: エンシェントエネミー] ---
(C)SEGA Implemented on June 15, 2022 A variant of an enemy that appears only in a specific section. It has the dual name of ``distant world'' and is characterized by its body covered in frost. The coloring is similar to Megalotics. It is positioned as an even more powerful enemy than Gigantics. See Ancient Enemy for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: 巨大化変異体] ---
Implemented on July 13, 2022 An enemy “mutant” that may appear on the field by meeting the conditions specified at the time of a specific seasonal event. All of them have the word "Geo" at the end of their names, and boast a much larger body than normal enemies. For details, see giant mutant. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: グローサードエネミー] ---
(C)SEGA Implemented on August 2, 2023 An enemy strengthened by a spiral-shaped part called "Grosser". Strengthening progresses over time. See Grocerd Enemy for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: バムロードエネミー] ---
(C) SEGA Implemented on August 2, 2023 An enemy with a yellow spiral-shaped part called "Bum Loader". When it goes down, its parts activate and cause an unblockable explosion. See Bumlord Enemy for details. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: ハイエネミー] ---
Implemented on July 2, 2025. A powerful enemy with a jet black body, white highlights, and a halo-shaped ring on its head. A powerful enemy whose parameters have been greatly enhanced compared to the original enemy. Attack patterns have also become more intense, including frequent use of area attacks and continuous attacks. There are common effects such as characteristic charges and technique activation effects. It is not an enhancement with two names, but a subspecies like Renus Vera for Renus Litem, and is treated as a separate enemy. Therefore, even if you defeat High Renus, you will not be able to proceed with the "Renus Ritem Subjugation Task" or the "Renus Vera Subjugation Task." However, the race does not change and tasks such as defeating the Dolls continue to count. ▲ ▼

--- [Section: 異属性化変異体] ---
Implemented on October 18, 2023 A special enemy that shines in the color of its weak attribute. There are also enemies that have changed from their original weaknesses. They are equipped with a barrier that reduces damage other than their respective weak points. Barriers can be destroyed by dealing a certain amount of damage, and non-weak attribute attacks can pass through them. As of November 2023, it only appears in the limited-time emergency quest "Haunted Domain". For details, see Different Attribute Variants. ▲ ▼
