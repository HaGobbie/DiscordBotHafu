## Additional success rate
Additional success rates are set for each type of special ability capsule. You can increase your success rate by consuming up to 10 capsules of the same type at the same time. For example, the additional success rate of Power I is 10%, so if you use 10 of them, it becomes 100%. You can also increase your success rate with auxiliary items. For additional success rates for each type of special ability, see the ``Success Rate'' column in the Special Abilities List. S Capsule Some special ability capsules have an "S" at the end. The rarity has been changed from the original capsule, and its special ability is treated as a different type from the regular product without S. This is mainly relevant when referring to the number of types of special abilities, such as the latent ability ``Skill Type'' or the preset ability ``Fixer Abundance''. This capsule has a 100% success rate and can always grant special abilities with just one consumption. It can be obtained from Treasure Shop SG, limited time SG Shop, specific AC scratch bonuses, etc.

## Number of special abilities that can be added (number of slots)
The number of special abilities (number of slots) that can be added is determined by the equipment's enhancement value. For details on strengthening equipment, see Item Enhancement/Limit Breaking. ## Effects of special abilities
HP The maximum value of HP increases (decreases if it is negative) PP The maximum value of PP increases Power [%] The power of all types of weapons increases Strike weapon power [%] The power of strike weapons increases Ranged weapon power [%] The power of range weapons increases Law weapon power [%] The power of law weapons increases Lower limit of power correction [%] Corrects the lower limit of the weapon's "damage correction" (width of variation in damage dealt to enemies) Damage Resistance [%] Reduces damage received (increases if negative) Condition Abnormal Resistance [%] Makes it difficult to receive abnormal conditions (easily occurs if negative) Special Environmental Resistance [%] Reduces environmental damage that occurs in a specific section Effects of powerful special abilities are multiplied. For example, if a total of 4 parts (weapon and 3 parts of armor) have Power III (2.0%) attached, it will be 1.02 x 1.02 x 1.02 x 1.02 = approximately 1.082, and the power will increase by approximately 8.2%. See also damage calculation. Special environmental resistance effects are added. For example, if 3 parts of armor have Decord Standard III (25.0%), 0.25 + 0.25 + 0.25 = 0.75, and environmental damage will be reduced by 75%.

## Inheritance of special abilities
Special abilities once added will not be lost unless overwritten. The inheritance success rate is always 100%. Therefore, it is not necessary to succeed in all additions at once, and it is possible to add multiple times. Since the equipment itself cannot be used as a material, special abilities cannot be transferred to other equipment.

## Transferring special abilities

By consuming N-Special Abilities Transplant Pass x1 and 10,000 N-Meseta, it is possible to transplant special abilities (all or EX special abilities only). Can be exchanged for x100. ① Transplanting all special abilities If the number of special ability slots of the base item and material item is the same, or the base item has more, it can be transplanted regardless of the series or weapon type. If the number of special abilities of the material item is less than the slots of the base item, all remaining slots of the base item will be empty slots. All special abilities of the material item will disappear and become empty slots. Only when the number of EX special abilities of the base item and the material item are the same, it can be transplanted regardless of the series or weapon type. All the EX special abilities of the base item will disappear and will be replaced by the EX special abilities of the material item. If you want to attach an EX special ability to a weapon that does not have an EX special ability, you will need to port it first in step 1. According to the specifications in step 1, if the weapon you want to add an EX special ability to has any abilities, all the attached abilities will be lost, so it is strongly recommended that you port them first. The base item (transfer destination) can be anything. On the other hand, even if you select these weapons as a base, if the materials are not compatible, the porting pass will be consumed as usual, so be careful. ## Defi (Duel Quest special effect)
Defi systems do not compete in the same phase. (P.01 and P.02, etc. of the same system, such as Elisis, conflict.) The effect will only be applied when the target Duel Quest is in progress and a weapon that can be equipped by the main class is used. The effect will be applied even if added to armor, but the effect will only be applied when a main class weapon is equipped. In addition, when using multiple weapons, if one is a main class weapon and the other is a different class weapon, the effect will only be applied when the main class weapon is equipped. (*Example: If a hunter is equipped with a multi-weapon of a sword and an assault rifle, the effect will only be applied when used as a sword.) As an aside, the name is thought to be derived from the solar system's dwarf planets or dwarf planet candidates: Pluto, Makemake, Haumea, Eris, Ceres, and Sedna. ## Should I use one capsule at a time or 10 at a time?
The (apparent) success rate increases if you use them all at once, but by using one capsule at a time, you can add ``N-ability additional success rate + α%'' each time. In conclusion, if you use success rate increasing items and insert capsules one by one, the expected value per capsule will increase, but the probability of not being successful at all increases. In other words, if you repeat inserting one capsule 10 times, you will succeed two or more times = the possibility of making multiple equipment with that ability will increase (If you try 18% success rate 10 times by combining one capsule with an 8% success rate and a 10% success rate, the expected value is that you will complete 1.8 pieces of equipment with that ability, but there is a ``probability of not succeeding at all'' of about 13.74%). 0.82 x 0.82 x 0.82 x 0.82 x 0.82 x 0.82 x 0.82 x 0.82 x 0.82 x 0.82≒0.1374 Conversely, if you put in 10 items at once, the probability of success increases, but no matter how you try, you will only complete one piece of equipment with that ability. (If you use 10 capsules with an 8% success rate and a 10% success rate to achieve a 90% success rate once, the expected value is to complete 0.9 pieces of equipment with that ability, but the probability of failure is suppressed to 10%.) Consult with your inventory of capsules and items that increase your success rate, and try to find ways to use each item in a way that is good for your mental health, such as using one item for things you want to have in multiple units such as units, putting 10 items into a weapon you absolutely want to use, or finding a sweet spot with a success rate of 30 to 80% with 2 to 9 items.
