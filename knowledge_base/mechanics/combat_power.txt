--- [overview] ---
A numerical value based on the player's status, the strength of equipped weapons and armor, and the number of class skills learned. You can check the simple status or go to Main Menu → Personal → Character Information → Parameter Information. Subclasses are affected only by the number of class skills acquired. Combat power is referenced below. --- [★Additional combat power value for armor of 6 or higher] ---
For armor with ★6 or higher, the unique combat power is added instead of the defense power/performance of the armor itself. This unique combat power appears to be set for each level at which armor can be equipped. However, the amount increased by strengthening will be added to the combat power by ``defense power ÷ 2''. Combat power = basic attack power + [weapon attack power × (damage correction upper limit % + damage correction lower limit %) ÷ 2] + weapon potential Lv × 10 + [defense power excluding armor ÷ 2 + armor combat power addition value + increased defense power due to reinforcement ÷ 2 + special correction value by special ability] + skill points used x 3 + style points used x 3 * [x] represents the integer part of x For example, Leclerc Armor In the case of +60, the combat power addition value is 34, the unenhanced defense power is 35, and the +60 defense power is 96, so the "additional combat power value of armor + increased defense power due to reinforcement ÷ 2" part of the above formula is 34 + (96-35) ÷ 2 = 64.5.
