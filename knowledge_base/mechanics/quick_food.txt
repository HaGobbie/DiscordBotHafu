--- [overview] ---
You can make ``quick food'' that temporarily boosts a player's abilities at food stands or Ryukor Devices in various locations. You will need food materials (vegetables, fruits, seafood, meat) that can be collected at gatherings. Quick food is not an item and is consumed on the spot where it is made to produce its effects. Effective time is 240 minutes. The effect time also decreases while logged out. Food ingredients are classified by type (vegetables, fruits, seafood, meat) and taste (matta, sappa, shakki, guttu). Effects are set for each category, and effects can be generated by freely combining 1 to 10 food materials and up to 5 materials with the same name. If you make quick food again during the effect period, the effect will be overwritten. *``Nota'' from Kvaris is an exception and will have the same effect as a material with the same name in the second half of the material (e.g. Nota Kvargacki → Same as Matta Kvargacki) You can check the status of the boost effect in the ``Current Effects'' window under Main Menu → Personal → Character Information → Parameter Information. Depending on the material used, [Vegetables], [Fruits], [Seafood], [Meat], and [Matteri], [Refreshing], [Crisp], and [Gatsuri] are displayed as effect names. --- [Region match effect] ---
If you use 4 or more ingredients of the same flavor obtained in the same region where you make quick food, a ``region matching effect'' will occur. The effect does not disappear even if you move to another region after it occurs. See "Region matching effect" in Effect list. ▲ ▼

--- [premium effect] ---
During the validity period of the premium set, "premium effect" will occur as an additional effect. See "Premium Effect" in Effect List. ▲ ▼

--- [My recipe] ---
In addition to selecting food ingredients one by one to create quick food, you can also save combinations of ingredients as "My Recipes." It saves you the trouble of choosing when combining many materials. There are usually 10 recipes that can be created. 30 pieces during the premium set validity period. Once the premium expires, you will no longer be able to select recipes beyond the 11th. When premium resumes, recipes from the 11th onward will be restored. *1 ▲ ▼
