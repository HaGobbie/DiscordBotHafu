--- [overview] ---
A multi-weapon is a weapon that combines different types of weapons from the same series. You can now set both normal attacks, weapon actions, and PA in one weapon palette. It can be created at the item lab in Central City, Ritem City, Kvaris Camp, and Stear Camp. The base weapon is called a "base item". In addition to the base item, "material items" from the same series as the base item, Photon Quartz, and N-Meseta are required to create it. *The "Thank Aim" series exceptionally requires Photon Scale instead of Photon Quartz. It is possible to use auxiliary items when creating, but as of February 2023, there are no auxiliary items. All enhancement values, potential abilities, special abilities, preset abilities, color variants, etc. of the base item will be applied. Weapon names, weapon icons, the form immediately after switching weapons, the form at the start of the quest, the behavior of simple operations, etc. depend on the base item. The material item part can be overwritten by performing multi-weapon conversion again. For example, after creating a multi-weapon with a sword (base) + assault rifle (material), you can recreate it into a sword (+Talis) by using this sword (+assault rifle) as a base and using Talis as the material. Multi-weapon conversion cannot be canceled. Weapons that have been converted into multi-weapons cannot be used as material items. As an exception, the following combinations can be used to create multi-weapons even with weapons from different series. Neos Astrion and Neos Eustilon, etc., ``Different color series implemented at the same time'' Kaiseram and Kaiseram Prototype 1, Roseram and Rouseram Prototype 1, etc., ``Original and trial series'' Excelio and Duocerio, etc., ``Upgrade related series'' Any weapon and multi-weapon substitute materials After creation, the appearance of the weapon on the material side will be reflected. Example 1: If Neos Eustylon Wire is used as a material based on Neos Astorion Sword, Neos Eustilon's appearance will appear only when in wired lance form.Example 2: If multi-weapon substitute material (Slash) is used as material based on Coldic Tact, the appearance of multi-weapon substitute material (Prim) will only appear in Gunslash form.Regarding the color variant, the effect color on the material side will be that of the base weapon, and the appearance of the material side will be the same as the non-CV material weapon. There are also series where only some weapon types exist. For details, see the list of weapons and armor series. ▼ Change history 2021/08/04 Fixed a bug where it was sometimes impossible to use a technique or set a technique to the palette when using a combination of two legal weapons. Fixed a bug where weapon reflection Robiak would refer to and reflect the base weapon of a multi-weapon regardless of the weapon status. 2021/06/09 (from CBT) Changed so that special abilities such as "Strike Weapon Power +" are judged according to the type of attack method at the time instead of the base weapon. Fixed a bug where chain trigger and chain finish could not be activated when twin machine guns were incorporated based on other weapon types ▲ ▼

--- [specification] ---
Regarding activation conditions for actions and skills Normal attacks and weapon actions can be used even if one of the combined weapons cannot be equipped by the main/subclass. Example: If you equip a sword/rifle multi-weapon with a Hu/Fi combination, you cannot use the rifle's PA, but you can use the rifle's normal attacks and weapon actions. Skills specific to the equipped weapon can be activated by the action of that weapon, regardless of whether it is a base or material. When activating Photon Blast, it depends on the last weapon type used, not the base weapon. Photon blasts that are not a usable weapon type for the main/subclass cannot be used. Example: Hu/Fi is a sword + rod, and the rod's photon blast cannot be used.If a combination of weapons that can use techniques is combined and both can be equipped, the weapon used for the technique depends on the weapon type used most recently. If you combine a weapon that can use techniques with a weapon that cannot use techniques, when you try to use techniques, the weapon type will automatically switch to a weapon type that can use techniques. If you include a legal weapon that cannot be equipped as a main or sub weapon, you will not be able to use techniques with that weapon even if it includes a class that can use techniques. Example: With the combination of Hu/Fo, techniques cannot be used even if a multi-weapon of sword/boots is equipped. In the case of a combination of legal weapons where one cannot be equipped as either main or sub, the weapon type will be automatically switched to one that can be equipped when using a technique. Example: In the Bo/Hu combination, if you equip Talis/Boots multi-weapon and use a technique after Talis normal attack, you will be forced to change to Boot form. Skills that activate an additional counter when performing a normal attack (such as Sword Guard Counter Plus) can only be activated by inputting a normal attack of the specified weapon type. Example: Sword Guard Counter Plus is activated only by a normal attack with a sword, and will not be activated even if you perform a JG with a sword and then perform a normal attack with another weapon type. Even for weapon actions similar to the guard type, it is not possible to substitute a guard that is a prerequisite for a counter (sword, rod, wand, etc.) Weapon actions placed in a weapon palette are performed with the weapon specified in the palette. Weapon actions with the "weapon action button *1" depend on the type of weapon used most recently, not on the base weapon. Example: With a sword + wired lance multi-weapon, if you press the weapon action button without performing a sword action after performing a normal attack or PA with the wired lance, the weapon action of the wired lance will be activated.If you press the weapon action button without performing any action with the wired lance after performing a normal attack or PA with the sword, the weapon action of the sword will be activated. When you press the weapon action of a wired lance registered in the weapon palette, the weapon action of the wired lance will be activated regardless of which weapon type was used immediately.Skills that are written as 〇〇 only will be applied only when attacking with that weapon, regardless of the base weapon. Example: Even if the base weapon is a twin machine gun and a sword etc. is incorporated as a sub weapon, the effect when chain boosting will only be applied to the twin machine gun's normal attack and PA. Regarding power correction and status Special abilities such as "Strike Weapon Power +" are only applied when using that attack method. Example: If a sword/rifle has "Impact Weapon Power +" attached, the effect will not be activated during normal attacks, PA, PB, etc. with the rifle. If you want to apply the effect to a rifle, you need to add "shooting weapon power +", and this effect does not apply to swords. When mixing different weapon types such as striking/shooting, it is best to give abilities such as ``power +'' that does not specify the weapon type, or ``strike/shooting weapon power +'' that is effective on the two applicable types. The power of weapons that can be equipped to the main class (power reduction in subclasses) is also adjusted depending on the attack method at the time, regardless of the base weapon. Example: Compared to equipping a sword/rifle on the main Ra and main Hu, the power of the rifle part will increase and the power of the sword part will decrease. Even if it becomes a multi-weapon, its combat power will not change. Regarding equipment requirements: Even if the class cannot equip base weapons, If the class that can equip the built-in weapon is included in either the main or sub class, it can be equipped. Example: If the base is a sword and a rod is installed, it can be equipped in a Fi/Fo class configuration. Regarding materials: Regardless of the condition of the equipment consumed for materials, the performance after turning it into a multi-weapon depends on the base weapon. Material weapons' enhancement values, special abilities, preset abilities, color variants, etc. will not be inherited at all. Multi-weapons can be overwritten by consuming materials again. Naturally, consumed materials will not be returned. It is not possible to only cancel multi-weapons. Weapons that have been made into multi-weapons cannot be used as materials for making them into multi-weapons. About Appearance The appearance when the sword is drawn changes to the corresponding appearance by performing normal attacks, PA, and weapon actions. In the case of techniques, it depends on the above activation conditions. The appearance of the sword when it is sheathed will be the base weapon immediately after switching from a different weapon, and if the sword is sheathed while the sword is unsheathed, the appearance when the sword was unsheathed will be maintained. Therefore, if you change weapons immediately after logging in or while in town, your base weapon will always be displayed (unless you go outside and switch). Weapon camouflage is enabled in both cases, and the weapon camouflage that matches the form you are currently using will be displayed. Effect changes of color variants follow the current form. Example: Create Tallis/Rod with non-CV products, set no camouflage on the Tallis side, and set Prim Almaty CV on the rod side. If you use Talis weapon action in this state, pillbox bullets will fly in non-CV default blue, and if you switch to rod form during the effect time, pillbox bullets will change to Primal Armati CV yellow. Furthermore, if you switch to Talis form, it will return to the non-CV default blue. Others If there is a sword draw effect, the effect will be expanded each time the form is switched. ▲ ▼

--- [merit] ---
You can fight by interweaving the normal attack, PA, and weapon action of two types of weapons without changing your weapon palette.You can fight in a variety of ways, such as using normal attacks and weapon actions to attack by sticking your knuckles together, and PA to mow down enemies in a wide range with wires. Timed/placement actions that normally disappear when changing weapons (Double Saber's Kamaitachi, Launcher's Sticky Bomb, Tallis's Placement Fire, etc.) can also be used at the same time as other weapons. You can use another type of weapon without loading.Almost the same content as above, but if it is a multi-weapon, loading will not occur. Depending on the line and the situation of the mackerel, even if you switch to a different weapon, it may not be reflected for a few seconds. This mainly happens when a burst occurs or when an emergency boss goes down, and you can only move during that time, resulting in a big loss. Since this waiting time for loading can be prevented, changing hands depending on the situation becomes much more stable. Enhanced equipment can be easily transferred to other classes, expanding the range of classes.If you use a single sword, you must have Hu as your main or sub weapon, but if you add Double Saber to your multi-weapon, you will be able to use it as long as you have a Hu or Fi class as your main or sub weapon. Enhancement values, special abilities, potential release, etc. depend on the base weapon, so one enhancement will be the same as two enhancements.Materials can be in a blank state immediately after acquisition. It may seem a bit expensive, but the cost of adding potential abilities alone easily exceeds the cost of making it a multi-weapon, so it's cheap. There is no particular disadvantage to fighting around mainly using the action of the weapon made as a material without using the base weapon. This is a compromise only for the same series, but if you get a good preset weapon that you don't use, you can also get a good preset for the weapon type you want through multi-weapon. Utilizing different potentials within a series Benefits include the introduction of the "Relic" series, which has different potentials within one weapon series. By creating a weapon type with the desired potential ability using your main/favorite weapon type as materials, you can assemble a weapon with the potential ability that is suitable for your own use. There is something a little close to the above cost reduction, but if you don't intend to use the main weapon, the multi-weapon portion will simply increase the cost. There is also the view that because the material weapon types are fixed, the benefits of multi-weapons that allow you to switch between two weapon types in battle are effectively restricted. ▲ ▼

--- [Disadvantages] ---
You will no longer be able to use it as a material for making multi-weapons.At present, it seems unlikely that you will use the upgraded weapon as a material, so basically there is no need to worry about it. It may be a good idea to consider this when converting a weapon that is extremely difficult to obtain into a multi-weapon. If you make weapons with different attack methods (striking weapons, shooting weapons, legal shooting weapons) into multi-weapons, it will be difficult to specialize for one attack method when adding special abilities.As mentioned above, if you turn hitting weapons and shooting weapons into multi-weapons, the special ability "Strike Weapon Power +" will be applied to striking weapons and not shooting weapons. There are special abilities that are effective on two or more attack methods with one slot, but it is also true that in many cases it is easier to add or cause more damage if you specialize in one attack method. When using weapons such as Braver and Bouncer, which have different attack methods from the main class, as a multi-weapon, it was necessary to be careful about adding special abilities, but on December 7, 2022, the class skills Special Ability Optimize Br and Special Ability Optimize Bo were implemented, and this disadvantage has been resolved. The first technique you use after moving to a room will be your main weapon. For example, if you use boots as a multi-weapon using rods as materials, you will use the boots to perform techniques unless you use the rod for a normal attack. Techniques have different usability depending on the weapon used, so although it's not a disadvantage, it is still a nuisance. ▲ ▼

--- [Usage example] ---
Put another category of PA into the leftover weapon palette. Combinations that compensate for the weaknesses of weapons and combinations that take advantage of the skills of the main class are good. It is quite effective to prepare each weapon and equip it on a separate palette, but when making it into a multi-weapon, the advantage is that it can be operated instantly without the time lag of switching weapons, and that item enhancements and special ability additions can be done with just one weapon. For this reason, if you combine the things you want to use in group battles and the things you want to use in boss battles, you will end up using only one. By combining two types of weapons into one, you can enjoy benefits such as reducing the hassle of strengthening two weapons, releasing upper limits, adding abilities, and unlocking potential to one weapon, and saving item pack space. When purchasing weapons with presets, you can reduce the purchase cost by choosing the cheapest option. Melee Weapons + Ranged Weapons (Sword + Assault Rifle, etc.) A means for main classes that mainly focus on melee combat to efficiently attack and collect PP against opponents who move around or move away. If your only goal is to collect PP or hunt materials, weapons that do not match the subclass are also effective. Especially in battles with small fry, if you use a weapon with a dash attack in the form of a piercing bullet, the efficiency of collecting PP will greatly increase. This is a combination that can be seen in the opening movie, but now that players are getting stronger and have more options, it is a combination that is rarely seen unless there is considerable synergy between the weapons. Non-legal weapons + legal weapons You can now use techniques even with weapon types that cannot normally use techniques. It is necessary to set the class that can equip the combined legal attack weapon as the main class or subclass (Fo for rods, Te for wands). Techniques registered in sub-palettes can also be used in the same way. It expands the range of responses, such as compensating for the weaknesses of weapons that are weak at range attacks and long distances, and taking down attributes that cannot be removed normally. However, you need to be aware that unless you configure your special abilities to take into account the combination of different types of weapons, the firepower of one of them will be significantly reduced. After the implementation of the Qvaris region, the number of special abilities combining two to three types has increased, so the status of this combination is improving along with the combination of legal attack + hitting/shooting described later. For assault rifle + launcher Ra. You can use attacks with both weapons at the same time that have a time lag before reaching full hit, allowing you to fight more smoothly. For example, against small fish enemies, you can use Homing Dart for large numbers in a wide area, Divine Impact for dense groups, and Revolt Aim, Multiple Launch, and Fallen Impact for single targets at the same time with one weapon. You can also use the rifle PA while keeping the sticky bomb, which disappears when you change hands. The advantage has diminished a little with the addition of Launcher Week Bullet, but it's still useful if you don't want to get Launcher WB due to its slow release. For twin machine guns or bullet bow + assault rifle GuRa, BrRa. Including the choice of subclasses, it is designed with twin machine guns and bullet bows in mind against bosses. In addition to the addition of Rifle Grenada, Sub-Ra has the advantage of being able to use Spread Shot over time even if you are not using a rifle due to Spread Shot Auto Charge. In the case of the T machine gun, the chain finish correction does not apply, and in the case of BrRa, the main class correction does not apply, but the two skills, which have counter-level power and PP recovery amount, can be used at any time, so using them between chains or at the break of enemy attacks will help increase firepower and manage PP. With the addition of various skills, you can now use rifle grenades as a powerful counter, so you can benefit from that as well. In addition, when acquiring the special ability Optimize Br and using equipment that emphasizes shooting magnification, it is possible to combine Katana + Assault Rifle with BrRa. Melee weapon + wired lance Uses wire weapon action to ensure mobility primarily in the air. Can be used even if it is not a sub-Hu. It is safe even with weapons that are difficult to reach on flying enemies or parts of high places. For long range weapons, It is also possible to use it to gain altitude for aiming at high areas with attacks such as blaze shots that have a limited vertical firing angle. The wired lance's normal attack is a range attack, and its advantage is that it has excellent PP recovery ability against groups of small fish. In the case of sub-Hu, it is also possible to use powerful range attacks. Although limited to the main Hu, Wired Anchor Avenge is applied to the wire-side weapon Aku immediately after PA with Hunter Arts Avenge on Sword or Partisan, or Wired Anchor Strike Back lets you stock up on Avenge-state PA and weapon Aku and fight with the other weapon type, and when the enemy goes down, use the wire-side Avenge PA and weapon Aku to secure high firepower when downed. Twin Daggers & Knuckles When using twin daggers primarily, the dagger spin counter has vertical tracking but lacks firepower, so in situations where vertical movement is not required, use the knuckle sway counter, which has excellent total firepower. Nowadays, Knuckle SC Alternation has been added, which powerfully tracks up and down, so depending on your opponent, you can rely completely on Knuckle for counters. It is also possible to use the movement of the accelerator drive and dagger spin counter while using the knuckle as the main movement. A fighting style like the one above, which relies on the proper use of counters and PA, is extremely difficult to operate on the fly when switching between weapons, and even weapons of the same class benefit greatly from having multiple weapons. In addition, Knuckles is also suitable for Double Saber, which has difficulty recovering PP counters. Here, Knuckle's counter, which is already powerful, is further strengthened, albeit slightly. Melee Weapon + Double Saber Once activated, the double saber will continue even if you are using other weapons, so if you maintain it properly, it will simply lead to an increase in DPS. However, be aware that while using a multi-weapon weapon other than Double Saber, Kamaitachi's power will be reduced to 1/4th (in the case of the 3rd stage, the power is the same as the 2nd stage, just over 1/7 of the original power). With the implementation of Katana & Bullet Bow Special Ability Optimize BR, this combination can now be combined even if you specialize in either hit magnification or shooting magnification. Although it can use long-range attacks that deal with up and down directions, which the katana is weak at, in terms of usability, the katana is often the main player in boss battles. The key when dealing mainly with Katana is to have a high-firepower combo that includes the charged version of Dimensional Ray and Bullet Bow Attack Impact, and stock up on the charged version of Dimensional Ray and Attack Impact before the battle or during free time when you cannot attack, or when you see an opening against an enemy that does not reveal its weakness unless it is knocked down.
