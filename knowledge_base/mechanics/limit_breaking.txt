--- [Section: 概要] ---
Weapons and armor can be strengthened using ``Item Enhancement'' at the item labs in Central City, Ritem City, Kvaris Camp, and Stear Camp. When enhanced, enhancement EXP is accumulated in the base item, and when a certain amount of enhancement EXP is reached, the enhancement value increases. To strengthen it, you need N-Grinder, N-Meseta, another weapon for weapons, and another armor for armor. Weapons and armor to be strengthened are called "base items", and other weapons and armor necessary for strengthening are called "material items". The upper limit of the reinforcement value in the initial state varies depending on the rarity of the base item. The current upper limit is written as "(MAX10)" in the "Enhancement value" column of item details tab 2. The upper limit can be expanded by "Limit Break" in the Item Lab, but the final enhancement limit may vary depending on the rarity. To break the limit, you need N-Meseta, ore up to +40, Arms Refiner after +50, and Arms Refiner II after +70 for ★1 to ★6 and +60 for ★7 or higher. Materials such as ores used to break through the limits are treated as "costs" and are affected by cost discounts. Breaking the limit itself can be done regardless of the current reinforcement value. As the reinforcement value increases, the number of special abilities (number of slots) that can be attached increases. Enhancement EXP added by special abilities is also subject to bonuses with the same name, so it is more efficient to add special abilities to material items with the same name before using them. If you achieve a "great success", the enhanced EXP you gain will be doubled. The probability of success is 10%. You can increase the probability with auxiliary items. The final enhanced EXP obtained is calculated using the following formula. Acquired enhanced EXP = (EXP from material item + EXP accumulated in material item ÷ 2 + EXP from special ability) x Same name bonus x Great success multiplier ▲ ▼

--- [Section: コストパフォーマンスが高い強化方法について] ---
Currently, the basics are to use Goldo Prim Sword II, Goldo Prim Armor II, or C/Endimio. These can be obtained from boss enemies after level 70, exchange shops, etc. You can trade them in My Shop, so even if you can't obtain them on your own, it's cheaper to buy them than to invest in a large amount of equipment with low experience points. If you are aiming to strengthen it to the upper limit, even if you use Gordo II, the strengthening cost alone will cost you a considerable amount of meseta. In that case, you can reduce the cost of strengthening by following the steps below and strengthening all at once to the upper limit. Open the upper limit of the equipment you want to strengthen with "Breakthrough" Prepare multiple pieces of equipment that have been strengthened to "+70" or higher (we recommend purchasing strengthened obsolete items from My Shop or creating them using event exchange items below) Prepare "N-Strengthening Great Success Rate +100%" (you can get it as a special scratch prize) Insert the strengthened equipment into the equipment you want to strengthen and strengthen it using the 100% success rate of reinforcement ▲ ▼
