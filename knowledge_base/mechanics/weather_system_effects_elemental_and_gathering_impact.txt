# Weather System — Effects on Combat, Elemental Buffs and Gathering

== Overview ==
In New Genesis, the weather in the surrounding areas can change overtime. Certain enemies such as Alters can be enhanced by certain weather conditions.

== Day/Night Cycle ==
An entire Day/Night cycle is estimated to last approximately 48 minutes.

== Weather Cycle ==
The weather cycle is shared across all ships and blocks. It has been theorized that the weather is selected at random from a list, with each weather change having their own unique probability and a set duration that the weather will last for. Once the duration of the selected weather is over, the game will cycle through the list to pick a new type of weather. It possible for the same type of weather to be selected, which results in the current weather being extended.

=== Aelio Region ===
Weather | Weather Duration | Estimated Probability
Clear | 4 minutes | 68%
Cloudy | 2 minutes | 12%
Rain | 3 minutes | 20%

== Special Weather Cycle ==
Occasionally, special weather conditions will occur across Planet Halpha. These special weather conditions have a fixed duration and have a chance to be extended.

=== Aelio Region ===
Weather | Weather Duration | Extended Weather Duration | Extension Probability | Cooldown
Thunderstorm | 6 minutes | 12 minutes | 20% | 3 minutes

=== Retem Region ===
Weather | Weather Duration | Extended Weather Duration | Extension Probability | Cooldown
Dust Storm | 6 minutes | 12 minutes | 20% | 3 minutes
Meteor Shower | 8 minutes | 12 minutes | 33% | 3 minutes

== PSE Levels ==
During extreme weather conditions, such as Thunderstorms and Dust Storms, Gigantix Enemies can appear. Additionally, the PSE levels in all applicable PSE Burst regions will always be at a minimum of 4 levels during these weather conditions. This effect will not subside after completing a PSE Climax until the weather is over.
