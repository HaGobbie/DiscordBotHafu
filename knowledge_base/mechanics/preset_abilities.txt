## Porting preset abilities
Implemented on December 15, 2021 In the item lab "Preset ability porting" in Central City, Ritem City, Kvaris Camp, and Stear Camp, you can transfer the preset ability by consuming equipment with the same name as the porting destination. *Unlike preset ability enhancement, weapon types from the same series or equipment with different upgrade evolution stages cannot be used. Must be exactly the same name. *Unlike transplanting special abilities, the equipment used as materials will not remain. Examples of cases where it cannot be transferred Example 1: Transferring the ability of "Towaru Schmetter Sword" to "Towaru Schmetter Sword AT". Example 2: Transferring the ability of "Praglu Armor" to "Eskul Armor". In the above case, it can be ported by upgrading and giving it the same name. Also, if you want to port the preset ability of "Primsword +0, Fixer Attack Lv5" to "Primsword +40, no preset ability", "Primsword +40, Fixer Attack Lv5" will remain on hand, and "Primsword +0, Fixer Attack Lv5" will disappear. (If the base item also has a preset, you will have to choose which preset ability to use when porting.) Color variant information has nothing to do with presets. Even if you specify color variant equipment as a material, that information cannot be inherited. If you want to use color variant equipment, you need to specify that equipment as the base. ## Enhanced preset ability
Implemented on August 2, 2023 Can be executed from ``Preset Ability Enhancement'' in the item lab in Central City, Ritem City, Kvaris Camp, and Stear Camp. The cost is 500 Meseta, and the basic success rate of reinforcement is 10%. If the enhancement is successful, the level of the preset ability will increase by 1. As a consumable material, in the case of a weapon, a weapon that can be used as a multi-weapon is required, and in the case of armor, an armor with the same name (or upgrade-related) is required. The material must have a preset ability of the same level or higher as the target to be strengthened. The types of preset abilities do not have to be the same. The used material equipment will disappear regardless of success or failure. Addition of preset ability With the implementation, if the target to be strengthened does not have a preset ability (no preset → Lv.1), it has been changed to execute from there.

## Added preset ability
Implemented on April 10, 2024 Can be executed from ``Add Preset Abilities'' in the item lab in Central City, Ritem City, Kvaris Camp, and Stear Camp. The cost is 500 meseta. The basic success rate when using equipment as materials is 10%. *If you use "Preset ability level 1 addition", the success rate will be 100% and material items will not be required. Since it is easy to obtain and has a 100% success rate, you will basically be using this item to add preset abilities (from none to Lv.1). Available as rewards for clearing various quests and at the exchange shop. If the added equipment does not have a preset ability (no preset → Lv.1), a random type of Lv.1 preset ability will be attached. If the added equipment has a preset ability, a random Lv.1 preset ability will be added instead of the preset ability that was originally attached. If the level of the added preset ability is 2 or higher, a window will appear to warn you. Even if you fail, the base ability will remain, but if you succeed, it will be overwritten by the preset ability. It's a remnant from the reinforcement section, and can be executed using equipment as materials, but the basic success rate is only 10%. In this format, the processing is similar to the case of preset reinforcement. In the case of weapons, the target and multi-weapon capable weapon are consumed, and in the case of armor, the armor with the same name (or upgrade related) is consumed. Material equipment does not need to have preset ability. Material equipment used for reinforcement will disappear regardless of success or failure.
