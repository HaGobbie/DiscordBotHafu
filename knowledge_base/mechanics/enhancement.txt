When enhanced, enhancement EXP is accumulated in the base item, and when a certain amount of enhancement EXP is reached, the enhancement value increases. To strengthen it, you need N-Grinder, N-Meseta, another weapon for weapons, and another armor for armor. Weapons and armor to be strengthened are called "base items", and other weapons and armor necessary for strengthening are called "material items". The upper limit of the reinforcement value in the initial state varies depending on the rarity of the base item. The current upper limit is written as "(MAX10)" in the "Enhancement value" column of item details tab 2. The upper limit can be expanded by "Limit Break" in the Item Lab, but the final enhancement limit may vary depending on the rarity. To break the limit, you need N-Meseta, ore up to +40, Arms Refiner after +50, and Arms Refiner II after +70 for ★1 to ★6 and +60 for ★7 or higher. Materials such as ores used to break through the limits are treated as "costs" and are affected by cost discounts. Breaking the limit itself can be done regardless of the current reinforcement value. As the reinforcement value increases, the number of special abilities (number of slots) that can be attached increases. These can be obtained from boss enemies after level 70, exchange shops, etc. You can trade them in My Shop, so even if you can't obtain them on your own, it's cheaper to buy them than to invest in a large amount of equipment with low experience points. If you are aiming to strengthen it to the upper limit, even if you use Gordo II, the strengthening cost alone will cost you a considerable amount of meseta. In that case, you can reduce the cost of strengthening by following the steps below and strengthening all at once to the upper limit. Open the upper limit of the equipment you want to strengthen with "Breakthrough" Prepare multiple pieces of equipment that have been strengthened to "+70" or higher (we recommend purchasing strengthened obsolete items from My Shop or creating them using event exchange items below) Prepare "N-Strengthening Great Success Rate +100%" (you can get it as a special scratch prize) Insert the reinforced equipment into the equipment you want to strengthen and use the 100% reinforcement success rate to strengthen it. ▲ ▼ Enhancement EXP obtained when using enhanced equipment as material ★ Material name Enhancement EXP obtained 11 "Eredym" series +60 (weapon) 74,800 7 Gordo II 100,900 7 Gordo II +60 150,900 7 Gordo II +80 9,850,900 7 Gordo II +90 14,700,900 7 Gordo II +100 19,550,900 4 Qualde Armor +80 9,435,600 6 Sestat Armor +80 9,645,800 7 Ajax Armor +80 9,750,900 *The value of Gordo II includes Endimio (+100,000) *The cost to add Endimio to other equipment is 100 Meseta. ▲ ▼ Creating reinforcement materials using event exchange items In the seasonal event, "Gold Prim Sword II +60 that has exceeded its limit" can be exchanged.
