--- [Overview] ---
Each weapon in PSO2: New Genesis has a unique "Potential Ability" (潜在能力). Potentials are unlocked at the Item Lab in Central City, Retem City, Kvaris Camp, or Stia Camp using the "Potential Release" option. Unlocking repeatedly raises the Potential Level (max Level 5 normally; Level 6 unlocked April 2023 for all rarities). Each Potential Level adds +10 to Combat Power (e.g., Lv4 = +40 CP). All potentials include a Power increase, which rises significantly even at Lv1. Upgrading requires N-Meseta plus ores (up to Lv3) or N-EX Cubes (Lv4). Exception: "Sanquaim" series needs Photon Scales through Lv4. "Renessa" series requires no materials or meseta. Some potentials have timed additional effects that disappear when switching weapons — UNLESS both weapons share the same potential name and level (including Multi-Weapons).

--- [Unlocking Costs by Rarity] ---
Lv1–Lv3 require ores (Monotite/Dualomite for Lv1, Trinite/Tedrasite Lv2, Pendorite/Hexscite Lv3) + N-Meseta (100k/200k/300k per level).
Lv4–Lv5 require N-EX Cubes (5 each) + N-Meseta (400k each).
Lv6 requires Photon Chunk IIA x60 + Photon Chunk IIB x60 + N-Meseta 500k.
Ore quantities scale with rarity (★14: 130 per ore type per level; ★13: 120; ★12: 110; ★11: 100; ★10: 90; ★9: 80; ★8: 70; ★7: 60; ★6: 50; ★5: 40; ★4: 30; ★3: 20).
Total N-Meseta to Lv6: 1,900,000 for most weapons.

Occasional "Potential Release Cost Discount" events reduce N-Meseta costs. Watch announcements for details.
